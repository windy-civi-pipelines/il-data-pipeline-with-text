Policies and procedures. 20    (a) Before June 1, 2027, each institution of higher 21education with an applicable program that offers at least one 22course for credit hours recommended for equivalency by a   HB1149 Engrossed- 2 -LRB104 03154 LNS 13175 b1national higher education association that provides credit 2recommendations for firefighting training courses and programs 3shall adopt a policy regarding its awarding of academic credit 4for firefighter training considered applicable to the 5requirements of a student's certificate or degree program. The 6policy shall apply to any individual who is enrolled in the 7institution of higher education and who has successfully 8completed a firefighting training course or program as part of 9his or her firefighting service that is: 10        (1) recommended for credit by a national higher 11    education association that provides credit recommendations 12    for firefighting training courses and programs; or 13        (2) otherwise documented as firefighting training or 14    experience. 15    (b) Each institution of higher education shall develop a 16procedure for receiving the necessary documentation to 17identify and verify the firefighting training course or 18program that an individual is claiming for academic credit. 19    (c) Each institution of higher education shall provide a 20copy of its policy for awarding academic credit for 21firefighter training to any applicant who listed prior or 22present firefighting service in his or her admission 23application. 24    (d) Each institution of higher education shall develop and 25maintain a list of firefighting training courses and programs 26that have qualified for academic credit.   HB1149 Engrossed- 3 -LRB104 03154 LNS 13175 b1    Section 15. Each institution of 2higher education shall submit its policy for awarding academic 3credit for firefighter training to the Board of Higher 4Education and the Illinois Community College Board, if 5applicable, by March 31, 2027 and by March 31 of each year 6thereafter for the policy that is effective in the subsequent 7fall semester.
