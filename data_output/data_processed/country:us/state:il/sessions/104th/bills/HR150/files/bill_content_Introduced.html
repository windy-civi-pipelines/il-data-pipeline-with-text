2    WHEREAS, The members of the Illinois House of 
3Representatives wish to mark Black History Month by 
4congratulating Illinois House Speaker Emanuel "Chris" Welch 
5for his continued service as the leader of the chamber and for 
6his historic election as the first Black lawmaker to hold this 
7position in the state's 200-plus-year history; and
 
8    WHEREAS, Speaker Welch was re-elected as Speaker of the 
9House in both 2023 and 2025, continuing his leadership in 
10shaping Illinois' legislative agenda; and
 
11    WHEREAS, Speaker Welch has been an advocate for diversity 
12and inclusion; he championed legislation requiring 
13Illinois-based corporations to disclose the racial and gender 
14composition of their boards and led a comprehensive study on 
15improving representation of women and minorities in leadership 
16roles; and
 
17    WHEREAS, Speaker Welch's commitment to workers' rights is 
18deeply personal; growing up in the Chicago suburbs, he saw 
19firsthand how his father's union job was lost when it moved to 
20a right-to-work state; this experience fueled his efforts as 
21an elected official to defend collective bargaining rights, 
22ultimately leading to the adoption of the Workers' Rights
