The Illinois Procurement Code is amended by 5changing Section 25-75 as follows: 6    (30 ILCS 500/25-75)7    Sec. 25-75. Purchase of motor vehicles.8    (a) Beginning on the effective date of this amendatory Act 9of the 94th General Assembly, all gasoline-powered vehicles 10purchased from State funds must be flexible fuel vehicles. 11Beginning July 1, 2007, all gasoline-powered vehicles 12purchased from State funds must be flexible fuel or fuel 13efficient hybrid vehicles.
