Full Text of HR0031 ILGA.GOV HR0031 - 104th General Assembly HR0031LRB104 06355 ECR 16391 r1HOUSE RESOLUTION 2    WHEREAS, January is Radon Action Month, and it is 3imperative for all Illinois citizens and property owners to 4test their homes, schools, apartments, healthcare facilities, 5and other occupied buildings for radon; and 6    WHEREAS, Radon is a colorless, odorless, radioactive gas 7that may threaten the health of Illinois citizens and their 8families; and 9    WHEREAS, Radon is the second-leading cause of lung cancer 10in the U.S., and it is the leading cause of lung cancer in 11non-smokers; and 12    WHEREAS, The U.S. Environmental Protection Agency 13estimates that radon is responsible for approximately 21,000 14lung cancer deaths each year; and 15    WHEREAS, In Illinois, 41% of homes, and an even higher 16percentage of schools and commercial buildings, have high 17radon levels; and 18    WHEREAS, Any building in Illinois may have elevated levels 19of radon, even if neighboring buildings do not, and elevated 20levels pose a serious health threat to Illinois citizens; and   HR0031- 2 -LRB104 06355 ECR 16391 r1    WHEREAS, Testing for radon is simple and inexpensive, and 2identified radon problems can be fixed; and 3    WHEREAS, The Illinois Emergency Management Agency, the 4U.S. Office of Homeland Security, and the U.S. Environmental 5Protection Agency are supporting efforts to encourage 6homeowners to test their homes for radon, to mitigate elevated 7levels of radon, and to build new homes with radon-resistant 8materials and features; therefore, be it 9    RESOLVED, BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE 10HUNDRED FOURTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF ILLINOIS, that 11we declare the month of January 2025 as Radon Action Month in 12the State of Illinois.
