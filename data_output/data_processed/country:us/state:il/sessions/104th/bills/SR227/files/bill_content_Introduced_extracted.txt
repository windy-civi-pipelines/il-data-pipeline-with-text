Peickert (Tribbensee); the father of Robert J. 10Peickert, Sue (Paul) Phillips, and Judy (Frank) Porch; the 11grandfather of five; the great-grandfather of three; and the 12brother of the late James Peickert and Alexis Castellano; 13therefore, be it 14    RESOLVED, BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTH GENERAL 15ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF ILLINOIS, that we mourn the passing of 16Robert A. "Bob" Peickert and extend our sincere condolences to 17his family, friends, and all who knew and loved him; and be it 18further 19    RESOLVED, That a suitable copy of this resolution be 20presented to the family of Robert Peickert as an expression of 21our deepest sympathy.
