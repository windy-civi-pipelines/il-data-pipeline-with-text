SENATE RESOLUTION

 
2    WHEREAS, The members of the Illinois Senate are saddened 
3to learn of the death of Robert A. "Bob" Peickert, who passed 
4away on March 5, 2025; and
 
5    WHEREAS, Robert Peickert, the son of Charles and Alvera 
6Peickert, was born in Chicago on December 5, 1944; and
 
7    WHEREAS, Robert Peickert worked as a math teacher and a 
8union official with the Illinois Federation of Teachers (IFT), 
9utilizing his pugnacity in contract negotiations; after the 
10fall of the Berlin Wall, he was sent as a union representative 
11to Poland, where he helped establish U.S.-style trade unions 
12for the teachers there; and
 
13    WHEREAS, Robert Peickert was the former chair of the 
14Democratic Party of DuPage County (DPDC) from 2008 through the 
152018 elections; during his tenure, he was noted for his 
16dedication, leading the organization with heart and vision and 
17serving as an ally and mentor to many; he spearheaded 
18Operation: Turn DuPage Blue and dramatically increased the 
19ranks of elected precinct committeepersons across the county; 
20his guidance helped elect three Democrats to the DuPage County 
21Board in 2008 and seven in 2018; he also oversaw a number of 
22successful fundraisers and gala events; after stepping down in
