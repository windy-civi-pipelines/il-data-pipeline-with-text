    Amends the Illinois Insurance Code. Provides that a group or individual policy of accident and health insurance or managed care plan that is amended, delivered, issued, or renewed after the effective date of the amendatory Act shall provide coverage for hippotherapy and other forms of therapeutic riding. Makes conforming changes in the State Employees Group Insurance Act of 1971, the Counties Code, the Illinois Municipal Code, the School Code, and the Health Maintenance Organization Act.

1    Section 25. The Illinois Insurance Code is amended by 
2adding Section 356z.80 as follows:
 
3    (215 ILCS 5/356z.80 new)
4    Sec. 356z.80. Coverage for hippotherapy and therapeutic 
5riding.
6    (a) As used in this Section:
7    "Disability" means a determinable physical or mental 
8characteristic of a person, including, but not limited to, a 
9determinable physical characteristic that necessitates the 
10person's use of a guide, hearing, or support dog, that may 
11result from a disease, injury, or congenital condition of 
12birth or a functional disorder.
13    "Hippotherapy" means physical, occupational, or speech 
14therapy, prescribed by a physician and delivered by a licensed 
15occupational therapist, physical therapist, or speech-language 
16pathologist, in conjunction with a professional horse handler 
17and a therapy horse.
18    "Therapeutic riding" means horseback riding lessons 
19adapted to an individual with a disability, delivered by a 
20professional horse handler and a therapy horse.
21    (b) A group or individual policy of accident and health 
22insurance or managed care plan that is amended, delivered, 
23issued, or renewed after the effective date of this amendatory 
24Act of the 104th General Assembly shall provide coverage for 
25hippotherapy and other forms of therapeutic riding.
