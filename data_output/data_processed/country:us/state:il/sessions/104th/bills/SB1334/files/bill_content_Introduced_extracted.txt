Dale Fowler SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 430 ILCS 65/4  from Ch. 38, par. 83-4430 ILCS 65/8  from Ch. 38, par. 83-8    Amends the Firearm Owners Identification Card Act. Lowers the minimum age in which a person who is not an active duty member of the United States Armed Forces or the Illinois National Guard may apply for a Firearm Owner's Identification Card without parental or legal guardian consent from 21 years of age to 18 years of age.
