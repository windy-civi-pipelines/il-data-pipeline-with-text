4    Section 5. The School Code is amended by changing Section 
527-20.8 as follows:
 
6    (105 ILCS 5/27-20.8)
7    Sec. 27-20.8. Asian American and Arab American history 
8study.
9    (a) Beginning with the 2022-2023 school year, every public 
10elementary school and high school shall include in its 
11curriculum a unit of instruction studying the events of Asian 
12American history, including the history of Asian Americans in 
13Illinois and the Midwest, as well as the contributions of 
14Asian Americans toward advancing civil rights from the 19th 
15century onward. These events shall include the contributions 
16made by individual Asian Americans in government and the arts, 
17humanities, and sciences, as well as the contributions of 
18Asian American communities to the economic, cultural, social, 
19and political development of the United States. The studying 
20of this material shall constitute an affirmation by students 
21of their commitment to respect the dignity of all races and 
22peoples and to forever eschew every form of discrimination in 
23their lives and careers.
