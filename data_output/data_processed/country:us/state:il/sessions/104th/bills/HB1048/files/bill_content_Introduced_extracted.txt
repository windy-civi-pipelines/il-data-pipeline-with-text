Cabello SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 5 ILCS 70/10 new    Amends the Statute on Statutes. Provides that, when interpreting the provisions of any State law or rule, a court shall not defer to an agency's interpretation of the law or rule and shall interpret its meaning and effect de novo.
