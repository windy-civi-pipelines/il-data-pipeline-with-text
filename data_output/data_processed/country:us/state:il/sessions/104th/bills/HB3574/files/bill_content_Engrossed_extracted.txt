The credit certificate earned is valid for 3 years 8from the date of issuance and shall not be applied to any 9future contract bid after the expiration of that period. 10    (f) Subsections (b), (c), (d), and (e) of this Section do 11not apply to contracts that are primarily for the provision of 12telecommunications services.
