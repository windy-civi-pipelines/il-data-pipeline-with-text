An association or other entity that has as one 9of its purposes promoting, sponsoring, regulating, or in any 10manner providing for interscholastic athletics or any form of 11athletic competition among schools and students within this 12State shall require all paid school head coaches or chief 13sponsors of the activity to obtain and maintain certification 14in cardiopulmonary resuscitation (CPR), first aid, and the use 15of an automatic external defibrillator (AED).
