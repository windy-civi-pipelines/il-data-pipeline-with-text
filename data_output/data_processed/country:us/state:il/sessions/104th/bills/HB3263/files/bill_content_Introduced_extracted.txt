Full Text of HB3263 ILGA.GOV HB3263 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026HB3263 Introduced 2/18/2025, by Rep. Anna Moeller SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 305 ILCS 5/12-4.57a new    Amends the Administration Article of the Illinois Public Aid Code. Provides that beginning July 1, 2025, and for subsequent years thereafter, the Prospective Payment System rates for Federally Qualified Health Centers (FQHC) shall be increased by $100,000,000 using an alternative payment method acceptable to the federal Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services and a trade association representing a majority of FQHCs operating in Illinois, including a rate increase that is an equal percentage increase to the rates paid to each FQHC.
