The Department of Human Services Act is amended 5by changing Section 10-25 as follows: 6    (20 ILCS 1305/10-25)7    Sec. 10-25. Women, Infants, and Children Nutrition 8Program. 9    (a) The Department shall participate in the Women, Infants 10and Children Nutrition program of the federal government to 11the maximum extent permitted by the federal appropriation and 12allocation to the State of Illinois. In order to efficiently 13process electronically issued WIC benefits, the Department may 14use an account held outside of the state treasury for the 15deposit and issuance of WIC benefits. Other appropriations and 16funds from any public or private source in addition to federal 17funds may be used by the Department for the purpose of maximum 18participation in the WIC nutrition program.19    (b) The Department shall maintain a drug abuse education 20program for participants in the Women, Infants and Children 21Nutrition Program. The program shall include but need not be 22limited to (1) the provision of information concerning the 23dangers of drug abuse and (2) the referral of participants who   HB3323 Engrossed- 2 -LRB104 11264 BDA 21348 b1are suspected drug abusers to drug abuse clinics, treatment 2programs, counselors or other drug abuse treatment providers.3    (c) The Department shall cooperate with the Department of 4Public Health for purposes of the smoking cessation program 5for participants in the Women, Infants and Children Nutrition 6Program maintained by the Department of Public Health under 7Section 2310-435 of the Department of Public Health Powers and 8Duties Law (20 ILCS 2310/2310-435).9    (d) The Department may contract with any bank as defined 10by the Illinois Banking Act to redeem bank drafts issued by the 11Department under the United States Department of Agriculture 12Special Supplemental Food Program for Women, Infants and 13Children (WIC). Any bank with which the Department has entered 14into a contract to redeem bank drafts may receive, pursuant to 15an appropriation to the Department, an initial advance and 16periodic payment of funds for the Women, Infants and Children 17Program in amounts determined by the Secretary. 18Notwithstanding any other law, such funds shall be retained in 19a separate account by the bank. Any interest earned by monies 20in such account shall accrue to the USDA Women, Infants and 21Children Fund and shall be used exclusively for the redemption 22of bank drafts issued by the Department. WIC program food 23funds received by the bank from the Department shall be used 24exclusively for the redemption of bank drafts. The bank shall 25not use such food funds, or interest accrued thereon, for any 26other purpose including, but not limited to, reimbursement of   HB3323 Engrossed- 3 -LRB104 11264 BDA 21348 b1administrative expenses or payments of administrative fees due 2the bank pursuant to its contract or contracts with the 3Department.4    Such initial and periodic payments by the Department to 5the bank shall be effected, pursuant to an appropriation, in 6an amount needed for the redemption of bank drafts issued by 7the Department under the United States Department of 8Agriculture Special Supplemental Food Program for Women, 9Infants and Children in any initial or succeeding period. The 10State Comptroller shall, upon presentation by the Secretary of 11adequate certification of funds needed for redemption of bank 12drafts, promptly draw a warrant payable to the bank for 13deposit to the separate account of the bank. Such 14certification may be in magnetic tape or computer output form, 15indicating the amount of the total payment made by the bank for 16the redemption of bank drafts from funds provided to the bank 17under this Section.18    The separate account of the bank established under this 19Section, any payments to that account, and the use of such 20account and funds shall be subject to (1) audit by the 21Department or a private contractor authorized by the 22Department to conduct audits, including but not limited to 23such audits as may be required by State law, (2) audit by the 24federal government or a private contractor authorized by the 25federal government, and (3) post audit pursuant to the 26Illinois State Auditing Act.  HB3323 Engrossed- 4 -LRB104 11264 BDA 21348 b1    (e) The Department may include a program of lactation 2support services as part of the benefits and services provided 3for pregnant and breast feeding participants in the Women, 4Infants and Children Nutrition Program. The program may 5include payment for breast pumps, breast shields, or any 6supply deemed essential for the successful maintenance of 7lactation, as well as lactation specialists who are registered 8nurses, licensed dietitians, or persons who have successfully 9completed a lactation management training program. 10    (f) The Department shall coordinate the operation of the 11Women, Infants and Children program with the Medicaid program 12by interagency agreement whereby each program provides 13information about the services offered by the other to 14applicants for services.15    (g) Subject to appropriation, the Department shall, by 16rule, expand the WIC Farmers' Market Nutrition Program across 17the State through local WIC agency grants and contracting 18local farmers to ensure access to fresh, locally grown fruits 19and vegetables. The Department shall determine which counties 20can participate based on funds available and whether there is 21a sufficient number of farmers willing to accept WIC Farmers' 22Market Nutrition Program benefits. The Department shall adopt 23rules to implement this subsection. 24(Source: P.A. 103-688, eff. 1-1-25.)
