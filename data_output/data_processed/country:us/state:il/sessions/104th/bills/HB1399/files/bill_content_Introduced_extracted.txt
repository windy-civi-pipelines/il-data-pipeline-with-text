Full Text of HB1399 ILGA.GOV HB1399 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026HB1399 Introduced 1/28/2025, by Rep. Maura Hirschauer SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 20 ILCS 2310/2310-438 new    Amends the Department of Public Health Powers and Duties Law of the Civil Administrative Code of Illinois. Requires the Department of Public Health to ensure that safe gun storage information is available at all Secretary of State driver services facilities. Provides that the Department may use existing public or private programs to implement these provisions. Requires the Department to disseminate safe gun storage information on its website and in Department facilities.
