Full Text of HB2867 ILGA.GOV HB2867 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026HB2867 Introduced 2/6/2025, by Rep. Amy Elik SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 225 ILCS 710/22  from Ch. 96 1/2, par. 4229    Amends the Fluorspar Mines Act. Provides that the Department of Natural Resources shall, no later than July 1, 2026, create and maintain an online underground mine workings portal. Provides that the portal shall be accessible to the general public without a fee. Provides that the portal shall allow access to all maps filed with the Department of Natural Resources. Requires the portal to allow a user to locate underground mine workings maps by searching for a street address.LRB104 08177 AAS 18226 b  A BILL FOR HB2867LRB104 08177 AAS 18226 b1    AN ACT concerning regulation. 2    Be it enacted by the People of the State of Illinois, 3represented in the General Assembly: 4    Section 5. The Fluorspar Mines Act is amended by changing 5Section 22 as follows: 6    (225 ILCS 710/22)  (from Ch. 96 1/2, par. 4229)7    Sec. 22. (a) The operator of every mine shall make and 8maintain, or cause to be made or maintained by a competent 9mining engineer or surveyor, a clear and accurate map or maps, 10with sections, if necessary, showing all the working of such 11mine. Such map shall be filed with the Department of Natural 12Resources and shall be brought up to date by such operator at 13one year intervals from the date it is filed. Such map or maps 14shall show all excavations, all parts of said mine that have 15been worked out or abandoned, and all underground workings.16    (b) The Department of Natural Resources shall, no later 17than July 1, 2026, create and maintain an online underground 18mine workings portal. The portal shall be accessible to the 19general public without a fee. The portal shall allow access to 20all maps filed with the Department of Natural Resources as 21required by this Section.
