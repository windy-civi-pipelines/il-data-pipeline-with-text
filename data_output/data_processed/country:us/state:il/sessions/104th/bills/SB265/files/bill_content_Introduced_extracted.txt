Full Text of SB0265 ILGA.GOV SB0265 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026SB0265 Introduced 1/24/2025, by Sen. Steve Stadelman SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 50 ILCS 205/30 new    Amends the Local Records Act. Provides that a law enforcement agency that encrypts police scanner transmissions must provide, by license or otherwise, real-time access to those transmissions to broadcast stations, broadcasting stations, radio broadcast stations, and newspapers.
