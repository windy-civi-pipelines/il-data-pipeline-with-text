9    (55 ILCS 5/3-16003 new)
10    Sec. 3-16003. Recall of countywide elected officials. 
11    (a) If the referendum described in Section 3-16002 is 
12approved by a majority of the voters voting on the 
13proposition, then the recall of countywide elected officials 
14of Sangamon County may thereafter be proposed by a petition 
15signed by a number of electors equal in number to at least 15% 
16of the total votes cast for Governor in that county in the 
17preceding gubernatorial election. A petition under this 
18Section shall have been signed by the petitioning electors not 
19more than 50 days after an affidavit has been filed with the 
20State Board of Elections providing notice of intent to 
21circulate a petition to recall the countywide elected 
22official. The affidavit may be filed no sooner than 6 months 
23after the beginning of the countywide elected official's term 
24of office and may not be filed in the last 6 months of the 
25countywide elected official's term of office.
