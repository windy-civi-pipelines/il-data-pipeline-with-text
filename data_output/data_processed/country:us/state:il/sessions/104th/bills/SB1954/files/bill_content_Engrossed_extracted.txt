A recall petition certified by 3the State Board of Elections under this Section may not be 4withdrawn and another recall petition under this Section may 5not be initiated against the countywide elected official 6during the remainder of the official's current term of office. 7Any recall petition or recall election pending on the date of 8the next general election at which a candidate for the same 9countywide office is elected is moot.10    (c) The countywide elected official is immediately removed 11upon certification of the recall election results if a 12majority of the electors voting on the question vote to recall 13the countywide elected official.
