SENATE RESOLUTION

 
2    WHEREAS, The members of the Illinois Senate wish to 
3congratulate Father Michael Louis Pfleger on the occasion of 
4celebrating the 50th anniversary of his ordination as a 
5priest; and
 
6    WHEREAS, Father Pfleger received his Bachelor of Arts in 
7Theology from Loyola University, his Master of Divinity from 
8the University of St. Mary of the Lake, and an honorary Doctor 
9of Divinity from North Park Theological Seminary; he has also 
10completed post-graduate studies at Mundelein College and the 
11Catholic Theological Union; and
 
12    WHEREAS, Father Pfleger has lived and ministered in the 
13African American community on both Chicago's West and South 
14Sides since 1968; he also worked in a Native American 
15community in Oklahoma for two summers, and he completed his 
16seminary internship as a chaplain at Cook County Jail and 
17Precious Blood Catholic Church, both in Chicago; and
 
18    WHEREAS, Father Pfleger became an ordained priest of the 
19Archdiocese of Chicago on May 14, 1975; at the age of 31, he 
20became the youngest full pastor in the diocese when he was 
21appointed as pastor of Saint Sabina Church in the Auburn 
22Gresham neighborhood of Chicago in 1981; and

1    WHEREAS, Father Pfleger has led an active and extensive 
2ministry, seeking to break down the walls of racism and 
3denominationalism by building unity among all people through a

16the Saint Sabina Social Service Center, the Thea Bowman 
17Spiritual Advance Center, the Samaritan House for the 
18Homeless, the Saint Sabina 80-unit Elders Village, and the 
19Beloved Community, Inc.; and
