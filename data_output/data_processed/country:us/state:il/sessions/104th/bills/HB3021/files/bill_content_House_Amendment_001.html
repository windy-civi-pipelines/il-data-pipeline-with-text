7    Sec. 2HHHH. Commercial transactions; artificial

9    (a) As used in this Section:
10    "Artificial intelligence" has the meaning given to that 
11term in Section 2-101 of the Illinois Human Rights Act. 
12    "Chatbot" means a generative artificial intelligence 
13system with which users can interact through an interface that 
14approximates or simulates conversation, including by means of 
15text or voice interaction.
16    "Generative artificial intelligence" has the meaning given

2    (b) It is an unlawful practice within the meaning of this 
3Act for any person to use a chatbot to engage in a commercial 
4transaction or trade practice with a consumer in which:
5        (1) the consumer is communicating or otherwise

10    communicating with a human representative; and
11        (3) the consumer is not notified in a clear and 
12    conspicuous manner that the consumer is communicating with

14    (c) The disclosures required under paragraph (3) of 
15subsection (b) shall be clear and conspicuous and reasonably 
16designed to inform consumers that the consumer is 
17communicating with a chatbot and not a human representative. A

19reasonable consumer that the consumer is communicating with a
