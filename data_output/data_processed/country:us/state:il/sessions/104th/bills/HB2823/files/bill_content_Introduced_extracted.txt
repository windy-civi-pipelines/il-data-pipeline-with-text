Chris Miller SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: New Act    Creates the Gender in Sports Act. Provides that a student-athlete may participate in an intercollegiate athletics program at a postsecondary educational institution that allows only a single gender to participate in that program only if the gender of the student-athlete is the same gender as the single gender designation for that program.LRB104 08283 LNS 18334 b  A BILL FOR HB2823LRB104 08283 LNS 18334 b1    AN ACT concerning education. 2    Be it enacted by the People of the State of Illinois, 3represented in the General Assembly: 4    Section 1.
