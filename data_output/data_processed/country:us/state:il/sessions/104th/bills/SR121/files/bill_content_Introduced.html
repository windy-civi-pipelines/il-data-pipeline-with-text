SENATE RESOLUTION

 
2    WHEREAS, The members of the Illinois Senate are saddened 
3to learn of the death of Officer Jason P. Roscow of North Las 
4Vegas, Nevada, formerly of Prairie du Rocher, who lost his 
5life in the line of duty on February 4, 2025; and
 
6    WHEREAS, Jason Roscow was born in Belleville to Ervin and 
7Ann Roscow on October 23, 1978; he grew up in Prairie du 
8Rocher, where he was affectionately known as "Peco" by his 
9friends and family; and
 
10    WHEREAS, Jason Roscow demonstrated a commitment to service 
11from a young age, volunteering as a firefighter for the 
12Prairie du Rocher Fire Department; after graduating from Red 
13Bud High School, he enlisted in the United States Air Force, 
14where he served in the security police and was stationed in 
15locations worldwide, including South Korea, Iraq, and Kuwait; 
16and
 
17    WHEREAS, Jason Roscow continued his dedication to 
18protecting others following his military service by becoming a 
19police officer with the North Las Vegas Police Department, 
20where he served honorably for 17 years; and
 
21    WHEREAS, Jason Roscow was a lifetime member of V.F.W. Post
