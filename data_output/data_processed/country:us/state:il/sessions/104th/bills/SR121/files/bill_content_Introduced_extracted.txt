Post   SR0121- 2 -LRB104 12344 MST 22528 r1#6632 in Red Bud and American Legion Post #622 in Prairie du 2Rocher; and 3    WHEREAS, Jason Roscow was widely regarded as a protector, 4a friend to all, and someone deeply committed to his community 5and country, and his passing is a profound loss to his family, 6friends, fellow officers, and all who knew him; and 7    WHEREAS, Jason Roscow was a beloved father to his two 8sons, Graysen and Andrew; he was cherished by his parents, 9Ervin and Ann Roscow; his siblings, Bridget Rider, Amy Owen, 10and Michael Roscow; his nephews, Chris Hanger, Zachery Rieke, 11Dylan Roscow, and Samuel Roscow; his nieces, Jessica DuFrenne 12and Jennifer Schreder; and numerous aunts, uncles, cousins, 13great-nieces, and great-nephews; therefore, be it 14    RESOLVED, BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTH GENERAL 15ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF ILLINOIS, that we mourn the passing of 16Officer Jason P. Roscow and extend our sincere condolences to 17his family, friends, and colleagues; and be it further 18    RESOLVED, That a suitable copy of this resolution be 19presented to the family of Jason Roscow as an expression of our 20deepest sympathy and gratitude for his dedicated service.
