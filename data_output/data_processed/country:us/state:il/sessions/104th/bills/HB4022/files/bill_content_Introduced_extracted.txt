Effective July 1, 2025.LRB104 11372 LNS 21460 b HB4022LRB104 11372 LNS 21460 b1    AN ACT concerning appropriations.  2    Be it enacted by the People of the State of Illinois, 3represented in the General Assembly: 4    Section 5.
