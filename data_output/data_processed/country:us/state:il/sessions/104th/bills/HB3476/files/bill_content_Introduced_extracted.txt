Jennifer Gong-Gershowitz SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 215 ILCS 5/355.5    Amends the Illinois Insurance Code. Removes provision stating that fees incurred directly by a dental care provider from third parties related to transmitting an automated clearing house network claim, transaction management, data management, or portal services and other fees charged by third parties that are not in the control of the dental plan carrier shall not be prohibited.
