Full Text of HB3007 ILGA.GOV HB3007 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026HB3007 Introduced 2/6/2025, by Rep. Tom Weber SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 35 ILCS 505/2e new    Amends the Motor Fuel Tax Law. Provides that the moneys received by the Department of Revenue under the Act on taxes imposed on the sale of motor fuel sold on the Chain O'Lakes shall be remitted back to the Chain O'Lakes - Fox River Waterway Management Agency or to the Department of Natural Resources and shall be used for waterway purposes.
