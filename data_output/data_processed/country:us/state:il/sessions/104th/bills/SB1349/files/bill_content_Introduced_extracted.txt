Full Text of SB1349 ILGA.GOV SB1349 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026SB1349 Introduced 1/28/2025, by Sen. Bill Cunningham SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 230 ILCS 45/25-10230 ILCS 45/25-45    Amends the Sports Wagering Act. Provides that applicants for a master sports wagering license shall pay an application fee of $250,000 and a license fee of $15,000,000. Removes provisions concerning the number of master sports wagering licenses to online sports wagering operators and public notice requirements. Provides that a master sports wagering licensee may conduct sports wagering over the Internet or through a mobile application. Removes provisions concerning eligibility requirements for a master sports wagering license, the duty of disclosure for an applicant, and outreach requirements for the Department of Commerce and Economic Opportunity and the Illinois Gaming Board. Makes a conforming change.
