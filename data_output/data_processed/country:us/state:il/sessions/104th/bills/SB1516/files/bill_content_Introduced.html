SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED:
 
15 ILCS 335/12  from Ch. 124, par. 32
    Amends the Illinois Identification Card Act. Provides that the Secretary of State shall not collect a fee for a standard Illinois Identification Card issued to a transgender person, or the immediate family of that person, moving to Illinois due to an act of persecution. Effective immediately.
