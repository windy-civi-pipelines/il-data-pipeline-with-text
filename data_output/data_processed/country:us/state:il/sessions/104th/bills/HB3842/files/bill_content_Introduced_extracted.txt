Full Text of HB3842 ILGA.GOV HB3842 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026HB3842 Introduced 2/18/2025, by Rep. Tracy Katz Muhl SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 60 ILCS 1/30-160    Amends the Township Code. When the electors in a township in a county with a population of 1,000,000 or more authorize the township board to contract with one or more municipalities in the township or with the county within which the township is located to furnish police protection in the unincorporated area of the township, requires using funds levied under the provisions to furnish the police protection. Provides that a township board's authority to declare the unincorporated area of the township a special police district is to provide and maintain police protection in the unincorporated area of the township. Allows the township board to use the special police district funds levied under the provisions for public safety but prohibits use of those funds for the purchase of red light cameras, speed cameras, or automatic license plate readers.
