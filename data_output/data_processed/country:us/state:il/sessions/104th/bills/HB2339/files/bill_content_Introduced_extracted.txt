Full Text of HB2339 ILGA.GOV HB2339 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026HB2339 Introduced 2/4/2025, by Rep. Tony M. McCombie SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 520 ILCS 5/2.25  from Ch. 61, par. 2.25    Amends the Wildlife Code. Provides that, if the Department of Natural Resources is using its sharpshooting program to manage chronic wasting disease in a deer herd that is found in a specified area or county in the State and if no cases of that disease have been identified in that herd in the preceding 3 calendar years, then the Department shall end the use of that program to manage that disease in that area or county.
