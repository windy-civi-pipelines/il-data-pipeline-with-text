6    WHEREAS, On November 3, 1979, the Waukegan Alumnae Chapter 
7became an official chartered chapter of Delta Sigma Theta 
8Sorority, Inc. through the efforts of 18 women, many with 
9young families, who worked tirelessly to form a chapter in 
10Lake County in the 1970s; and
 
11    WHEREAS, Upon becoming chartered, the members of the 
12Waukegan Alumnae Chapter immediately established its 
13organizational structure by electing officers, with the first

15Roberts, in addition to establishing committees, setting dues, 
16and more; and
 
17    WHEREAS, Further, the members of the Waukegan Alumnae 
18Chapter started fulfilling the critical functions of the 
19sorority, including public service and social action

22children's projects, and the Beautillion Militaire program and
