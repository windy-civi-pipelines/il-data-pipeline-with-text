Full Text of SB1893 ILGA.GOV SB1893 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026SB1893 Introduced 2/6/2025, by Sen. Robert F. Martwick SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 40 ILCS 5/17-142.1  from Ch. 108 1/2, par. 17-142.1    Provides that the total payments under a provision concerning the Board providing partial reimbursement of health insurance costs may not exceed $100,000,000 (instead of $65,000,000) in any year. Provides that the amount shall annually thereafter be increased by the annual unadjusted percentage increase (but not less than zero) in the consumer price index for the 12 months ending with the September preceding each November 1, including all previous adjustments. Defines "consumer price index".
