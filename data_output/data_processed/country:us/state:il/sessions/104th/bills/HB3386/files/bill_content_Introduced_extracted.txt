Full Text of HB3386 ILGA.GOV HB3386 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026HB3386 Introduced 2/18/2025, by Rep. Mary Gill and Michelle Mussman SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 5 ILCS 100/5-45.65 new210 ILCS 47/3-217 new    Amends the ID/DD Community Care Act. Provides that, by no later than January 1, 2026, the Department of Public Health shall adopt and implement streamlined and person-centered procedures for appealing a resident assessment conducted by the inspection of care team. Requires the Department to adopt rules, including emergency rules, to implement the provisions. Amends the Illinois Administrative Procedure Act to make a conforming change.
