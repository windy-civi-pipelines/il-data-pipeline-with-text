SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED:
 
New Act
    Creates the Energy Utility Sales Agent Licensing Act. Defines terms. Provides that no individual or entity shall act as a sales agent for an energy utility in the State without first obtaining a license from the Illinois Commerce Commission. Provides that all licensed sales agents must carry his or her license during all sales activities and provide proof of licensure upon request by consumers or the Commission. Sets forth provisions for the application for licensure; training and continuing education requirements; penalties; and rulemaking.
