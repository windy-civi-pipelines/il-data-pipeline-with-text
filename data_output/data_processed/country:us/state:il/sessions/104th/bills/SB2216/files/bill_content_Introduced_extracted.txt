Full Text of SB2216 ILGA.GOV SB2216 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026SB2216 Introduced 2/7/2025, by Sen. Suzy Glowiak Hilton SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: New Act    Creates the Energy Utility Sales Agent Licensing Act. Defines terms. Provides that no individual or entity shall act as a sales agent for an energy utility in the State without first obtaining a license from the Illinois Commerce Commission. Provides that all licensed sales agents must carry his or her license during all sales activities and provide proof of licensure upon request by consumers or the Commission. Within 90 days of the 3effective date of this Act, the Commission shall create an 4application form for a sales agent license and make the form 5available on the Commission's website. Once issued by the 6Commission, a license is valid for a period of 2 years from the 7date of issuance.
