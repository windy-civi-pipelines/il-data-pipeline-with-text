Full Text of HB3295 ILGA.GOV HB3295 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026HB3295 Introduced 2/18/2025, by Rep. Marcus C. Evans, Jr. SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 820 ILCS 175/40    Amends the Day and Temporary Labor Services Act. Provides that a day and temporary labor service agency may charge a placement fee to a third party client for employing a day or temporary laborer for whom a contract for work was effected by the day and temporary labor service agency as agreed upon in advance by the day and temporary labor service agency and the third party client.
