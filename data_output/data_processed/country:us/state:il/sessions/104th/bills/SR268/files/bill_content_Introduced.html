SENATE RESOLUTION

 
2    WHEREAS, The members of the Illinois Senate are saddened 
3to learn of the death of Matthew Patrick "Matt" Jones of 
4Edwards, who passed away on April 26, 2025; and
 
5    WHEREAS, Matt Jones was born in Pekin on September 30, 
61965; he graduated from Pekin Community High School in 1983; 
7he received an associate's degree from Illinois Central 
8College; he earned his bachelor's degree and Juris Doctor from 
9the University of Illinois, completing his education in 1991; 
10during college, he served as a national officer for the honor 
11society Phi Theta Kappa and was a member of Sigma Phi Epsilon 
12Fraternity; and
 
13    WHEREAS, Matt Jones served as an intern for the late 
14Illinois Governor James T. Thompson through the Michael Curry 
15Summer Internship Program; he worked as an assistant state's 
16attorney in Peoria County before joining the legal counsel 
17team of Illinois Senate President Pate Philip, where he 
18developed expertise in Illinois' criminal code; he later 
19worked in the private sector as a lobbyist with Chicago-based 
20law firms Sidley Austin LLP and Altheimer & Gray, and he 
21practiced privately at Johnson, Bunce, and Noble, P.C. in 
22Peoria; for over 15 years, he served as associate director of 
23the Office of the Illinois Appellate Prosecutor and as an
