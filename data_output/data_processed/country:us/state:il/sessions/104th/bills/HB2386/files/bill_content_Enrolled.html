17direct support of the United Nations, or U.S. operations of 
18assistance for friendly foreign nations. The special plates 
19issued pursuant to this Section shall be affixed only to 
20passenger vehicles of the first division, motorcycles, and 
21motor vehicles of the second division weighing not more than
