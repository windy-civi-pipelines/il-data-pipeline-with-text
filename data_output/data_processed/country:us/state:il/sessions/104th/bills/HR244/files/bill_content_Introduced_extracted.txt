and 6    WHEREAS, Founded on April 15, 1925, Caterpillar has grown 7to become a prominent name in the construction industry; the 8company is renowned for its extensive range of services and 9products painted in its iconic yellow color, including 10construction and mining equipment, diesel and natural gas 11engines, industrial gas turbines, and diesel-electric 12locomotives, as well as products that support energy 13transition, including hybrid and electric power solutions; and 14    WHEREAS, Caterpillar employs over 100,000 people worldwide 15with significant employment in Illinois, where several major 16facilities are located, directly supporting over 19,000 jobs 17in the State; and 18    WHEREAS, Caterpillar's unwavering commitment to its core 19values of integrity, excellence, teamwork, commitment, and 20sustainability has driven the company's operations and 21established a strong corporate culture; and   HR0244- 2 -LRB104 13111 ECR 25197 r1    WHEREAS, Caterpillar's code of conduct, titled "Values in 2Action", encompasses a range of initiatives designed to uphold 3ethical conduct, environmental stewardship, and community 4engagement, reflecting the company's dedication to making a 5positive impact on society; and 6    WHEREAS, Caterpillar has demonstrated a profound 7commitment to philanthropy, contributing millions of dollars 8annually through the Caterpillar Foundation to support various 9causes, including disaster relief, education, and economic 10development in communities around the world; therefore, be it 11    RESOLVED, BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE 12HUNDRED FOURTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF ILLINOIS, that 13we recognize Caterpillar Inc. on achieving its 100th 14anniversary, and we commend the company for its remarkable 15achievements, enduring legacy, and continued contributions to 16the economy, society, and the global community; and be it 17further 18    RESOLVED, That a suitable copy of this resolution be 19presented to Caterpillar as a symbol of our esteem and 20respect.
