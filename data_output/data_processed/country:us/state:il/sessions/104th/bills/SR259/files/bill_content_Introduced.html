SENATE RESOLUTION

 
2    WHEREAS, The members of the Illinois Senate are saddened 
3to learn of the death of Lillie Mae Tillis of North Lawndale; 
4and
 
5    WHEREAS, Lillie Tillis was born in Lambert, Mississippi to 
6Mamie and N.I. Tillis on December 22, 1950, joining her older 
7brother, Willie Smith, as part of the family; at the age of 11, 
8she moved with her family to Chicago's West Side; she attended 
9Hess Elementary School and graduated from Carter H. Harrison 
10High School; and
 
11    WHEREAS, Lillie Tillis earned a Keypunch Operation 
12certificate from the College of Automation in 1979, which 
13opened doors to a wide range of roles from sales clerk to legal 
14and data entry positions; she worked at Bank of America until 
15retiring in 2010 to help care for her grandchildren; and
 
16    WHEREAS, Lillie Tillis moved to Summit later in life, 
17where she built new bonds with neighbors and friends; she took 
18a job at a nearby game room in 2021, where she enjoyed 
19socializing with regulars and neighbors alike; and
 
20    WHEREAS, Lillie Tillis was a member of St. Rest MB Church; 
21she loved music and laughter and was never far from the dance
