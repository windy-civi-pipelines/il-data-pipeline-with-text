Full Text of HB0015 ILGA.GOV HB0015 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026HB0015 Introduced 1/9/2025, by Rep. Chris Miller SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: New Act    Creates the Illinois Cultivated Meat Act. Provides that it is unlawful for any person to manufacture for sale, sell, hold or offer for sale, or distribute cultivated meat. Provides that a person who knowingly violates this Act commits a Class C misdemeanor. Allows for rulemaking by the Department of Agriculture. Makes a finding and states the purpose.
