Full Text of SB2260 ILGA.GOV SB2260 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026SB2260 Introduced 2/7/2025, by Sen. Laura Fine SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 225 ILCS 10/4.1  from Ch. 23, par. 2214.1    Amends the Child Care Act of 1969. Provides that day care homes, group day care homes, and day care centers may hire on a probationary basis any employee or volunteer who authorizes a criminal background investigation and who meets the initial requirements set out by the Department of Children and Family Services or the Department of Early Childhood and any applicable federal laws or regulations. Provides that, pending completion of all of the background check requirements, the probationary employee or volunteer must be supervised at all times by an individual who received a qualifying result on all background check components.
