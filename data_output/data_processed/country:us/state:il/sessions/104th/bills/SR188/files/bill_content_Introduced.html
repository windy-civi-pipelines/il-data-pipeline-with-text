SENATE RESOLUTION

 
2    WHEREAS, The members of the Illinois Senate are saddened 
3to learn of the death of John H. "Jack" Long of Springfield, 
4who passed away on March 9, 2025; and
 
5    WHEREAS, Jack Long was born in Jacksonville to Walter 
6Myron and Kathleen Vortman Long on September 29, 1939; he 
7served in the U.S. Army; he married Rosemarie Feleccia on 
8August 10, 1963; and
 
9    WHEREAS, Jack Long was a bricklayer and worked for 
10Pillsbury; he retired from the State of Illinois as a 
11stationary engineer; he also served as the assessor for 
12Springfield Township from 1993 to 2009; and
 
13    WHEREAS, Jack Long's passion was spending time playing 
14golf with his friends and family; he was also an avid sports 
15fan, and his favorite teams were the St. Louis Cardinals and 
16the St. Louis Rams; he was also a University of Illinois fan 
17and enjoyed watching games with his family and friends; he was 
18a coach for the Dale Teubner Memorial Little League and 5th and 
196th grade basketball at St. Cabrini; he also was a major 
20supporter of Williamsville girls softball and loved watching 
21his two youngest granddaughters pitch; and
