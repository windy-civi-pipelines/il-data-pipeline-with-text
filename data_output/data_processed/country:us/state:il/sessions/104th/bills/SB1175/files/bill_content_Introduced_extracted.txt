Full Text of SB1175 ILGA.GOV SB1175 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026SB1175 Introduced 1/24/2025, by Sen. Michael W. Halpin SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 20 ILCS 605/605-1118 new    Amends the Department of Commerce and Economic Opportunity Law of the Civil Administrative Code of Illinois. Provides that the Department of Commerce and Economic Opportunity shall establish a property tax rebate program that allows licensed, not-for-profit child care centers that were obligated to pay property taxes to a unit of local government or special district in the State to apply to the Department for a rebate of those taxes. Grants the Department rulemaking authority to implement this provision.
