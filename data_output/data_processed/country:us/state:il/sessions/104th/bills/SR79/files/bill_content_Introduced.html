SENATE RESOLUTION

 
2    WHEREAS, The members of the Illinois Senate are saddened 
3to learn of the death of A. Dale Huston of Springfield, who 
4passed away on January 20, 2025; and
 
5    WHEREAS, Dale Huston, the son of Frederic W. "Ted" and 
6Dorothy B. (Jardine) Huston, was born in Gibson City on 
7September 5, 1940; he was raised on a livestock farm near 
8Gibson City, graduating from the local high school; he earned 
9a Bachelor of Science in Agronomy from the University of 
10Illinois; he also graduated from the Illinois Bankers 
11Association School of Banking at Southern Illinois University 
12Carbondale, the Graduate School of Banking at the University 
13of Wisconsin-Madison, and the Senior Commercial Lenders School 
14at the University of Oklahoma Norman; he married Judy A. Dube 
15at Fort Bliss in El Paso, Texas in 1963; and
 
16    WHEREAS, Dale Huston belonged to the Reserve Officers' 
17Training Corps (ROTC) program while attending the University 
18of Illinois, where he received his commission as a second 
19lieutenant; he served in the U. S. Army during the Vietnam Era 
20from 1962 to 1964, and he was assigned to the 6th Missile 
21Battalion (HAWK), 71st Artillery, Fort Bliss, Texas; he then 
22served in the U.S. Army Reserve from 1965 to 1969; and
