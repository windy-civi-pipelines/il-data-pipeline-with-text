Full Text of SB2667 ILGA.GOV SB2667 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026SB2667 Introduced 5/26/2025, by Sen. Sara Feigenholtz SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 5 ILCS 100/5-45.65 new230 ILCS 45/25-10230 ILCS 45/25-66 new    Amends the Sports Wagering Act. Specifies that, for purposes of the Act, the term "sports event" includes any awards show. Provides that "awards show" means the Emmy Awards Show, the Grammy Awards Show, the Academy Awards Show, the Tony Awards Show, or any other live competition or talent contest. Grants rulemaking and emergency rulemaking powers to the Illinois Gaming Board to authorize wagering on awards shows. Amends the Illinois Administrative Procedure Act to make conforming changes.
