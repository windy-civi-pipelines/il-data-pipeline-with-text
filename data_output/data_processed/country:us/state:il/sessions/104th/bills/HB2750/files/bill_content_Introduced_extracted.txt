Full Text of HB2750 ILGA.GOV HB2750 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026HB2750 Introduced 2/6/2025, by Rep. Barbara Hernandez SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 305 ILCS 5/12-4.13b    Amends the Administration Article of the Illinois Public Aid Code. Provides that any program of study at a public institution of higher education improves employability and shall be considered equivalent to an acceptable SNAP Employment and Training (E&T) program component, unless prohibited by federal law. Defines "public institution of higher education". Requires the Department of Human Services to adopt any rules necessary to implement the amendatory Act. Provides that rulemaking shall not delay full implementation of the amendatory Act.
