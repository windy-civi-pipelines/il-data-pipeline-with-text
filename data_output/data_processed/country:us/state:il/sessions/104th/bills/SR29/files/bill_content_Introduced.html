2    WHEREAS, The members of the Illinois Senate are saddened

6    WHEREAS, David Clark was born to Robert Lincoln Clark and 
7Mary Rose Clark in Honolulu, Hawaii on July 4, 1938; he served 
8in the U.S. Navy; he resided in Des Plaines for the majority of 
9his life; and
 
10    WHEREAS, David Clark joined the Des Plaines Fire 
11Department on May 26, 1962, serving as Fire Chief of the Des 
12Plaines Fire Department from 1985 to 1993; while at the helm, 
13he spearheaded professionalizing the fire department; under 
14his leadership, the fire department was transformed into one 
15that emphasized better training and better equipment, 
16increased the minimum man requirements on fire trucks, and led 
17the effort in requiring fire sprinklers in certain types of 
18residential buildings; he retired on September 4, 1993; and
 
19    WHEREAS, David Clark loved traveling and taking road trips 
20with his wife, Martha; he was an avid reader of history, 
21particularly naval aviation and World War II; he was a 
22lifelong Chicago Bears fan; and
