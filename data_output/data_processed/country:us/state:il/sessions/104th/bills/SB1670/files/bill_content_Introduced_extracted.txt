Full Text of SB1670 ILGA.GOV SB1670 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026SB1670 Introduced 2/5/2025, by Sen. Christopher Belt SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 820 ILCS 154/5820 ILCS 154/10    Amends the Family Bereavement Leave Act. Provides that all employees shall be entitled to use a maximum of one week (5 work days) of unpaid bereavement leave to grieve the death of a covered companion animal. Provides that, in the event of the death of more than one covered companion animal in a 12-month period, an employee is entitled to up to a total of 2 weeks (10 work days) of bereavement leave during the 12-month period. Defines "companion animal".
