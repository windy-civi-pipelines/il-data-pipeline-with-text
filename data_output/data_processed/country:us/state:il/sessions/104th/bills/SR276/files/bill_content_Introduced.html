SENATE RESOLUTION

 
2    WHEREAS, The members of the Illinois Senate wish to 
3congratulate Anne Flanigan Bassi on the occasion of her 
4retirement as Moraine Township Supervisor; and
 
5    WHEREAS, Township Supervisor Flanigan Bassi possesses a 
6Master of Science in Taxation; outside of her duties to the 
7township, she works professionally as a certified public 
8accountant (CPA) and operates a private tax practice; and
 
9    WHEREAS, Township Supervisor Flanigan Bassi has served as 
10Moraine Township Supervisor since 2013; during her tenure, she 
11has implemented a number of programs and initiatives, 
12including Volunteer Income Tax Assistance in conjunction with 
13the IRS, access to health care through the Affordable Care 
14Act, special food access and deliveries for Food Pantry 
15clients during the COVID-19 pandemic, and the Hunger Summit to 
16assess the food insecurity epidemic within the township, 
17leading to expanded food programs, particularly for students 
18and families with children; she also spearheaded the efforts 
19to create the Community Mental Health Board; and
 
20    WHEREAS, Anne Flanigan Bassi has served her community and 
21Lake County in a number of other roles over the decades; she 
22currently serves on the Lake County Housing and Community
