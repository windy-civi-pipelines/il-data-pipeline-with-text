Full Text of SB1297 ILGA.GOV SB1297 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026SB1297 Introduced 1/28/2025, by Sen. Doris Turner SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 15 ILCS 405/21  from Ch. 15, par. 221    Amends the State Comptroller Act. Provides that the Comptroller may provide in his or her rules and regulations for periodic transfers, with the approval of the State Treasurer, for use in accordance with the imprest system, subject to the rules and regulations of the Comptroller as respects vouchers, controls, and reports to the Department of Agriculture to pay State Fair competition personnel and entertainment support contracts necessary to provide the entertainment at each State Fair.
