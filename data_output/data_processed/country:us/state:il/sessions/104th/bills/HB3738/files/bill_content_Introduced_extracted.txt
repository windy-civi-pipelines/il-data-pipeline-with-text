Full Text of HB3738 ILGA.GOV HB3738 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026HB3738 Introduced 2/18/2025, by Rep. Abdelnasser Rashid SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 35 ILCS 5/235 new    Amends the Illinois Income Tax Act. Creates an income tax credit for taxpayers who (1) paid rent on a personal residence in Illinois for at least 6 months during the taxable year; (2) are not claimed as a dependent on someone else's federal income taxes for the taxable year; and (3) have a federal adjusted gross income of less than $83,250, if the taxpayer is married filing a joint return, or $40,770, if the taxpayer is not married filing a joint return. Provides that, if the taxpayer is married filing a joint return, then the amount of the credit is $1,000 for both taxpayers as a single unit. Provides that, if the taxpayer is not married filing a joint return, then the amount of the credit is $500.
