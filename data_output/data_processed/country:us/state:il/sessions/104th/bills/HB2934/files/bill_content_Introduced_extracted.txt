Full Text of HB2934 ILGA.GOV HB2934 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026HB2934 Introduced 2/6/2025, by Rep. Maura Hirschauer - Kam Buckner SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 625 ILCS 5/11-601  from Ch. 95 1/2, par. 11-601    Amends the Illinois Vehicle Code. Provides that, on and after October 1, 2025, the default speed limit is 20 (instead of 30) miles per hour within an urban district, and 10 (instead of 15) miles per hour in an alley within an urban district. Requires, by September 1, 2025, the Secretary of State to communicate the speed limit change to every licensed driver in the State via direct postal mail and a broad statewide communications campaign. Provides that a county, municipality, or township with speed enforcement authority may only issue warnings for violations during the first 60 days after the change of the speed limit is enacted.
