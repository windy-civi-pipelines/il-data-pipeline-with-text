Diane Blair-Sherlock SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 765 ILCS 160/1-72 new    Amends the Common Interest Community Association Act. Provides that an association may not prohibit a law enforcement officer or a firefighter who is a parcel owner, or who is a tenant, guest, or invitee of a parcel owner, from parking his or her assigned law enforcement vehicle or firefighter vehicle in an area where the parcel owner, or the tenant, guest, or invitee of the parcel owner, otherwise has a right to park.LRB104 02905 LNS 12915 b  A BILL FOR HB0067LRB104 02905 LNS 12915 b1    AN ACT concerning civil law. 2    Be it enacted by the People of the State of Illinois, 3represented in the General Assembly: 4    Section 5. The Common Interest Community Association Act 5is amended by adding Section 1-72 as follows: 6    (765 ILCS 160/1-72 new)7    Sec. 1-72. Law enforcement and firefighter vehicles. An 8association may not prohibit a law enforcement officer, as 9defined in Section 2 of the Illinois Police Training Act, or a 10firefighter, as defined in Section 10-2.1-6.4 of the Illinois 11Municipal Code, who is a parcel owner, or who is a tenant, 12guest, or invitee of a parcel owner, from parking his or her 13assigned law enforcement vehicle or firefighter vehicle in an 14area where the parcel owner, or the tenant, guest, or invitee 15of the parcel owner, otherwise has a right to park.
