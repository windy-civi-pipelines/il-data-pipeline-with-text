Full Text of HB3055 ILGA.GOV HB3055 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026HB3055 Introduced 2/6/2025, by Rep. Barbara Hernandez SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 110 ILCS 947/68 new    Amends the Higher Education Student Assistance Act. Provides that, notwithstanding any other law, a student who otherwise qualifies for any grant or scholarship offered under this Act, by any other State law, or by a unit of local government shall not be denied the award of the grant or scholarship based solely on the student's status as a noncitizen.
