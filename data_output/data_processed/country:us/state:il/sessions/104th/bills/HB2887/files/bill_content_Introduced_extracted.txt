Terra Costa Howard SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 5 ILCS 140/2  from Ch. 116, par. 2025 ILCS 140/3.2    Amends the Freedom of Information Act. Reduces the number of record requests that must be made for a person to be considered a recurrent requester under the Act. Provides that public bodies must respond to requests from recurrent requesters with 30 (rather than 21) days after receipt of a request. Specifies that notice that requests are being treated as recurrent requests must be provided only once every 30 days.
