Full Text of SB2102 ILGA.GOV SB2102 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026SB2102 Introduced 2/6/2025, by Sen. Erica Harriss SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 35 ILCS 200/18-50    Amends the Property Tax Code. Provides that a taxing district's budget and appropriation ordinance and estimate of revenues may be filed electronically with the county clerk. Provides that, if a taxing district's budget and appropriation ordinance and estimate of revenues are filed electronically with the county clerk, the county clerk shall accept and acknowledge that electronic filing by providing a receipt to the taxing district.
