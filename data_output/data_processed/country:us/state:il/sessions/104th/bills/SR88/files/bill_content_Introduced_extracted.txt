and 5    WHEREAS, Joan Edwards was born to Joe M. and Lena Mae 6(Cavaness) Graves in Mounds on September 3, 1929; she was a 7resident of Southern Illinois for all of her life, moving to 8Anna when she was two years old; and 9    WHEREAS, Joan Edwards worked as an licensed practical 10nurse (LPN) administrator at Choate Mental Health as well as a 11dietary and purchasing agent for Head Start; and 12    WHEREAS, Joan Edwards was a community servant of the 13highest caliber and always an outspoken advocate for Union 14County; as a hospital board member for 25 years, her proudest 15achievement was having the heliport at Union County Hospital 16named after her due to her efforts in securing it; and 17    WHEREAS, Joan Edwards was a member of Mount Moriah 18Lutheran Church and several other organizations, including the 19Daughters of the Union Veterans and the Masonic Order of the 20White Shrine of Jerusalem, where she held the honor of Past 21Worthy High Priestess; she was also a Kentucky Colonel of the   SR0088- 2 -LRB104 11908 LAW 21999 r1Honorable Order of Kentucky Colonels, a commission of 2distinction that can only be given by the Governor of the 3Commonwealth of Kentucky; and 4    WHEREAS, Joan Edwards will be remembered as the 5"Queen-Mom" of her local Red Hat Society; and 6    WHEREAS, Joan Edwards was preceded in death by her 7parents; her son, Major Louis Joe Wohlwend; her granddaughter, 8Jackie Edwards; and one great-grandson; and 9    WHEREAS, Joan Edwards is survived by her sons, Quinn 10Edwards and Jason Edwards; her daughters-in-law, Holly 11Wohlwend and Katie Edwards; her stepdaughter, Mary Lenigk; 12five grandchildren; and four great-grandchildren; therefore, 13be it 14    RESOLVED, BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTH GENERAL 15ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF ILLINOIS, that we mourn the passing of 16Joan Cavaness (Graves) Edwards and extend our sincere 17condolences to her family, friends, and all who knew and loved 18her; and be it further 19    RESOLVED, That a suitable copy of this resolution be 20presented to the family of Joan Edwards as an expression of our 21deepest sympathy.
