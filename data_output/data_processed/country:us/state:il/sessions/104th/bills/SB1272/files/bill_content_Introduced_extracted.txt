Adriane Johnson SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 625 ILCS 5/11-306.1 new    Amends the Illinois Vehicle Code. Amends the Illinois Vehicle Code. Provides that a municipality or unit of local government may erect and maintain a specialized traffic control device at an intersection where an emergency response vehicle enters the roadway or within 1,000 feet from a structure where such vehicles are stored.
