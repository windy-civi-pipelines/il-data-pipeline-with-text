Full Text of HB2932 ILGA.GOV HB2932 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026HB2932 Introduced 2/6/2025, by Rep. Nabeela Syed SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 105 ILCS 80/20    Amends the Speech Rights of Student Journalists Act. Provides that any student or student media adviser may institute proceedings for injunctive or declaratory relief in any court of competent jurisdiction to enforce the rights to free speech or free press. Provides that nothing shall be construed to create any private action on behalf of a student other than to seek injunctive relief allowing the publication of the speech in question. Allows a court to award reasonable attorney's fees to a plaintiff that substantially prevails.LRB104 10290 LNS 20364 b  A BILL FOR HB2932LRB104 10290 LNS 20364 b1    AN ACT concerning education. 2    Be it enacted by the People of the State of Illinois, 3represented in the General Assembly: 4    Section 5.
