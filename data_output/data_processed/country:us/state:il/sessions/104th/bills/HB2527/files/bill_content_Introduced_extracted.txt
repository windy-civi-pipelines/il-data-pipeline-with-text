Full Text of HB2527 ILGA.GOV HB2527 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026HB2527 Introduced 2/4/2025, by Rep. Jaime M. Andrade, Jr. SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 225 ILCS 45/1  from Ch. 111 1/2, par. 73.101225 ILCS 45/1a  from Ch. 111 1/2, par. 73.101a225 ILCS 45/2c new225 ILCS 45/10  from Ch. 111 1/2, par. 73.110    Amends the Illinois Funeral or Burial Funds Act. Defines "transportation protection agreement". Provides that the Illinois Insurance Code does not apply to any transportation protection agreement sold by any seller. Provides that nothing in the Act shall be deemed to apply to (1) merchandise that is delivered within 30 days of purchase, (2) a transportation protection agreement, or (3) pre-need cemetery sales (rather than only pre-need cemetery sales) under the Illinois Pre-Need Cemetery Sales Act.
