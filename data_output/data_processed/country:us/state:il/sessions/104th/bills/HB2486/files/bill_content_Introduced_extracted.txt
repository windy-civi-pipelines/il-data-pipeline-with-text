Full Text of HB2486 ILGA.GOV HB2486 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026HB2486 Introduced 2/4/2025, by Rep. Katie Stuart SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 105 ILCS 5/14-1.08a    Amends the Children with Disabilities Article of the School Code. Provides that "separate public special education day school" does not mean any school in a school district that exceeds its Adequacy Target under the evidence-based funding formula.
