2    WHEREAS, The members of the Illinois House of 
3Representatives wish to congratulate Patrick Statter on the 
4occasion of his retirement from the Northeastern Federation of 
5Labor; and
 
6    WHEREAS, Patrick Statter joined the United Retail Workers 
7Union in 1979 and has dedicated the last 45 years to 
8representing working people, including 40 years on the Central 
9Labor Council, serving as a steward for his local union, and 
10working in several business and leadership roles within the 
11United Food and Commercial Workers Union (UFCW); and
 
12    WHEREAS, Patrick Statter, since being tasked by Local 881 
13UFCW President Ron Powell to assume the role of president of 
14the Northeastern Federation of Labor, has served with 
15distinction for 18 years and found a unique purpose in 
16bringing the federation into a new era for organized labor in 
17Illinois; and
 
18    WHEREAS, Under Patrick Statter's leadership, the activity 
19and influence of the Northeastern Federation of Labor grew 
20exponentially, and the NEFL now enjoys a reputation as a 
21regional leader in advancing justice and opportunity for all 
22Illinoisans; and
