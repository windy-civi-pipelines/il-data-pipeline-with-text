The purpose of this Act is to ensure 7Illinois proactively plans for climate-driven displacement by 8identifying the projected climate risks and climate-driven 9displacement and the future resource needs, supporting 10community resilience, and developing policies in collaboration 11with residents, advocates, and industry stakeholders.
