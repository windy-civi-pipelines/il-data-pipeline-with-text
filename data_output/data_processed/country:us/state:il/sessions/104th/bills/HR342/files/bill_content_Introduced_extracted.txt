and 6    WHEREAS, On April 23, 2025, at approximately 10:30 p.m., 7Andy Goforth and Daven Hug were on their way home when they 8noticed smoke billowing from the second floor of the senior 9apartments at the Village Apartments in Nashville, Illinois; 10and 11    WHEREAS, After calling 9-1-1, Andy Goforth and Daven Hug 12selflessly ran into the building and started heroically 13notifying and evacuating residents; and 14    WHEREAS, As Andy Goforth and Daven Hug were risking their 15lives to help the residents of the building, seven volunteer 16fire departments joined together to respond to this emergency; 17working together, the seven rural fire departments 18extinguished the fire; and 19    WHEREAS, The first responders of Southern Illinois banded 20together to answer this emergency; the fire departments of 21Nashville, Hoyleton, Addieville, Ashley, Okawville, Carlyle,   HR0342- 2 -LRB104 13674 MST 26337 r1and Coulterville and the ambulance services of Washington 2County, Pinckneyville, New Baden, and Litton County bravely 3responded to the emergency and were instrumental in preventing 4further harm; and 5    WHEREAS, Two residents were airlifted to the Mercy Burn 6Center at Mercy Hospital in Saint Louis while two other 7residents were admitted to the Washington County Hospital, 8where one was released on April 27 with minor injuries and the 9other remains in hospital; and 10    WHEREAS, Without the quick and heroic action of Andy 11Goforth and Daven Hug, this incident could have resulted in a 12tragic loss of life; therefore, be it 13    RESOLVED, BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE 14HUNDRED FOURTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF ILLINOIS, that 15we commend the brave actions of Andy Goforth and Daven Hug and 16honor them for nobly putting their lives on the line to protect 17some of Illinois' most vulnerable residents; and be it further 18    RESOLVED, That suitable copies of this resolution be 19presented to Andy Goforth and Daven Hug as an expression of our 20esteem and respect.
