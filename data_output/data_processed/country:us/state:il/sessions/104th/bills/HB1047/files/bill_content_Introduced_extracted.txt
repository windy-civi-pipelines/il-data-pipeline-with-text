Full Text of HB1047 ILGA.GOV HB1047 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026HB1047 Introduced 1/9/2025, by Rep. John M. Cabello SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: New Act5 ILCS 100/5-45.61 new    Creates the Social Gaming Act. Provides that no person may operate a social gaming board game or manufacture and distribute social gaming boards without a license issued by the Illinois Gaming Board. Provides that the Board shall license social gaming board manufacturers, social gaming board distributors, and social gaming locations and specifies license fees. Provides that the Board shall report to the Governor and the General Assembly, no later than December 31, 2025 and each year thereafter through 2029, on the implementation and administration of the Act. Sets forth provisions concerning license fees, maximum payouts, and relief from disciplinary actions. Provides that the Board shall adopt rules and emergency rules to implement and administer the Act. Makes a conforming change in the Illinois Administrative Procedure Act.
