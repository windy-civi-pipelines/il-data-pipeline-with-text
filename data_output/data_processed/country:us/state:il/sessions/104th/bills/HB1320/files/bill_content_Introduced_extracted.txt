Davidsmeyer SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 20 ILCS 5/5-735 new    Amends the Civil Administrative Code of Illinois. Provides that each Department shall restore, within 18 months after closure, the site of a facility operated by the Department to its original condition before the facility was built if the facility is closed and no longer is maintained as a facility.
