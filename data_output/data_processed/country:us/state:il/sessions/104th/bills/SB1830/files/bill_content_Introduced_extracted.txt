Full Text of SB1830 ILGA.GOV SB1830 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026SB1830 Introduced 2/5/2025, by Sen. Chapin Rose SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 35 ILCS 200/1-130    Amends the Property Tax Code. Provides that, except as provided with respect to mobile homes, a building or structure that is not affixed to or installed on a permanent foundation or connected to utilities for year-round occupancy is not considered real property. Effective immediately.
