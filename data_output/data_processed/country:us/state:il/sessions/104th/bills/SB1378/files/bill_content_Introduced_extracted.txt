Chapin Rose SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 110 ILCS 1010/3  from Ch. 144, par. 233    Amends the Academic Degree Act. With respect to a public university, provides that only the board of trustees of the university may award an honorary degree, and the university's faculty shall have no role in the award of the honorary degree.LRB104 06343 LNS 16379 b  A BILL FOR SB1378LRB104 06343 LNS 16379 b1    AN ACT concerning education. 2    Be it enacted by the People of the State of Illinois, 3represented in the General Assembly: 4    Section 5. The Academic Degree Act is amended by changing 5Section 3 as follows: 6    (110 ILCS 1010/3)  (from Ch. 144, par. 233)7    Sec. 3. Award of degrees.) A degree or any object in 8evidence thereof may be awarded only by a degree granting 9institution. Anything in this Act to the contrary 10notwithstanding, a degree granting institution may award 11honorary degrees; provided that any such degree be clearly 12represented to be honorary and that any other tangible object 13issued or purporting to be in evidence thereof shall state on 14its face expressly, clearly and conspicuously that the degree 15is honorary.
