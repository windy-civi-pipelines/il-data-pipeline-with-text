Joyce Mason SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 820 ILCS 70/10    Amends the Employee Credit Privacy Act. Provides that an employer shall not order or obtain an applicant's social security number, except for the purpose of conducting a background check of the applicant at the time the background check is completed.
