SENATE RESOLUTION

 
2    WHEREAS, The members of the Illinois Senate are saddened 
3to learn of the death of Dr. Jose "Joe" Austriaco of Park 
4Ridge, formerly of Lombard and Oak Brook, who passed away on 
5January 29, 2025; and
 
6    WHEREAS, Dr. Austriaco, the son of Cesar and Lucia 
7Dumaguit Austriaco, was born in Manila, Philippines on 
8December 8, 1934; and
 
9    WHEREAS, Dr. Austriaco immigrated to the United States at 
10the age of 34 in April 1969; while studying for his Educational 
11Commission for Foreign Medical Graduates (ECFMG) exam, he 
12worked as an x-ray technician to earn a living, in preparation 
13for his wife, Lina, and their children to arrive in November; 
14upon passing the exam, he began practicing medicine in 1973, 
15and having saved enough money, he and Lina moved their family 
16to Oak Brook; he continued practicing family medicine in 
17Maywood and Chicago until 2000, and he served as staff at St. 
18Anthony Hospital; and
 
19    WHEREAS, Dr. Austriaco was very involved in many Filipino 
20organizations, including the Philippines Medical Association 
21of Chicago (PMAC), the Philippines National Basketball 
22Association of America (PNBAA), and the Chicago Philippines
