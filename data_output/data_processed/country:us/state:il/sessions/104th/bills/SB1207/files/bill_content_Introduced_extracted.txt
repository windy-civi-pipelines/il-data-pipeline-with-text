Steve McClure SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 35 ILCS 200/21-27    Amends the Property Tax Code. Provides that no interest or penalties shall be imposed with respect to property that is included in a decedent's probate estate at the time of a delinquency if the representative of the decedent's estate applies with the county treasurer for a waiver of those amounts and is granted that waiver.
