SENATE RESOLUTION

 
2    WHEREAS, The members of the Illinois Senate are saddened 
3to learn of the death of Thomas Foster "Tom" Londrigan of 
4Springfield, who passed away on January 28, 2025; and
 
5    WHEREAS, Tom Londrigan, the son of Joseph A. and Loretta 
6(Foster) Londrigan, was born in Springfield on May 10, 1937; 
7he attended Hazel Dell Elementary School, Little Flower 
8School, and Cathedral Boys High School, where he was an 
9All-City football player; he graduated from the University of 
10Notre Dame, where he was chosen as the first captain of the 
11university's inaugural varsity swim team, and he received his 
12Juris Doctor from the University of Illinois; he served in the 
13U.S. Air National Guard on kitchen duty and later in the Judge 
14Advocate General's Office; he also served in the U.S. Navy 
15Reserve from 1962 to 1980, rising to the rank of commander; he 
16married Carol Ann Fish at the Cathedral of the Immaculate 
17Conception on August 31, 1963; and
 
18    WHEREAS, Tom Londrigan first worked as an Assistant United 
19States Attorney for the U.S. Department of Justice under 
20Robert F. Kennedy; he later worked at his father's firm of 
21Londrigan and Londrigan before creating his own firm, 
22Londrigan and Potter, later known as Londrigan, Potter & 
23Randle, P.C.; during his career, he served as president of the
