9    (a) The General Assembly finds the following:
10        (1) There are persistent adverse health effects that

14    hospital or other health care provider and are

16    vulnerable housing situation, would benefit from 
17    information on shelter and homeless support services.
18        (3) There are opportunities to improve systems of care 
19    for individuals experiencing homelessness in Illinois.

21Department of Human Services shall maintain on the 
22Department's publicly accessible website information on how a 
23hospital or health care provider may connect a patient who is
