Morrison SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 425 ILCS 60/3.1 new425 ILCS 60/4  from Ch. 127 1/2, par. 804    Amends the Smoke Detector Act. Bans the selling, offering for sale, or giving as a gift a smoke detector that is not designed to receive primary power from the building wiring or does not meet certain battery requirements set forth in the Act.
