Full Text of HB4021 ILGA.GOV HB4021 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026HB4021 Introduced 3/19/2025, by Rep. Katie Stuart SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED:     Appropriates $340,000 from the General Revenue Fund to the State Board of Education to address shortages of school psychologists in this State through specified methods. Effective July 1, 2025.LRB104 11370 LNS 21458 b HB4021LRB104 11370 LNS 21458 b1    AN ACT concerning appropriations.  2    Be it enacted by the People of the State of Illinois, 3represented in the General Assembly: 4    Section 5. The sum of $340,000, or so much of that sum as 5may be necessary, is appropriated from the General Revenue 6Fund to the State Board of Education to address shortages of 7school psychologists in this State by hiring or contracting 8for a school psychology liaison within the State Board of 9Education, providing a grant to fund a program within the 10Illinois School Psychology Internship Consortium to recruit 11and retain doctoral interns in faculty positions within public 12universities, and funding a school psychologist retention 13program with a group consultation model. 14    Section 99. Effective date. This Act takes effect July 1, 152025.
