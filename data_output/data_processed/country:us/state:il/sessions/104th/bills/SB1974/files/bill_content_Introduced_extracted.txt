Full Text of SB1974 ILGA.GOV SB1974 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026SB1974 Introduced 2/6/2025, by Sen. Laura Ellman SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 225 ILCS 150/20 new    Amends the Telehealth Act. Provides that no health care provider, health care facility, or associated entity shall impose or collect a facility fee in connection with any telehealth services provided to patients in the State of Illinois. Provides that the prohibition does not apply to any fees that are directly related to in-person services that may be required to supplement telehealth care if such fees are itemized and clearly communicated to the patient before the in-person services are provided.LRB104 10992 BAB 21074 b  A BILL FOR SB1974LRB104 10992 BAB 21074 b1    AN ACT concerning regulation. 2    Be it enacted by the People of the State of Illinois, 3represented in the General Assembly: 4    Section 5. The Telehealth Act is amended by adding Section 520 as follows: 6    (225 ILCS 150/20 new)7    Sec. 20. Facility fees for telehealth services.8    (a) As used in this Section, "facility fee" means any 9charge, cost, or fee imposed by a health care provider or 10health care facility in connection with the provision of 11telehealth services, excluding fees for the actual delivery of 12health care services.13    (b) No health care provider, health care facility, or 14associated entity shall impose or collect a facility fee in 15connection with any telehealth services provided to patients 16in the State of Illinois.17    (c) The prohibition in subsection (b) does not apply to 18any fees that are directly related to in-person services that 19may be required to supplement telehealth care if such fees are 20itemized and clearly communicated to the patient before the 21in-person services are provided.
