HOUSE RESOLUTION

 
2    WHEREAS, The members of the Illinois House of 
3Representatives mourn the passing of Caleb Thomas Elliott, a 
4constituent of the 109th House District, and would like to 
5spread awareness of his tragic and preventable incident; and
 
6    WHEREAS, Caleb Elliott, the son of Doug and Dawn Elliott, 
7tragically passed away on August 11, 2017 at the young age of 
819 years; his family wishes for this tragedy to bring to light 
9the dangers of unsafe electrical wiring; and
 
10    WHEREAS, Caleb Elliot was electrocuted to death while 
11working at a house in Troy; despite an inspection, the 
12building was not wired to code; simple mistakes like this can 
13have devastating consequences on communities and families; and
 
14    WHEREAS, Caleb Elliot is survived by his two sisters, 
15Lauren Schmidt and Rachaelle Elliott; his three brothers, 
16Christian, Brannon, and Jadon Elliot; and his parents, Doug 
17and Dawn Elliot, who wish to spread awareness about his case to 
18prevent it from happening again; and
 
19    WHEREAS, In the State of Illinois, electricians are 
20certified on the local and county level with each jurisdiction 
21passing its own rules or codes with which each electrician
