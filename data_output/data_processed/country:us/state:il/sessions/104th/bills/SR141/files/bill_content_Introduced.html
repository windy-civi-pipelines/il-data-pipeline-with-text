SENATE RESOLUTION

 
2    WHEREAS, The members of the Illinois Senate are saddened 
3to learn of the death of Harold Edwin King Sr. of Peoria, who 
4passed away on February 11, 2025; and
 
5    WHEREAS, Harold King was born to Harold William and Elva 
6(Abell) King in East St. Louis on February 24, 1929; he 
7attended Peoria High School; he graduated from Bradley 
8University in 1950, where he was a member of the swim team and 
9Lambda Chi Alpha fraternity; he married Ann Frauenthal in St. 
10Louis in 1950; he served in the Korean War; he later graduated 
11from the Indiana University Savings & Loan Institute; and
 
12    WHEREAS, Harold King started his financial career in 
13Alton; he returned to Peoria to become president of Provident 
14Federal Savings and Loan in 1965; he also served as a board 
15member of the Illinois League of Savings and Loans and as an 
16adviser at the Indiana University Savings & Loan Institute; 
17after retirement, he enjoyed a second career as CEO of East 
18Bluff Neighborhood Housing Services; and
 
19    WHEREAS, Harold King was an active member of the Peoria 
20Area Chamber of Commerce, a past president of the Downtown 
21Rotary in Peoria, and president of Mt. Hawley Country Club, 
22where he and Ann played tennis and golf; after they moved to
