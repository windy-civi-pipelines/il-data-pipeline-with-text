Full Text of SB0180 ILGA.GOV SB0180 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026SB0180 Introduced 1/17/2025, by Sen. Paul Faraci SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 705 ILCS 35/2f-14    Amends the Circuit Courts Act. Provides, in a provision concerning resident judgeships of the 6th judicial circuit, that when a vacancy occurs in an at-large judgeship, the at-large judgeship shall be converted to a resident judgeship for either Champaign County or Macon County, depending on which of those 2 counties the incumbent at-large circuit judge resided in when the incumbent judge was elected.
