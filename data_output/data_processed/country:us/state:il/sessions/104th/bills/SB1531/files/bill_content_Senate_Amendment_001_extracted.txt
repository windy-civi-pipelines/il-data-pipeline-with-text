Disposable food service containers composed of 13polystyrene foam prohibited. 14    (a) Except as provided in subsection (b), a person may not 15sell or distribute in this State a disposable food service 16container that is composed in whole or in part of polystyrene 17foam beginning January 1, 2030.18    (b) The prohibition in subsection (a) does not apply to 19any activity authorized under an ordinance or resolution 20adopted by a home rule municipality or home rule county on or 21before January 1, 2024 or with respect to sales made to a unit 22of local government for use by the unit of local government for 23its internal operations.24    (c) The prohibition in subsection (a) does not prohibit 25manufacturing of disposable food service containers that are   10400SB1531sam001- 3 -LRB104 10048 BDA 23419 a1composed in whole or in part of polystyrene foam for 2distribution or sale outside the State. 3    Section 15.
