2    AMENDMENT NO. ______. Amend Senate Bill 1531 by replacing

4    "Section 1. Short title. This Act may be cited as the 
5Disposable Food Service Container Act.
 
6    Section 5. Definitions. In this Act, unless the context 
7requires otherwise:
8    "Disposable food service container" means service ware 
9designed for one-time use. "Disposable food service container" 
10includes, but is not limited to, service ware for take-out 
11foods, bakery products, and leftovers from partially consumed 
12meals. "Disposable food service container" does not include 
13polystyrene foam coolers, ice chests that are used for the 
14processing or shipping of seafood, or service ware that is 
15used to contain, transport, or otherwise package raw, 
16uncooked, or butchered meat, poultry, fish, or seafood.

3    Section 15. Penalties and enforcement.
4    (a) A person who violates any provision of this Act shall 
5be provided with a written warning with a notice of violation; 
6a civil penalty of up to $500 for a second offense; and a civil 
7penalty of up to $1,000 for any subsequent offense within a 
812-month period.
9    (b) Any violation of this Act may be enforced by the 
10Attorney General or the State's Attorney of the county in 
11which the violation occurs. The penalties provided for in 
12subsection (a) may be recovered in a civil action brought in 
13the name of the people of the State of Illinois by the State's 
14Attorney of the county in which the violation occurred or by 
15the Attorney General. Any penalties collected under this 
16Section in an action in which the Attorney General has 
17prevailed shall be deposited into the Environmental Protection 
18Trust Fund, to be used in accordance with the provisions of the 
19Environmental Protection Trust Fund Act. The Attorney General 
20or the State's Attorney of a county in which a violation occurs 
21may institute a civil action for an injunction, prohibitory or 
22mandatory, to restrain violations of this Act or to require 
23such actions as may be necessary to address violations of this 
24Act.
