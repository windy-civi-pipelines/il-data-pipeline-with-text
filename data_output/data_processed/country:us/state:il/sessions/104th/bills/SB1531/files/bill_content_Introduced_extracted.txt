Full Text of SB1531 ILGA.GOV SB1531 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026SB1531 Introduced 2/4/2025, by Sen. Laura Fine SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: New Act    Creates the Disposable Food Service Container Act. Provides that, beginning January 1, 2030, a person may not sell or distribute in this State a disposable food service container that is composed in whole or in part of polystyrene foam. Specifies that the prohibition does not apply to any activity authorized under an ordinance or resolution adopted by a unit of local government on or before January 1, 2024 or with respect to sales made to a unit of local government for use by the unit of local government for its internal operations. Sets forth penalties for violations of the Act. Limits home rule powers.
