Full Text of HB0060 ILGA.GOV HB0060 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026HB0060 Introduced 1/9/2025, by Rep. Anthony DeLuca SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: New Act    Creates the Prohibition on Taxpayer Funding of Guaranteed Income Act. Provides that, on and after July 1, 2025, no unit of government may use taxpayer money to fund a guaranteed income program. Preempts the exercise of home rule powers. Effective July 1, 2025.LRB104 03699 HLH 13723 b  A BILL FOR HB0060LRB104 03699 HLH 13723 b1    AN ACT concerning State government. 2    Be it enacted by the People of the State of Illinois, 3represented in the General Assembly: 4    Section 1.
