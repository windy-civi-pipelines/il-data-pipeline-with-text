3everything after the enacting clause with the following:
 
4    "Section 1. Short title. This Act may be cited as the State 
5Agency Retainage Act.
 
6    Section 5. Definitions. As used in this Act:
7    "Retainage" means a portion of moneys withheld from the 
8payment of a contract for a specified period of time to ensure 
9that a contractor or subcontractor completes a construction 
10project in the manner specified in the contract. "Retainage" 
11does not include moneys withheld due to violations of local, 
12State, or federal laws.
13    "Satisfactory progress" means that a project is moving 
14forward according to the established schedule and budget and 
15that the work has been performed in accordance with industry 
16standards and without deficiencies.

1    "State agency" has the meaning given to "State agencies" 
2in Section 1-7 of the Illinois State Auditing Act.
 
3    Section 10. Determination of satisfactory progress. 
4Subject to the limitations set forth in Section 15, with 
5respect to a contract entered into on or after the effective 
6date of this Act, a State agency may withhold a portion of the 
7moneys from the payment of a contract as retainage if and only 
8if the State agency determines that satisfactory progress has 
9not been achieved by a contractor or subcontractor during any 
10period for which a payment is to be made. Retainage may not be 
11used as a substitute for good contract management, and the 
12State agency may not withhold funds without cause. 
13Determinations to retain and the specific amount to be 
14withheld must be made by the State agency on a case-by-case 
15basis. These determinations must be based on an assessment of 
16past performance and the likelihood that such performance will 
17continue.
 
18    Section 15. Limitations on the amount of retainage. The 
19amount of retainage withheld may not exceed 10% of the amount 
20of any and all draw amounts submitted and approved under the 
21terms of the contract until the contract is 50% completed, may 
22not exceed 5% of the amount of any and all draw amounts 
23submitted and approved under the terms of the contract for the 
24duration of the contract, and may be adjusted as the contract
