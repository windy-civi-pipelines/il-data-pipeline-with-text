Minimum funding requirements for 8ambulances, fire engines, and other vehicles used to provide 9emergency services. A municipality with a population of more 10than 500,000 must set aside 10% of the funds the municipality 11receives from private insurers as direct payment for ambulance 12or fire services provided by the municipality. Nothing in this Section 16applies to funds received through (i) public health programs, 17including, but not limited to, Medicaid and Medicare, or (ii) 18reimbursements for liability claims, settlements, or 19judgments, including payments from excess insurers or 20self-insurance reserves.
