These rules shall be 13consistent with requirements of relevant professional 14associations, specialty societies, or boards. The rules shall 15also address variances in part or in whole for good cause, 16including, but not limited to, temporary illness or hardship.   10400HB3850ham002- 2 -LRB104 09696 AAS 24251 a1The rules may also require that the educational requirements 2include simulation training or standardized patient training. 3In establishing these rules, the Department shall consider 4educational requirements for medical staffs, requirements for 5specialty society board certification or for continuing 6education requirements as a condition of membership in 7societies representing the 2 categories of licensee under this 8Act. These rules shall assure that licensees are given the 9opportunity to participate in those programs sponsored by or 10through their professional associations or hospitals which are 11relevant to their practice. Each licensee is responsible for 12maintaining records of completion of continuing education and 13shall be prepared to produce the records when requested by the 14Department. Any education or simulation training taken by a 15licensee that is in addition to the educational requirements 16in the rules adopted by the Department under this Section may 17be counted toward the licensee's continuing education 18requirements. 19(Source: P.A. 97-622, eff. 11-23-11.)".
