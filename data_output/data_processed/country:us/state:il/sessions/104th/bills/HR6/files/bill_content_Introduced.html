HOUSE RESOLUTION

 
2    WHEREAS, It is of the upmost importance for all entities 
3throughout the State of Illinois to express support for our 
4country and State by only flying the American flag and the 
5Illinois State flag; and
 
6    WHEREAS, The American flag and Illinois State flag are 
7representative of the diverse make up of this State and this 
8country, making flying any other flag unnecessary; and
 
9    WHEREAS, All people from different walks of life are 
10welcome to our State where people are free to be themselves to 
11the fullest, a freedom that our American flag represents best; 
12and
 
13    WHEREAS, In light of recent events in the world, there 
14have been public institutes throughout the United States and 
15Illinois that have taken it upon themselves to fly foreign 
16flags alongside or instead of the United States flag; this is a 
17disgrace to our country and to those who have made sacrifices 
18to keep this country the best in the world; and
 
19    WHEREAS, The United States has been a trailblazer for 
20freedom by demonstrating what is achievable in a country while 
21providing a blueprint for other nations to follow to provide
