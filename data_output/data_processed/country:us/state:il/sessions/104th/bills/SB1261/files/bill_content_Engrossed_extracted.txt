Community land trusts and other ownership 2    structures that promote long-term affordability are 3    critical housing options that can reduce the affordable 4    housing gap.5        (5) Community affordable for 9    generations.10        (6) Community land trusts and other ownership 11    structures that promote long-term affordability preserve 12    naturally occurring affordable housing by closing the 13    affordability gap so that low-to-moderate income 14    households can live in high opportunity neighborhoods.15        (7) The property tax sale system contributes to the 16    racial wealth gap in homeownership by transferring home 17    equity from communities of color to investors, threatening 18    community stability, and increasing housing costs. 19        (8)
