SENATE RESOLUTION

 
2    WHEREAS, The members of the Illinois Senate are saddened 
3to learn of the death of Shirley Mungai of Oak Park, who passed 
4away on October 4, 2024 at the age of 93; and
 
5    WHEREAS, Shirley Mungai was born in Pendleton, Indiana on 
6November 15, 1930 and moved to Oak Park in the 1940s; and
 
7    WHEREAS, Shirley Mungai attended St. Edmunds School and 
8later Oak Park River Forest High School; and
 
9    WHEREAS, Shirley Mungai married Joseph J. Mungai before 
10they moved to Elgin to start a family, and they had four 
11children together; and
 
12    WHEREAS, Shirley Mungai moved back to Oak Park, living at 
13the Oak Park Arms for six years and attending mass at St. 
14Edmunds or Ascension every Sunday, and she was a frequent 
15sight around Oak Park, enjoying long walks all over town; and
 
16    WHEREAS, Shirley Mungai loved children, and making them 
17happy was a source of her own joy; life at the Oak Park Arms 
18enabled her to see many preschoolers for whom she knitted 
19dolls and stuffed animals; and
