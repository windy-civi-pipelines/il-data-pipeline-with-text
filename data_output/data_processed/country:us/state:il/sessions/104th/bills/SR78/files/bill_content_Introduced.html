SENATE RESOLUTION

 
2    WHEREAS, The members of the Illinois Senate are saddened 
3to learn of the death of Paul Keller Boll, who passed away on 
4January 15, 2025; and
 
5    WHEREAS, Paul Boll, the son of Walter J. Boll and Margaret 
6(Keller) Boll, was born in Detroit, Michigan on May 10, 1940; 
7he graduated from Farmersville High School and Springfield 
8Junior College; he married Diane Seelbach in 1961; and
 
9    WHEREAS, Paul Boll served as the first male lay teacher at 
10Little Flower School before he began selling pharmaceuticals 
11throughout central Illinois; he then opened Paul Boll Painting 
12and Decorating in 1978, where he worked with his wife until he 
13retired in 2014; his business was credited for several works, 
14including the Governor's Mansion, the Old State Capitol Dome, 
15Memorial Medical Center, the Main Gate at the Illinois State 
16Fair, and Cooper's Hawk; and
 
17    WHEREAS, Paul Boll was a member of the Church of the Little 
18Flower for over 60 years, and he was active in the Little 
19Flower Men's Club; and
 
20    WHEREAS, Paul Boll was an avid Detroit Tigers and Notre 
21Dame Fighting Irish fan; he enjoyed reading, history,
