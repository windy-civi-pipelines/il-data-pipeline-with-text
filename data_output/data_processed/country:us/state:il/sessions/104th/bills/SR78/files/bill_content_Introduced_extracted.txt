(Roberta) Boll; and several cousins, 12nieces, friends, and other loved ones; therefore, be it 13    RESOLVED, BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTH GENERAL 14ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF ILLINOIS, that we mourn the passing of 15Paul Keller Boll and extend our sincere condolences to his 16family, friends, and all who knew and loved him; and be it 17further 18    RESOLVED, That a suitable copy of this resolution be 19presented to the family of Paul Boll as an expression of our 20deepest sympathy.
