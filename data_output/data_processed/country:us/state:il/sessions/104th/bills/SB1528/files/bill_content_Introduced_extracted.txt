Cristina Castro SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 65 ILCS 5/3.1-20-12 new    Amends the Illinois Municipal Code. In any municipality exceeding 100,000 inhabitants but not exceeding 1,000,000 inhabitants that adopts a ward system after the effective date of the amendatory Act, requires alderpersons to be elected by ward and allows for the election of 2 additional at-large alderpersons.
