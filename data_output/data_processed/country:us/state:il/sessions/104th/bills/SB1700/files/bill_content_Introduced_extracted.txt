Full Text of SB1700 ILGA.GOV SB1700 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026SB1700 Introduced 2/5/2025, by Sen. Bill Cunningham SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 55 ILCS 5/3-7008  from Ch. 34, par. 3-7008    Amends the Cook County Sheriff's Merit Board Division of the Counties Code. Provides that all deputy sheriffs shall be not less than 19 years of age at the time of their appointment (rather than 21 years of age or 20 years of age and have successfully completed 2 years of law enforcement studies at an accredited college or university).
