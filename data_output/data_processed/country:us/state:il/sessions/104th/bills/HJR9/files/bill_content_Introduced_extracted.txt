Full Text of HJR0009 ILGA.GOV HJR0009 - 104th General Assembly HJ0009LRB104 08742 MST 18796 r1HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 2    WHEREAS, It is highly fitting that the Illinois General 3Assembly pays honor and respect to those who have served our 4country and, in doing so, have made the ultimate sacrifice for 5our nation; and 6    WHEREAS, Sergeant First Class Theodore Alexander 7Katsoolias served in the U.S. Army as a member of 1st 8Battalion, 38th Infantry Regiment, 2nd Infantry Division; and 9    WHEREAS, On November 25, 1950, approximately 300,000 10Chinese Communist Forces launched a counteroffensive against 11U.N. forces after crossing the Yalu River; SFC Katsoolias' 12unit was positioned near the Chongchon River and soon came 13under intense attack; unable to halt the enemy advance and in 14danger of being surrounded, his unit pulled back and was 15forced to fight against enemy pursuit while also attempting to 16break through well-defended roadblocks; the withdrawal was not 17complete until early December and inflicted extremely heavy 18casualties on his unit; and 19    WHEREAS, SFC Katsoolias went missing at some point during 20the withdrawal to Kujang; he was never reported as a prisoner 21of war, and his remains have not been recovered; and   HJ0009- 2 -LRB104 08742 MST 18796 r1    WHEREAS, SFC Katsoolias is memorialized on the Courts of 2the Missing at the National Memorial Cemetery of the Pacific; 3therefore, be it 4    RESOLVED, BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE 5HUNDRED FOURTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF ILLINOIS, THE 6SENATE CONCURRING HEREIN, that we designate Route 59 in West 7Chicago from Route 38 to Route 64 as the "SFC Theodore A. 8Katsoolias Memorial Highway"; and be it further 9    RESOLVED, That the Illinois Department of Transportation 10is requested to erect, at suitable locations consistent with 11State and federal regulations, appropriate plaques or signs 12giving notice of the name "SFC Theodore A. Katsoolias Memorial 13Highway"; and be it further 14    RESOLVED, That suitable copies of this resolution be 15presented to the family of SFC Katsoolias, West Chicago Mayor 16Ruben Pineda, and the Secretary of the Illinois Department of 17Transportation.
