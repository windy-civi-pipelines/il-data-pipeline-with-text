Stroger 6Jr. Hospital of Cook County, replacing the old Cook County 7Hospital, in 2002; he retired from the county board in 2018; 8and 9    WHEREAS, Jerry Butler was preceded in death by his wife, 10Annette; and 11    WHEREAS, Jerry Butler is survived by his sons, Anthony and 12Randy; four grandchildren; and a great-grandchild; therefore, 13be it 14    RESOLVED, BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTH GENERAL 15ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF ILLINOIS, that we mourn the passing of 16Jerry "Iceman" Butler and extend our sincere condolences to 17his family, friends, and all who knew and loved him; and be it 18further 19    RESOLVED, That a suitable copy of this resolution be 20presented to the family of Jerry Butler as an expression of our 21deepest sympathy.
