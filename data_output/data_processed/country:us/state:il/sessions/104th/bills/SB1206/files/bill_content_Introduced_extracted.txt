Full Text of SB1206 ILGA.GOV SB1206 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026SB1206 Introduced 1/24/2025, by Sen. Jil Tracy SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 430 ILCS 67/5    Amends the Firearms Restraining Order Act. Provides that if the petitioner for a firearms restraining order against a respondent is a law enforcement officer, the petitioner shall be referred to as the law enforcement agency in which the officer is employed on all public indexes concerning the petition and not the individually named officer who filed the petition.
