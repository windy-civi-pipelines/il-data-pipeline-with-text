5    WHEREAS, Clarence Thomas was born to Viola (Wordlaw) and

11District 89, demonstrating his commitment to education and his

151975; his career spanned across five decades while he served 
16as an adviser on congressional, statewide, and county races 
17for various candidates running for the U.S. Senate, Governor, 
18State's Attorney, and county board seats; and
 
19    WHEREAS, Clarence Thomas was elected as a trustee of 
20Proviso Township in 2017, and he was reelected in 2021, 
21holding that position until his passing; and
