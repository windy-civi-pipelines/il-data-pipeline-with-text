Charles Meier SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 20 ILCS 1705/77 new    Amends the Mental Health and Developmental Disabilities Administrative Act. Choate Mental Health and Developmental Center at Anna to remain at the facility upon request of the resident or the resident's family or legal guardian if the resident maintains an Illinois home address in a county located within the geographically organized service region designated by the Department as Division of Mental Health Region 5, excluding the following counties: Madison, Bond, Fayette, Clay, Jasper, or Crawford.LRB104 03869 KTG 13893 b  A BILL FOR HB1135LRB104 03869 KTG 13893 b1    AN ACT concerning State government. 2    Be it enacted by the People of the State of Illinois, 3represented in the General Assembly: 4    Section 5.
