Jil Tracy SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 105 ILCS 5/10-20.24a new105 ILCS 5/34-18.88 new    Amends the School Code. Beginning with the 2025-2026 school year, requires a school board to allow a student who resides in the school district but attends a nonpublic school to participate in extracurricular athletic activities sponsored by the district without being required to enroll or complete coursework at a public school within the district if certain conditions are met.
