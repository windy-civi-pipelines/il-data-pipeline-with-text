Full Text of SB1413 ILGA.GOV SB1413 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026SB1413 Introduced 1/31/2025, by Sen. Linda Holmes SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 105 ILCS 5/24-3.10 new    Amends the School Code. Provides that any public school employee who is a member of a statewide association and is appointed to a State board, advisory council, committee, commission, or task force to represent the association in State work may spend up to 10 days during a school term representing the association in State work. Provides that no deduction of wages may be made for such absence.
