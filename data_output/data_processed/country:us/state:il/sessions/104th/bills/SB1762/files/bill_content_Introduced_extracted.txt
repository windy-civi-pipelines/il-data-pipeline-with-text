Full Text of SB1762 ILGA.GOV SB1762 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026SB1762 Introduced 2/5/2025, by Sen. Chris Balkema SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED:     Appropriates $10,000,000 from the General Revenue Fund to the Department of Commerce and Economic Opportunity for the purpose of awarding grants to units of local government for the demolition or repair of commercial buildings that are detrimental to public health or safety as a result of one or more of the following: dilapidation; obsolescence; deterioration; or the failure of the building to meet minimum building code standards.
