Ram Villivalam SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED:     Appropriates $4,000,000 to the State Board of Education for costs associated with the implementation of inclusive curriculum mandates under the School Code. Effective July 1, 2025.LRB104 12786 LNS 24083 b SB2643LRB104 12786 LNS 24083 b1    AN ACT concerning appropriations.  2    Be it enacted by the People of the State of Illinois, 3represented in the General Assembly: 4    Section 5. The amount of $4,000,000, or so much of that 5amount as may be necessary, is appropriated from the General 6Revenue Fund to the State Board of Education for costs 7associated with the implementation of inclusive curriculum 8mandates, including, but not limited to, Native American 9history, LGBTQ+ history, Muslim American history, Asian 10American history, Latina and Latino history, Black history, 11and Holocaust education under Article 27 of the School Code. 12    Section 99. Effective date. This Act takes effect July 1, 132025.
