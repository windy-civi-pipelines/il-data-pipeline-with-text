Full Text of HB3719 ILGA.GOV HB3719 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026HB3719 Introduced 2/18/2025, by Rep. La Shawn K. Ford SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 50 ILCS 750/85 new    Amends the Emergency Telephone System Act. Provides that a law enforcement agency that encrypts police scanner transmissions must provide, by license or otherwise, real-time access to those transmissions to broadcast stations, broadcasting stations, radio broadcast stations, and newspapers.
