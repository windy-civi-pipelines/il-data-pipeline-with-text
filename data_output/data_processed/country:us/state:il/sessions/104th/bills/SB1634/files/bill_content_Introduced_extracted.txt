Full Text of SB1634 ILGA.GOV SB1634 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026SB1634 Introduced 2/4/2025, by Sen. Julie A. Morrison SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 25 ILCS 170/10  from Ch. 63, par. 180    Amends the Lobbyist Registration Act. Provides that any person who is convicted of any felony relating to, arising out of, or in connection with any activity regulated by the Act is prohibited for a period of 5 years from the date of such conviction from lobbying as a registered lobbyist under the Act.
