Full Text of HB2589 ILGA.GOV HB2589 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026HB2589 Introduced 2/6/2025, by Rep. Kelly M. Cassidy SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 720 ILCS 635/2  from Ch. 38, par. 22-51    Amends the Hypodermic Syringes and Needles Act. Provides that, upon request, a pharmacist shall (rather than may) sell up to 100 sterile hypodermic syringes or needles to a person who is at least 18 years of age.
