Snell Memorial Bridge"; 20and be it further 21    RESOLVED, That suitable copies of this resolution be 22presented to the family of PFC Snell and the Secretary of the   SJ0007- 3 -LRB104 05677 LAW 15707 r1Illinois Department of Transportation.
