HOUSE RESOLUTION

 
2    WHEREAS, The United States has almost 2,000,000 of its 
3people in prison, which is more than any other developed 
4nation in the world; and
 
5    WHEREAS, The vast majority of the men and women in our 
6prisons, approximately 95%, will eventually return to their 
7communities; and
 
8    WHEREAS, Few of those who return to their communities are 
9prepared for their release and often face barriers 
10reintegrating into society, such as serious physical and 
11mental health problems, limited housing options, and 
12inadequate education or qualifications to hold a job; and
 
13    WHEREAS, One-third of all correction departments provide 
14no services to released offenders, and most departments do not 
15offer a transitional program, placing a heavy burden on 
16families and communities; and
 
17    WHEREAS, Two-thirds of persons released will be rearrested 
18for new crimes within the first three years after their 
19release; and
 
20    WHEREAS, Congressman Danny K. Davis sponsored the Second
