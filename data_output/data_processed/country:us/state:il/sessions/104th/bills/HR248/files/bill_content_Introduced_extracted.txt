Full Text of HR0248 ILGA.GOV HR0248 - 104th General Assembly HR0248LRB104 13156 LAW 25287 r1HOUSE RESOLUTION 2    WHEREAS, The United States has almost 2,000,000 of its 3people in prison, which is more than any other developed 4nation in the world; and 5    WHEREAS, The vast majority of the men and women in our 6prisons, approximately 95%, will eventually return to their 7communities; and 8    WHEREAS, Few of those who return to their communities are 9prepared for their release and often face barriers 10reintegrating into society, such as serious physical and 11mental health problems, limited housing options, and 12inadequate education or qualifications to hold a job; and 13    WHEREAS, One-third of all correction departments provide 14no services to released offenders, and most departments do not 15offer a transitional program, placing a heavy burden on 16families and communities; and 17    WHEREAS, Two-thirds of persons released will be rearrested 18for new crimes within the first three years after their 19release; and 20    WHEREAS, Congressman Danny K. Davis sponsored the Second   HR0248- 2 -LRB104 13156 LAW 25287 r1Chance Act of 2007 for the purposes of providing transitional 2assistance to ex-offenders in coping with the challenges of 3reentry, reducing recidivism, helping to reunite families and 4protect communities, and enhancing public safety; and 5    WHEREAS, The Second Chance Act of 2007 was signed into law 6on April 9, 2008; and 7    WHEREAS, The Second Chance Act of 2007 provides grants to 8state and local governments to promote the safe and successful 9reintegration of offenders into the community upon their 10release; therefore, be it 11    RESOLVED, BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE 12HUNDRED FOURTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF ILLINOIS, that 13we declare July 3, 2025 and July 3, 2026 as Illinois Citizens 14Reentry Day in the State of Illinois in recognition of 15Congressman Danny K. Davis' work in helping to provide 16ex-offenders with a second chance and opportunity to reenter 17society as productive citizens and to encourage continuing 18support of such citizens as they reenter their communities; 19and be it further 20    RESOLVED, That a suitable copy of this resolution be 21presented to Congressman Danny K. Davis with our esteem and 22respect.
