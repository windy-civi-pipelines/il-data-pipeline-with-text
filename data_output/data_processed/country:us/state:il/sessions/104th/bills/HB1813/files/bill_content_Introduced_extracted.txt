Full Text of HB1813 ILGA.GOV HB1813 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026HB1813 Introduced 1/28/2025, by Rep. Robert "Bob" Rita SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 65 ILCS 5/11-13-30 new    Amends the Control Over Building and Construction Article of the Illinois Municipal Code. Provides that a municipality may not prohibit the building or usage of accessory dwelling units in the municipality. Provides that a municipality may provide reasonable regulations relating to the size and location of accessory dwelling units similar to other accessory structures unless a regulation would have the effect of prohibiting accessory dwelling units. Limits home rule powers.
