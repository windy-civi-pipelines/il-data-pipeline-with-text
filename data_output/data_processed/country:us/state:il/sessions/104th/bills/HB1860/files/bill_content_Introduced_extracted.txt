Full Text of HB1860 ILGA.GOV HB1860 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026HB1860 Introduced 1/29/2025, by Rep. Abdelnasser Rashid SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 10 ILCS 5/29-21 new    Amends the Election Code. Provides that a person shall not distribute, or enter into an agreement with another person to distribute, materially deceptive media if: (1) the person knows the media falsely represents a depicted individual; (2) the distribution occurs within 90 days before an election; (3) the person intends the distribution to harm the reputation or electoral prospects of a candidate in an election or otherwise deceive voters; and (4) the person intends the distribution to change the voting behavior of electors in an election by deceiving the electors into incorrectly believing that the depicted individual in fact engaged in the speech or conduct depicted, and the distribution is reasonably likely to cause that result. Sets forth exceptions to the provision and penalties for violations of the provision.
