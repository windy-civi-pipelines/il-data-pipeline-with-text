SENATE RESOLUTION

 
2    WHEREAS, The members of the Illinois Senate are saddened 
3to learn of the death of Patricia Ann "Patty" Stremsterfer of 
4Pleasant Plains, who passed away on February 23, 2025; and
 
5    WHEREAS, Patty Stremsterfer was born in Springfield to 
6Harry and Ruth (Fetzer) Hartmann on January 23, 1932; she 
7graduated as valedictorian from Pleasant Plains High School in 
81950; she married Harold Stremsterfer in Pleasant Plains on 
9December 2, 1951; and
 
10    WHEREAS, Patty Stremsterfer worked for Franklin Life 
11Insurance until her marriage; her part-time and seasonal 
12employments included her parents's business, Hartmann's 
13Grocery Store, So-Fro Fabrics, and Richland Elevator, but her 
14full-time job was being a farmer's wife; and
 
15    WHEREAS, Patty Stremsterfer's community involvement was 
16her passion; she volunteered for over 40 years at Memorial 
17Medical Center, first with the gift cart and later as the 
18cardiac waiting room receptionist; twice a month she gave 
19manicures at the Walker Nursing Home in Virginia and enjoyed 
20the company of the residents; she shared her sewing knowledge 
21by teaching children at the local library; she also served as 
22an election judge; and
