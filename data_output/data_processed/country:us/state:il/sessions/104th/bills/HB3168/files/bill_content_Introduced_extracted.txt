Full Text of HB3168 ILGA.GOV HB3168 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026HB3168 Introduced 2/18/2025, by Rep. Jawaharial Williams SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: New Act    Creates the Retail Sale of Tianeptine Prohibition Act. Provides that beginning January 1, 2026, no person shall sell, offer to sell, or distribute in this State any product containing tianeptine. Provides that the prohibition does not apply to any product containing tianeptine that is lawfully dispensed or prescribed by a pharmacist or a health care professional. Provides that a violation is a business offense, punishable by a minimum fine of $1,500 for each violation. Defines "health care professional".
