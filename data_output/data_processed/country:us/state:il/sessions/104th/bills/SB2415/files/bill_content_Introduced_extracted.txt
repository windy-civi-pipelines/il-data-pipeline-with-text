Full Text of SB2415 ILGA.GOV SB2415 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026SB2415 Introduced 2/7/2025, by Sen. Julie A. Morrison SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 215 ILCS 5/143.13b new    Amends the Illinois Insurance Code. Provides that any policy of automobile insurance that carries comprehensive coverage may not exclude theft coverage for lack of evidence of forcible entry or for the insured leaving the vehicle unlocked or leaving a key or key fob in the vehicle. Provides that nothing in the provisions prohibits a company from investigating and denying a claim due to fraudulent acts. Effective January 1, 2026.LRB104 09593 BAB 19657 b  A BILL FOR SB2415LRB104 09593 BAB 19657 b1    AN ACT concerning regulation. 2    Be it enacted by the People of the State of Illinois, 3represented in the General Assembly: 4    Section 5.
