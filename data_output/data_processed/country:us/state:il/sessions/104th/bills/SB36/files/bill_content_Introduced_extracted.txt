Full Text of SB0036 ILGA.GOV SB0036 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026SB0036 Introduced 1/13/2025, by Sen. Sally J. Turner SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 20 ILCS 805/805-306 new    Amends the Department of Natural Resources (Conservation) Law of the Civil Administrative Code of Illinois. Provides that, notwithstanding any other provision of law or rule of the Department of Natural Resources to the contrary, a State authorized veterans' organization seeking to hold a memorial service at a State Memorial Site shall not pay a fee and shall not be required to obtain liability insurance if the permit to hold such service is approved by the Department of Natural Resources. Provides that the Department of Natural Resources shall be provided a list of State authorized veterans' organizations by the Department of Veterans' Affairs.
