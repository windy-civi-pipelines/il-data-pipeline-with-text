Full Text of SB1242 ILGA.GOV SB1242 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026SB1242 Introduced 1/24/2025, by Sen. Sally J. Turner SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 20 ILCS 405/405-316 new    Amends the Department of Central Management Services Law of the Civil Administrative Code of Illinois. Requires the Department, at least annually, to review State facilities that are no longer in service, determine the status of those facilities, and determine which of those facilities need to be demolished. Requires the Department of Central Management Services to annually submit a report to the Governor and General Assembly regarding estimated costs for the demolition and removal of State facilities no longer in service.
