and 5    WHEREAS, Dan Edmonds was born to Daniel Sr. and Donna 6(Brown) Edmonds on July 22, 1946; he graduated from Flora 7Township High School in 1964; after attending Southern 8Illinois University Carbondale, he joined the U.S. Air Force, 9serving his country during the Vietnam War for four years; and 10    WHEREAS, After his service, Dan Edmonds worked for the 11Veterans' Administration for 22 years until his retirement; 12and 13    WHEREAS, Dan Edmonds was a member of several groups, 14including the Coon Creek Ridge Riders, the Marion Elks Lodge, 15and the American Legion; and 16    WHEREAS, Dan Edmonds was an avid bowler, often hitting the 17lanes up to four times a week and belonging to several bowling 18leagues, and he was a fan of the Washington Nationals baseball 19team; his interests also included fishing, horses, and most 20importantly, his grandchildren; and   SR0089- 2 -LRB104 11907 LAW 21998 r1    WHEREAS, Dan Edmonds was preceded in death by his parents; 2his niece, Lindsey West; his nephew, Jeff Kingery; his bonus 3grandson, Dayne Harrison; and several aunts, uncles, and 4cousins; and 5    WHEREAS, Dan Edmonds is survived by his children, Angela 6(Mark Skivers) Edmonds, Felicia (Jason) Miller, and Anita 7Edmonds; his sister, Mary (James) West; his nephew, Rob West; 8two grandchildren; two bonus grandsons; one bonus 9granddaughter; two great-nephews; several cousins; and many 10special friends, including Judy Thomas; therefore, be it 11    RESOLVED, BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTH GENERAL 12ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF ILLINOIS, that we mourn the passing of 13Daniel Wilford "Dan" Edmonds Jr. and extend our sincere 14condolences to his family, friends, and all who knew and loved 15him; and be it further 16    RESOLVED, That a suitable copy of this resolution be 17presented to the family of Dan Edmonds as an expression of our 18deepest sympathy.
