HOUSE RESOLUTION

 
2    WHEREAS, The members of the Illinois House of 
3Representatives wish to congratulate The Peoples State Bank of 
4Newton on the occasion of its 150th anniversary; and
 
5    WHEREAS, The Peoples State Bank of Newton, the oldest 
6financial institution in Jasper County, was organized under 
7Illinois' state banking laws as People's Bank with a capital 
8stock of $100,000 on May 1, 1875; and
 
9    WHEREAS, People's Bank was converted to The Peoples State 
10Bank in July 1911, and it later prospered as one of the few 
11banks that weathered the Great Depression while continuing to 
12deliver uninterrupted service throughout; and
 
13    WHEREAS, After a period of considerable growth, The 
14Peoples State Bank purchased and expanded into an adjacent 
15former grocery store in 1947; and
 
16    WHEREAS, The Peoples State Bank added convenience to its 
17list of services with the addition of its first drive-in 
18facility at the rear of the main bank in 1961; and
 
19    WHEREAS, Due to its increasing size, The Peoples State 
20Bank purchased a building and lot from the Jasper Columbian
