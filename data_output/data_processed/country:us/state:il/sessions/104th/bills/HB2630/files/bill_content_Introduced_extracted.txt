Full Text of HB2630 ILGA.GOV HB2630 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026HB2630 Introduced 2/6/2025, by Rep. Jason R. Bunting SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: New Act    Creates the Solar Powered Road Signs Pilot Program Act. Requires the Department of Transportation to develop a pilot program to test the use of solar powered LED road signs in 5 counties of varied population. After getting reports of the program from the counties, the Department shall file a report with the General Assembly that contains the complete findings of the program.
