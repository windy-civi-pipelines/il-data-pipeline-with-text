Nabeela Syed SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 410 ILCS 513/20215 ILCS 5/224.5 new    Amends the Genetic Information Privacy Act. Provides that an insurer may not seek information derived from genetic testing for use in connection with a policy of life insurance. Provides that an insurer may consider the results of genetic testing in connection with a policy of life insurance if the individual voluntarily submits the results and the results are favorable to the individual. Amends the Illinois Insurance Code.
