he remained 19dedicated to his coaching duties despite any personal 20hardships he faced; and 21    WHEREAS, Chuck Acasili served as a mentor to many young 22men in the track and field and cross country programs at   HR0280- 2 -LRB104 13291 ECR 25528 r1Glenbrook South High School, offering them inspiration in 2overcoming their own personal struggles and helping them deal 3with some of the many issues teenagers face; and 10    WHEREAS, Chuck Acasili was the son of Virgilio and the 11late Aida Acasili, the brother of Charlene (Philip) Millan, 12and the uncle of Iris; therefore, be it 13    RESOLVED, BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE 14HUNDRED FOURTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF ILLINOIS, that 15we mourn the passing of Charles Daniel "Chuck" Acasili and 16extend our sincere condolences to his family, friends, and all 17who knew and loved him;
