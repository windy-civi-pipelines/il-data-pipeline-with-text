SENATE RESOLUTION

 
2    WHEREAS, The members of the Illinois Senate are saddened 
3to learn of the death of Margaret Ann Timoney of Springfield, 
4who passed away on March 8, 2025; and
 
5    WHEREAS, Margaret Timoney was born in Springfield to 
6Harold and Mary Madelyn Hannah Carter on March 2, 1936; she 
7attended St. Joseph Grade School, graduated from Ursuline 
8Academy in 1954, and attended Springfield College; she married 
9Lawrence "Larry" Timoney in Springfield on July 28, 1956; and
 
10    WHEREAS, Margaret Timoney retired from the City of 
11Springfield; and
 
12    WHEREAS, Margaret Timoney was a member of St. Joseph's 
13Church, and her faith was very important to her; she attended 
14daily mass and was active in her parish community; she was 
15always putting the needs of others before her own; she 
16actively volunteered at Ursuline Academy, St. Joseph School 
17and Church, Griffin High School, Sacred Heart Griffin High 
18School, St. John's Breadline, St. Joseph's Home, Daily Bread, 
19Meals on Wheels, and the Senior Citizens Center; and
 
20    WHEREAS, Margaret Timoney loved working with numbers; 
21after retiring, she took college accounting and tax prep
