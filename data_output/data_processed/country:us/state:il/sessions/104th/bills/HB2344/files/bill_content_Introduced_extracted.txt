Full Text of HB2344 ILGA.GOV HB2344 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026HB2344 Introduced 2/4/2025, by Rep. Laura Faver Dias SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 35 ILCS 200/9-25    Amends the Property Tax Code. Provides that, on and after January 1, 2026, in counties with a population of more than 700,000 residents and fewer than 725,000 residents, at the request of the supervisor of assessments, property record cards supplied by the townships shall be in a digital format compatible with the system used by the supervisor of assessments.
