Regan Deering SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 745 ILCS 75/1  from Ch. 70, par. 201745 ILCS 75/2  from Ch. 70, par. 202    Amends the Snow and Ice Removal Act. Includes within the protection of the Act a person in charge of any business who attempts to remove snow or ice from sidewalks and parking areas abutting the business. Prohibits liability unless the removal of the snow and ice was done in a willful or wanton manner.
