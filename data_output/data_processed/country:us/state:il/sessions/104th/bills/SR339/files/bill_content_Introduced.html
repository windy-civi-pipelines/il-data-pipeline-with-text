SENATE RESOLUTION

 
2    WHEREAS, The members of the Illinois Senate are saddened 
3to learn of the death of Francis J. "Bud" Daly Jr., who passed 
4away on January 28, 2025 at the age of 92; and
 
5    WHEREAS, Bud Daly was born to Joseph and Evelyn (Fagen) 
6Daly in Chicago on April 11, 1932; and
 
7    WHEREAS, Bud Daly attended St. Philip's High School, 
8opening an auto parts store with his friends, Tim and Ralph, 
9shortly after graduating; and
 
10    WHEREAS, Bud Daly married his wife, Mary, in 1960, and 
11together, they shared 52 years of marriage before Mary's 
12passing in 2012; and
 
13    WHEREAS, Bud Daly had a successful 30-year career in sales 
14when, at a crossroads in his personal and professional life, 
15he decided to visit the church to reflect; Fr. Lennon noticed 
16him in the pews and started a conversation that ultimately 
17changed Bud's direction and outlook; though he did not share 
18specific details, he spoke of it until his final days, and his 
19children would refer to him as "Fr. Bud" when they believed 
20advice was about to be delivered; and
