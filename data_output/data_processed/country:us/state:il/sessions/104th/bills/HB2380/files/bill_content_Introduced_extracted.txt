Lindsey LaPointe SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 415 ILCS 120/15    Amends the Electric Vehicle Rebate Act. Provides that, beginning July 1, 2026, the rules adopted under the Act shall cause the Agency to establish one rebate application cycle for each State fiscal year, shall cause each rebate application cycle to have a duration of one State fiscal year, and shall cause the Agency to accept rebate applications and issue rebates under the Act throughout each rebate application cycle.LRB104 08736 BDA 18790 b  A BILL FOR HB2380LRB104 08736 BDA 18790 b1    AN ACT concerning safety. 2    Be it enacted by the People of the State of Illinois, 3represented in the General Assembly: 4    Section 5.
