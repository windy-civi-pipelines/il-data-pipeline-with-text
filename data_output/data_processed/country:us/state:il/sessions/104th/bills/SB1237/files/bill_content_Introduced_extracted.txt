Karina Villa SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED:     Appropriates $9,000,000 from the General Revenue Fund to the Department of Public Health for continued funding to the Illinois Association of Free and Charitable Clinics. Effective July 1, 2025.LRB104 06478 HLH 16514 b SB1237LRB104 06478 HLH 16514 b1    AN ACT concerning appropriations.  2    Be it enacted by the People of the State of Illinois, 3represented in the General Assembly: 4    Section 5. The amount of $9,000,000 or so much thereof as 5may be necessary, is appropriated to the Department of Public 6Health from the General Revenue Fund for continued funding to 7the Illinois Association of Free and Charitable Clinics for 8grants and other expenses of free and charitable clinics to 9provide care or make referrals for uninsured or underinsured 10people living with acute and chronic health issues, including, 11but not limited to, medical health care, behavioral health 12care, oral health care, outreach, health education, screening 13and testing, medication and related expenses, disease 14prevention, and vaccinations. 15    Section 99.
