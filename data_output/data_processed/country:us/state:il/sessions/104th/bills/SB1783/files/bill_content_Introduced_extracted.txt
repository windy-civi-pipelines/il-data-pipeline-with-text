Full Text of SB1783 ILGA.GOV SB1783 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026SB1783 Introduced 2/5/2025, by Sen. Chris Balkema SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: New Act    Creates the Prohibition on Government Sponsored Sexual Performances. Provides that a unit of local government or a school district may not allow a live adult performance to be held on publicly owned or operated property under its control. Requires the State to stop distributing all funds to a unit of local government or school district that violates the provisions. Limits home rule powers.
