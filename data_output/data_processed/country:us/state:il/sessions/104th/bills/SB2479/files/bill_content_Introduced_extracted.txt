Full Text of SB2479 ILGA.GOV SB2479 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026SB2479 Introduced 2/7/2025, by Sen. Mattie Hunter SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 205 ILCS 670/17.5    Amends the Consumer Installment Loan Act. Provides that the certified database provider shall indemnify the licensee against all claims and actions arising from illegal or willful or wanton acts on the part of the certified database provider. The certified database provider may charge a fee not to exceed the lesser of $1 or 0.1% of the loan principal for each loan entered into the certified database. Prohibits the database provider from charging any additional fees or charges to the licensee.
