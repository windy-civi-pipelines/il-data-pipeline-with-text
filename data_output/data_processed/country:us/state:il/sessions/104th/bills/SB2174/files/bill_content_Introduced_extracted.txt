Full Text of SB2174 ILGA.GOV SB2174 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026SB2174 Introduced 2/7/2025, by Sen. Mike Porfirio SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 330 ILCS 56/10    Amends the Veterans Preference in Private Employment Act. Expands the definition of "veteran" to include a member of the United States Armed Forces Reserves who has never been deployed but separated under conditions other than dishonorable as noted on the individuals' service (rather than NGB-22) discharge form.
