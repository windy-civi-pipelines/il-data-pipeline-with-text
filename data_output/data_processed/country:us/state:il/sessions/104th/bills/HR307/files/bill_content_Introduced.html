HOUSE RESOLUTION

 
2    WHEREAS, The members of the Illinois House of 
3Representatives wish to congratulate Norman "Norm" Fanning on 
4the occasion of the Elwood Fire Protection District Training 
5Tower Facility being renamed in his honor; and
 
6    WHEREAS, Norm Fanning was born to James and Albertha 
7Fanning in Joliet on December 16, 1932; he attended Washington 
8Junior High School and Joliet Central High School; he worked 
9briefly at Goldblatt's Department Store before being drafted 
10into the U.S. Army in 1953, serving as a staff sergeant first 
11class during the Korean War until he was honorably discharged 
12in 1954; he and his family moved to Elwood in 1965; and
 
13    WHEREAS, Norm Fanning worked as a welder for Caterpillar, 
14Inc. in Joliet for 33 years, retiring in 1989; and
 
15    WHEREAS, Norm Fanning served as a trustee of the Elwood 
16Fire Protection District from May 1, 1987 until June 30, 2023, 
17dedicating 36 years of service to the fire district; during 
18his tenure, he served as president for a time; he also served 
19as a school bus driver for Elwood Community Schools, ensuring 
20the safety of countless children for over a decade; and
 
21    WHEREAS, Norm Fanning volunteered with the Memorial Squad
