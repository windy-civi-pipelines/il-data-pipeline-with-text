McCombie, Dan Ugaste, Brad Stephens, et al. SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: New Act    Creates the Revenue Estimate Act. Provides that the General Assembly shall not enact any bill to appropriate funds within any fiscal year prior to its adoption of a joint resolution reflecting the estimate of funds available for that fiscal year as required under the Commission on Government Forecasting and Accountability Act.
