Full Text of SB2012 ILGA.GOV SB2012 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026SB2012 Introduced 2/6/2025, by Sen. Steve Stadelman SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 20 ILCS 835/3b    If and only if Senate Bill 867 of the 103rd General Assembly becomes law, amends the State Parks Act. Provides that the land management agreement with the Prairie Band Potawatomi Nation and other necessary parties for the purpose of managing, maintaining, or operating the real property conveyed to the Prairie Band Potawatomi Nation shall contain restrictions on gaming operations.
