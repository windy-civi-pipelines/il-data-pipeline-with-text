Herreid Memorial Highway"; and be it further 14    RESOLVED, That the Illinois Department of Transportation 15is requested to erect, at suitable locations consistent with 16State and federal regulations, appropriate plaques or signs 17giving notice of the name "SSG Robert D. Herreid Memorial 18Highway"; and be it further 19    RESOLVED, That suitable copies of this resolution be 20presented to the family of SSG Herreid, Batavia Mayor Jeff 21Schielke, and the Secretary of the Illinois Department of 22Transportation.
