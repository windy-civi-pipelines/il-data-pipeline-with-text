"Bill" Wilkinson and extend our sincere condolences 15to his family, friends, and all who knew and loved him; and be 16it further 17    RESOLVED, That a suitable copy of this resolution be 18presented to the family of Bill Wilkinson as an expression of 19our deepest sympathy.
