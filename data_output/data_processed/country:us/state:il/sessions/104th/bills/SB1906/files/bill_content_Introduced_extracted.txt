Full Text of SB1906 ILGA.GOV SB1906 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026SB1906 Introduced 2/6/2025, by Sen. Mike Simmons SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: New Act    Creates the Prescription Drug Purchasing Task Force Act. Creates a Prescription Drug Purchasing Task Force in the Department of Healthcare and Family Services to study the different ways that other states are using multi-state prescription drug purchasing pools to increase the State's negotiating power and lower prices for patients. Provides for duties of the Task Force. Provides that the Department of Healthcare and Family Services shall provide administrative and other support to the Task Force. Provides that, on or before July 31, 2026, the Task Force shall report its findings and recommendations to the General Assembly and to the Governor. Provides that the Prescription Drug Purchasing Task Force is abolished and the Act is repealed on January 1, 2027.LRB104 09739 BDA 19805 b  A BILL FOR SB1906LRB104 09739 BDA 19805 b1    AN ACT concerning State government. 2    Be it enacted by the People of the State of Illinois, 3represented in the General Assembly: 4    Section 1.
