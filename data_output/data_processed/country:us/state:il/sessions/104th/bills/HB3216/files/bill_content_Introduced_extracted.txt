Wayne A Rosenthal SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 35 ILCS 5/246 new    Amends the Illinois Income Tax Act. Creates an income tax credit for qualified physicians who work in hospitals, health clinics, or independently and who work in underserved areas or in rural counties or rural municipalities. Provides that the credit shall be in the amount of $5,000.
