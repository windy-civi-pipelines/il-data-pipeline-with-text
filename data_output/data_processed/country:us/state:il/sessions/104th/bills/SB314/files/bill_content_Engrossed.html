7    Sec. 7.5. Contract requirements; lifetime memberships.
8    (a) As used in this Section, "lifetime membership" means a 
9membership advertised or represented by a physical fitness 
10center as a membership to that physical fitness center that 
11lasts for the duration of the life of the customer unless 
12cancelled by the customer.
13    (b) A physical fitness center that offers a lifetime 
14membership that includes provisions in the membership contract 
15that allow for increases in costs or fees or reductions in 
16benefits shall disclose those provisions clearly and 
17conspicuously in a bold, clearly readable font that is 
18consistent with the typeface used in the contract.
19    (c) If a physical fitness center that offers a lifetime 
20membership fails to clearly and conspicuously disclose that 
21the lifetime membership's costs or fees could increase or the 
22lifetime membership's benefits could be reduced, as described 
23in subsection (b), either during the contract's initial term
