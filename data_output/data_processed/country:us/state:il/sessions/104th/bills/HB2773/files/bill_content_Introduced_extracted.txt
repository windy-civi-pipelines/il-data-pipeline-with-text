Full Text of HB2773 ILGA.GOV HB2773 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026HB2773 Introduced 2/6/2025, by Rep. Barbara Hernandez SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 210 ILCS 45/3-711  from Ch. 111 1/2, par. 4153-711    Amends the Nursing Home Care Act. Provides that the Department of Public Health shall furnish the record of all specified proceedings to an interested party at $3 per page (instead of 70 cents) for each original transcript and 75 cents per page (instead of 25 cents) for each certified copy. Provides that the charge for any part of the transcript ordered and paid for previous to the writing of the original record shall be 75 cents (instead of 25 cents) per page.LRB104 08401 BAB 18452 b  A BILL FOR HB2773LRB104 08401 BAB 18452 b1    AN ACT concerning regulation. 2    Be it enacted by the People of the State of Illinois, 3represented in the General Assembly: 4    Section 5. The Nursing Home Care Act is amended by 5changing Section 3-711 as follows: 6    (210 ILCS 45/3-711)  (from Ch. 111 1/2, par. 4153-711)7    Sec. 3-711. The Department, at its expense, shall provide 8a stenographer to take the testimony, or otherwise record the 9testimony, and preserve a record of all proceedings under this 10Section. The notice of hearing, the complaint and all other 11documents in the nature of pleadings and written motions filed 12in the proceedings, the transcript of testimony, and the 13findings and decision shall be the record of the proceedings. 14The Department shall furnish a transcript of such record to 15any person interested in such hearing upon payment therefor of 16$3 70 cents per page for each original transcript and 75 25 17cents per page for each certified copy thereof.
