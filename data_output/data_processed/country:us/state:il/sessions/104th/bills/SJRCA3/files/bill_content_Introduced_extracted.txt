Full Text of SJRCA0003 ILGA.GOV SJRCA0003 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION CONSTITUTIONAL AMENDMENTSC0003 Introduced 2/4/2025, by Sen. Terri Bryant SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: ILCON Art. I, Sec. 20.1 new    Proposes to amend the Bill of Rights Article of the Illinois Constitution. Provides that the right of a parent to determine his or her child's education and health care shall not be infringed prior to the child's attainment of the age of majority, except where the child is in the care and custody of the State.
