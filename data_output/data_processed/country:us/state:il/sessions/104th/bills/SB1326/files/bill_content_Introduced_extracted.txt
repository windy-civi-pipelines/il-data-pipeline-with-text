Full Text of SB1326 ILGA.GOV SB1326 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026SB1326 Introduced 1/28/2025, by Sen. Julie A. Morrison SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 35 ILCS 200/16-1705 ILCS 100/5-45.62 new    Amends the Property Tax Code. Provides that, in counties with a population of more than 700,000 residents and fewer than 725,000 residents, upon motion of any party and provided that other parties to the appeal do not object, hearings upon appeal to the Property Tax Appeal Board shall be heard online with parties to the appeal participating in the hearing remotely. Requires the Property Tax Appeal Board to amend its rules of practice and procedure within 60 days after the effective date of the amendatory Act to accommodate the remote hearings. Amends the Illinois Administrative Procedure Act. Authorizes the Property Tax Appeal Board to adopt emergency rules to implement the amendatory Act.
