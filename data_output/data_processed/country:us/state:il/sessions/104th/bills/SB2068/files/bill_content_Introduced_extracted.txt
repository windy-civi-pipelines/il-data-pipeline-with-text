Full Text of SB2068 ILGA.GOV SB2068 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026SB2068 Introduced 2/6/2025, by Sen. Sally J. Turner SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 410 ILCS 705/45-20    Amends the Cannabis Regulation and Tax Act. Provides that, in addition to other grounds specified in this Act, the Department of Agriculture, upon notification by a county of a cultivation center's failure to pay all or part of any property tax or penalty due to the reporting county, shall refuse the issuance or renewal of a license, or suspend or revoke the license of the cultivation center.
