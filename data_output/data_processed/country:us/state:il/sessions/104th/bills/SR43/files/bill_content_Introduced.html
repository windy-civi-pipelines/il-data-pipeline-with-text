SENATE RESOLUTION

 
2    WHEREAS, The members of the Illinois Senate wish to 
3recognize Officer Anthony J. Rouba for his service in the U.S. 
4Marine Corps and as a Chicago police officer; and
 
5    WHEREAS, Officer Rouba was born in Chicago on January 27, 
61967; after growing up in the city, he graduated from high 
7school, married his wife, and welcomed his child in 1986; and
 
8    WHEREAS, Officer Rouba joined the U.S. Marine Corps, 
9trained in San Diego, and was stationed at Camp Pendleton, 
10California; he served with the 1st Marine Division, 3rd 
11Amphibious Assault Battalion, Charlie Company; he was deployed 
12overseas on naval vessels, including during Desert 
13Shield/Desert Storm in August 1990, extending his service by 
14one month; and
 
15    WHEREAS, Throughout Officer Rouba's career with the Marine 
16Corps, he received multiple awards and was meritoriously 
17promoted twice; and
 
18    WHEREAS, After being discharged, Officer Rouba began 
19training at the Chicago Police Academy in September 2022, 
20where he became a squad leader and then a Chicago police 
21officer; he joined the Gang Enforcement Unit and sustained
