Full Text of HB1364 ILGA.GOV HB1364 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026HB1364 Introduced 1/28/2025, by Rep. Lawrence "Larry" Walsh, Jr. SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 70 ILCS 508/55    Amends the Joliet Arsenal Development Authority Act. Provides that the Joliet Arsenal Development Authority shall be abolished upon the last to occur of the following: (1) expiration of the 35-year (rather than 30-year) period that begins on the effective date of the Act; or (2) one year after all revenue bonds, notes, and other evidences of indebtedness of the Authority have been fully paid and discharged or otherwise provided for.
