Full Text of HB1902 ILGA.GOV HB1902 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026HB1902 Introduced 1/29/2025, by Rep. Jennifer Sanalitro SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 225 ILCS 605/3.16 new    Amends the Animal Welfare Act. Provides that dog breeders must genetically test or have their dogs genetically tested for diseases that cause early death or physical impairments. Provides that if the dog breeder finds that a dog he or she breeds has a genetic defect or mutation that causes early death or physical impairments, the dog breeder must have that dog sterilized.
