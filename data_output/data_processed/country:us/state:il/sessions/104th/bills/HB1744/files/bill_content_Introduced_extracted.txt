Full Text of HB1744 ILGA.GOV HB1744 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026HB1744 Introduced 1/28/2025, by Rep. Joe C. Sosnowski SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 750 ILCS 5/203  from Ch. 40, par. 203750 ILCS 5/301  from Ch. 40, par. 301750 ILCS 5/302  from Ch. 40, par. 302750 ILCS 5/208 rep.    Amends the Illinois Marriage and Dissolution of Marriage Act. Requires proof that each party to the marriage has attained the age of 18 years in order to obtain a license to marry and a marriage certificate from the county clerk. Deletes language allowing proof that each party to the marriage will have attained the age of 18 years at the time the marriage license is effective or will have attained the age of 16 years and has either the consent to the marriage of both parents or his guardian or judicial approval. Makes corresponding changes. Repeals a provision regarding judicial approval of underage marriages.
