Full Text of HB0032 ILGA.GOV HB0032 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026HB0032 Introduced 1/9/2025, by Rep. Daniel Didech SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 60 ILCS 1/70-50    Amends the Township Code. Provides that, in the event of a vacancy in the office of township supervisor, the township trustee who has the longest term of continuous service as a township trustee shall be ex officio supervisor of general assistance in the township and shall administer the general assistance program in the township as provided in Articles VI, XI, and XII of the Illinois Public Aid Code during the pendency of the vacancy in the office of township supervisor.LRB104 05560 RTM 15590 b  A BILL FOR HB0032LRB104 05560 RTM 15590 b1    AN ACT concerning local government. 2    Be it enacted by the People of the State of Illinois, 3represented in the General Assembly: 4    Section 5.
