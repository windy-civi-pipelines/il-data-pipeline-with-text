Full Text of HB1273 ILGA.GOV HB1273 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026HB1273 Introduced 1/28/2025, by Rep. Maurice A. West, II SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 330 ILCS 45/1  from Ch. 23, par. 3081    Amends the Military Veterans Assistance Act. Defines "veteran" to mean any person who: (i) served on active duty in any branch of the United States Armed Forces and whose last discharge or release was under conditions other than dishonorable; (ii) served on active duty in any branch of the United States Armed Forces, including active duty solely for training purposes, and was either awarded a service-connected disability or died in such service under conditions other than dishonorable; (iii) served in the National Guard or as a reservist in any branch of the United States Armed Forces, including active duty solely for training purposes; or (iv) is determined to be a veteran according to the U.S. Department of Veterans Affairs; provided, that in any case, the service of such person was entered into or served in Illinois, or such person has resided in the State for at least one day, except for the purpose of determining the residential eligibility of a deceased veteran's dependents.LRB104 06365 KTG 16401 b  A BILL FOR HB1273LRB104 06365 KTG 16401 b1    AN ACT concerning service members. 2    Be it enacted by the People of the State of Illinois, 3represented in the General Assembly: 4    Section 5. The Military Veterans Assistance Act is amended 5by changing Section 1 as follows: 6    (330 ILCS 45/1)  (from Ch. 23, par. 3081)7    Sec. 1. Definitions. As used in this Act:8    "United States Armed Forces" means the United States Army, 9Navy, Marine Corps, Air Force, Space Force, and Coast Guard. 10    "Veteran" means any person who: (i) served on active duty 11in any branch of the United States Armed Forces and whose last 12discharge or release was under conditions other than 13dishonorable; (ii) served on active duty in any branch of the 14United States Armed Forces, including active duty solely for 15training purposes, and was either awarded a service-connected 16disability or died in such service under conditions other than 17dishonorable; (iii) served in the National Guard or as a 18reservist in any branch of the United States Armed Forces, 19including active duty solely for training purposes; or (iv) is 20determined to be a veteran according to the U.S. Department of 21Veterans Affairs; provided, that in any case, the service of 22such person qualified under items (i) through (iv) was entered 23into or served in Illinois, or such person has resided in the   HB1273- 2 -LRB104 06365 KTG 16401 b1State for at least one day, except for the purpose of 2determining the residential eligibility of a deceased 3veteran's dependents. 4    "Veteran service organization" means a post, ship, camp, 5chapter, or detachment of a congressionally chartered or state 6chartered organization that (i) is formed by and for veterans, 7(ii) has a paid membership of at least 15 individuals, and 8(iii) provides responsible aid, assistance, or services to the 9veteran community. 10    "Administrator of military veterans assistance" means the 11commanders of the various veteran service organizations, the 12superintendent of a County Veterans Assistance Commission, or 13other persons whose duty it is, under the existing statutes, 14to care for, relieve or maintain, wholly or in part, any person 15who may be entitled to such assistance under the statutes of 16the State of Illinois.
