Full Text of HB2508 ILGA.GOV HB2508 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026HB2508 Introduced 2/4/2025, by Rep. Anna Moeller SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 305 ILCS 5/5-65 new    Amends the Medical Assistance Article of the Illinois Public Aid Code. Provides that subject to federal approval, for dates of service on and after January 1, 2026, the Department of Healthcare and Family Services shall reimburse diagnostic testing facilities that provide long-term ambulatory electrocardiogram monitoring services at a rate not less than 80% of the Medicare Physician Fee Schedule rate for such services. Defines "long-term ambulatory electrocardiogram monitoring services".
