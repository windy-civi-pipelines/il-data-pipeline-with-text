Full Text of HB4046 ILGA.GOV HB4046 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026HB4046 Introduced , by Rep. William "Will" Davis SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: New Act    Creates the Emergency Digital Communication Pilot Permits Act. Allows the Department of Transportation to issue pilot permits for digital public safety communication devices on State highway rights-of-way. Provides that digital public safety communication devices shall be operated solely by a unit of local government, shall not display commercial advertising, and must be installed on municipally owned infrastructure with emergency override functionality. Requires the Department to adopt rules to implement the Act.
