2    WHEREAS, It is highly fitting that the Illinois General 
3Assembly pays honor and respect to the truly great individuals

6    WHEREAS, Daniel Lee Kick was born on January 26, 1948 and

9    WHEREAS, After graduating high school, Daniel Lee Kick

12    WHEREAS, PFC Kick served with the 1st Marine Division, 1st 
13Battalion, 5th Marines, H&S Company; and
 
14    WHEREAS, From February 13 through February 23, 1968, the 
151st Battalion, 5th Marines were engaged with heavily-armed and 
16well-entrenched North Vietnamese Army forces as part of the 
17Tet Offensive in the city of Hue, South Vietnam; and
 
18    WHEREAS, For six consecutive days during the fighting, PFC 
19Kick continuously risked his life by performing medevac and 
20resupply missions for his embattled unit in the face of small
