7    Sec. 22-101. Railroad safety messaging. Each school 
8district shall include in its student handbook for grades 
9kindergarten through 12 the following railroad safety 
10messaging:
11        (1) Cross tracks only at designated pedestrian or 
12    roadway crossings and observe and obey all warning signs 
13    and signals. 
14        (2) Never walk down a train track; it is illegal and 
15    dangerous. By the time a locomotive engineer sees a 
16    trespasser or vehicle on the tracks, it is too late to stop 
17    even when the emergency brakes are activated. 
18        (3) The only safe place to cross is at a designated 
19    public crossing, equipped with crossbucks, flashing red 
20    lights, or gates. Crossing anywhere else is trespassing, 
21    which can result in a ticket and fine. 
22        (4) Do not cross tracks immediately after a train 
23    passes. Additional trains may be approaching when blocked
