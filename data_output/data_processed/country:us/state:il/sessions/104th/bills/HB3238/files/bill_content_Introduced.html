SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED:
 
605 ILCS 5/6-207  from Ch. 121, par. 6-207
    Amends the Illinois Highway Code. Allows a highway commissioner's salary to be paid from the corporate road and bridge fund or the permanent road fund or both.
