and be it further 9    RESOLVED, That we encourage the IDFPR and the Illinois 10Board of Pharmacy to consider alternative approaches to 11ensuring pharmacists' understanding of state-specific laws and 12rules, including continuing education, attestation statements, 13or integration of jurisprudence content within the North 14American Pharmacist Licensure Examination (NAPLEX);
