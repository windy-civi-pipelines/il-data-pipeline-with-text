Full Text of HB3755 ILGA.GOV HB3755 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026HB3755 Introduced 2/18/2025, by Rep. Thaddeus Jones SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 215 ILCS 5/143.13b new    Amends the Illinois Insurance Code. Provides that any policy of automobile insurance that carries comprehensive coverage may not exclude theft coverage for lack of evidence of forcible entry or for the insured leaving the vehicle unlocked or leaving a key or key fob in the vehicle. Provides that nothing in the provisions prohibits a company from investigating and denying a claim due to fraudulent acts.
