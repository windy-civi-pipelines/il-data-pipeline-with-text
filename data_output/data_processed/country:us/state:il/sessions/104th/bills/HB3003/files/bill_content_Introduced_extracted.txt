Full Text of HB3003 ILGA.GOV HB3003 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026HB3003 Introduced 2/6/2025, by Rep. Norine K. Hammond SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 50 ILCS 105/1  from Ch. 102, par. 1    Amends the Public Officer Prohibited Activities Act. In provisions concerning offices a member of a county board may hold during the member's term of office, provides that a member of the county board may serve as a member of an intergovernmental joint self-insurance pool board during the county board member's term of office.
