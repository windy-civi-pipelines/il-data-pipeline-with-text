Full Text of HB2538 ILGA.GOV HB2538 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026HB2538 Introduced 2/4/2025, by Rep. Maurice A. West, II SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 20 ILCS 1705/67.5 new    Amends the Mental Health and Developmental Disabilities Administrative Act. Provides that within 6 months of learning that an adult with a developmental disability has lost a parental guardian while living with that parental guardian or independently, the Department of Human Services shall request a wellness check from the designated adult protective services agency or provider agency as defined in the Adult Protective Services Act to: (1) verify that the adult with a developmental disability is not experiencing or at imminent risk of abuse, neglect, exploitation, or self-neglect; and (2) establish mandatory standards for the provision of emergent casework and follow-up services to mitigate the risk of harm or death to the adult with a developmental disability.LRB104 10205 KTG 20279 b  A BILL FOR HB2538LRB104 10205 KTG 20279 b1    AN ACT concerning State government. 2    Be it enacted by the People of the State of Illinois, 3represented in the General Assembly: 4    Section 5.
