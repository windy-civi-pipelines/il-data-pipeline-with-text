Ram Villivalam SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 70 ILCS 5/6  from Ch. 15 1/2, par. 68.6    Amends the Airport Authorities Act. Provides that, in airport authorities having a population of not more than 500,000, the compensation for services of any commissioner shall not exceed $300 (rather than $150) in any one month for services within the corporate limits of the authority or within a distance of 50 miles from the corporate limits of the authority.
