7    Sec. 10-100. Release of aquatic life. 
8    (a) It shall be unlawful to release any aquatic life into 
9the the wild in this State without first securing permission 
10of the Department to do so, except that the owner of a body of 
11water may release aquatic life indigenous to the State of 
12Illinois into waters wholly upon his or her property. The 
13Department shall have the authority to promulgate necessary 
14rules and regulations, under the Illinois Administrative 
15Procedure Act, regulating the possession, transportation, and 
16shipping of aquatic life not indigenous to the State of 
17Illinois. All aquatic life may be immediately returned 
18unharmed from where they were taken. A violation of this 
19subsection (a) is a Class B misdemeanor.
