Full Text of HB1748 ILGA.GOV HB1748 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026HB1748 Introduced 1/28/2025, by Rep. Joe C. Sosnowski SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 25 ILCS 10/19 new    Amends the General Assembly Operations Act. Provides that all bills introduced in the General Assembly must remain in the chamber of origin for at least 5 calendar days before they can be voted upon on third reading and passed out of the chamber. Provides that, upon passage to the other chamber of the General Assembly, the 5 calendar day requirement is in effect and begins with the day of the bill's arrival in the new chamber. Provides that if a bill is amended in a chamber, the bill must remain in the chamber for an additional 5 calendar days starting with the day the amendment is filed and adopted.LRB104 03564 SPS 13588 b  A BILL FOR HB1748LRB104 03564 SPS 13588 b1    AN ACT concerning State government. 2    Be it enacted by the People of the State of Illinois, 3represented in the General Assembly: 4    Section 5.
