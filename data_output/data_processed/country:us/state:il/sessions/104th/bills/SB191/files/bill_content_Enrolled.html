7    Sec. 12-826. Seat safety belts required. 
8    (a) Beginning July 1, 2031, each designated seating 
9position in any newly purchased school bus shall be equipped 
10with a combination pelvic and upper torso seat safety belt 
11system (also known as a 3-point seat safety belt, Type 2 seat 
12safety belt, or lap and shoulder restraint system) in good 
13operating condition and meeting all applicable federal 
14standards.
15    (b) Nothing in this Section requires a public or nonpublic 
16school, school district, transportation provider or 
17contractor, or other entity operating a school bus, including 
18a bus driver, bus aide, volunteer, or other staff, to ensure 
19that the seat safety belt or other restraint system is 
20correctly adjusted and fastened or to give instruction on how 
21to correctly adjust or fasten the seat safety belt or other 
22restraint system. 
23    (c) This Section does not apply to a school bus that is
