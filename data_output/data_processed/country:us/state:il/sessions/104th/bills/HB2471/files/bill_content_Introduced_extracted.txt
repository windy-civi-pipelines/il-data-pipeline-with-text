Paul Jacobs SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 5 ILCS 430/5-10.5    Amends the State Officials and Employees Ethics Act. Provides that any person who has successfully completed the training required under this Act is considered to have successfully completed similar training requirements for harassment and discrimination prevention that may be required for employment in Illinois or to receive a license from the State.
