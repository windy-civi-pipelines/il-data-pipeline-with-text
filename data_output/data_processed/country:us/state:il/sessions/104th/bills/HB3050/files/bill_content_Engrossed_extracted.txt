The Swimming Facility Act is amended by 5changing Section 5 as follows: 6    (210 ILCS 125/5)  (from Ch. 111 1/2, par. 1205)7    Sec. 5. Permit for construction or major alteration. No 8swimming facility shall be constructed or altered in a major 9manner until plans, specifications, and other information 10relative to such swimming facility and appurtenant facilities 11as may be requested on forms provided by the Department are 12submitted to and reviewed by the Department and found to 13comply with minimum sanitary and safety requirements and 14design criteria, and until a permit for the construction or 15major alteration is issued by the Department. Permits are 16valid for a period of 2 years one year from date of issue. They 17may be reissued upon application to the Department and payment 18of the permit fee. 19    The fee to be paid by an applicant for a permit for 20construction, major alteration, or installation of each 21swimming facility shall be in accordance with Sections 8.1, 228.2, and 8.3 of this Act and shall accompany such application. 23(Source: P.A. 96-1081, eff. 7-16-10; 97-957, eff. 1-1-13.)
