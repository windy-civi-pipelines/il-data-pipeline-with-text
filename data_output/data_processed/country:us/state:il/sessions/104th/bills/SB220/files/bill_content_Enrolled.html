12    Sec. 15. Employee benefits protection. 
13    (a) Any employee who exercises the right to family 
14military leave or funeral honors detail leave under this Act, 
15upon expiration of the leave, shall be entitled to be restored 
16by the employer to the position held by the employee when the 
17leave commenced or to a position with equivalent seniority 
18status, employee benefits, pay and other terms and conditions 
19of employment. This Section does not apply if the employer 
20proves that the employee was not restored as provided in this 
21Section because of conditions unrelated to the employee's 
22exercise of rights under this Act.

24detail leave taken under this Act, the employer shall make it

6    Sec. 20. Effect on existing employee benefits. 
7    (a) Taking family military any leave under this Act shall 
8not result in the loss of any employee benefit accrued before 
9the date on which the leave commenced. 
10    (b) Nothing in this Act shall be construed to affect an 
11employer's obligation to comply with any collective bargaining 
12agreement or employee benefit plan that provides greater leave 
13rights to employees than the rights provided under this Act.

15Act shall not be diminished by any collective bargaining 
16agreement or employee benefit plan.

18diminish the contract rights or seniority status of any other 
19employee of any employer covered under this Act.
