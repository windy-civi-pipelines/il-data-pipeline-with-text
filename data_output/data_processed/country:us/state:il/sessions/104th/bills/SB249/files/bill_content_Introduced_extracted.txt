Full Text of SB0249 ILGA.GOV SB0249 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026SB0249 Introduced 1/22/2025, by Sen. Doris Turner SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 5 ILCS 490/161 new    Amends the State Commemorative Dates Act. Provides that the fourth day of August of each year shall be designated as Eva Carroll Monroe Recognition Day, to be observed throughout the State as a day to honor and celebrate Eva Carroll Monroe's legacy and her work on behalf of orphaned Black children during the harsh times of the Jim Crow era.
