Full Text of HB1874 ILGA.GOV HB1874 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026HB1874 Introduced 1/29/2025, by Rep. Janet Yang Rohr SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 35 ILCS 200/15-87 new    Amends the Property Tax Code. Provides that certain property on which a community-integrated living arrangement is located is entitled to a reduction in its equalized assessed value in an amount equal to the product that results when the number of occupants who use the community-integrated living arrangement as a primary residence is multiplied by $2,000.
