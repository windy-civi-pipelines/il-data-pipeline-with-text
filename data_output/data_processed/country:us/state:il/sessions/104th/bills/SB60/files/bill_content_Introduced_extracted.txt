Full Text of SB0060 ILGA.GOV SB0060 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026SB0060 Introduced 1/13/2025, by Sen. Jil Tracy SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 10 ILCS 5/19-25 new    Amends the Election Code. Requires the State Board of Elections, in conjunction with election authorities, to implement a secure, free access system available to voters by January 1, 2026. Provides that the system shall allow a voter to track receipt of the voter's vote by mail ballot and check the ballot's status with the election authority. Requires the election authority to make the system available to vote by mail voters from the first day that vote by mail ballots are accepted until 30 days after completion of the official canvass. The Election Code is amended by adding Section 519-25 as follows: 6    (10 ILCS 5/19-25 new)7    Sec. 19-25. Vote by mail tracking. In conjunction with 8election authorities, the State Board of Elections shall 9implement by January 1, 2026 a secure, free access system 10available to voters in each election authority that allows 11each vote by mail voter to:12        (1) track the delivery and receipt of the voter's vote 13    by mail ballot by the election authority; and14        (2) learn if the voter's vote by mail ballot was 15    accepted for counting and, if not, the reason why the 16    ballot was rejected.17    For each election, the election authority shall make the 18secure, free access system available to vote by mail voters 19from the first day that vote by mail ballots are accepted until 2030 days after completion of the official canvass. 21    Section 99.
