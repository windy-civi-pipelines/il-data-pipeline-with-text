Full Text of SB1742 ILGA.GOV SB1742 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026SB1742 Introduced 2/5/2025, by Sen. Mike Porfirio SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: New Act    Creates the Rooftop Safety for First Responders Act. Requires the installation of a parapet, extended masonry, or guard for low-sloped roofs. Sets forth requirements for skylights and other openings located in the plane of a low-sloped roof. Requires municipalities to complete a survey of buildings in their jurisdiction. Requires sharing the results of the surveys with local police and fire departments.
