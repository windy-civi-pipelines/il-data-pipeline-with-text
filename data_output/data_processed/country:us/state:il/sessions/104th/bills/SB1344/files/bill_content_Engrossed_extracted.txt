Full Text of SB1344 ILGA.GOV SB1344 - 104th General Assembly SB1344 EngrossedLRB104 08238 SPS 18288 b1    AN ACT concerning employment. 2    Be it enacted by the People of the State of Illinois, 3represented in the General Assembly: 4    Section 5. The Workers' Compensation Act is amended by 5changing Section 19.1 as follows: 6    (820 ILCS 305/19.1)  (from Ch. 48, par. 138.19a)7    Sec. 19.1. The time within which any act is required to be 8performed under any of the provisions of this Act shall be 9computed by excluding the first day and including the last, 10unless the last day is Saturday, Sunday or is a holiday as 11defined or fixed in any statute now or hereafter in force in 12this State, and then such days shall also be excluded. If the 13day succeeding such holiday is also a holiday then such 14succeeding day shall also be excluded. The time within which 15any act is required to be performed under any of the provisions 16of this Act shall be the same for this State and its political 17subdivisions as it is for private employers.18(Source: Laws 1959, p. 1733.)
