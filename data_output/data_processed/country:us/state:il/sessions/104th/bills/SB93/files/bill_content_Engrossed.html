3    Sec. 13.5. Prohibited food additives.
4    (a) Beginning January 1, 2027, a person or entity shall 
5not manufacture a food product for human consumption that 
6contains any of the following substances:
7        (1) Potassium bromate (Chemical Abstract Services 
8    number 7758-01-2); or 
9        (2) Propylparaben (Chemical Abstract Services number 
10    94-13-3). 
11    (b) Beginning January 1, 2028, a person or entity shall 
12not sell, deliver, distribute, hold, or offer for sale a food 
13product for human consumption that contains any of the 
14substances listed in paragraphs (1) through (4) of subsection 
15(a).
16    (c) A person or entity that violates subsection (a) or (b) 
17shall be liable for a civil penalty not to exceed $5,000 for a 
18first violation and not to exceed $10,000 for each subsequent 
19violation. A violation of this Section may be enforced by the 
20Attorney General or the State's Attorney of the county in 
21which the violation occurred.
