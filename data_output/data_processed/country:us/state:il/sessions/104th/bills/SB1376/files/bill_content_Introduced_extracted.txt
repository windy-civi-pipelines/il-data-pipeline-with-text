Full Text of SB1376 ILGA.GOV SB1376 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026SB1376 Introduced 1/29/2025, by Sen. Chapin Rose SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: New Act    Creates the Historical Cost of Attendance Disclosure Act. Requires each public or private college or university to post its cost of attendance on its website for each of the 10 academic years immediately preceding the effective date of the Act and for every academic year thereafter. Requires the posted cost of attendance to delineate which expenses are included in the Internal Revenue Service's Form 1098-T and which expenses are not included in Form 1098-T.LRB104 03574 LNS 13598 b  A BILL FOR SB1376LRB104 03574 LNS 13598 b1    AN ACT concerning education. 2    Be it enacted by the People of the State of Illinois, 3represented in the General Assembly: 4    Section 1.
