2    WHEREAS, The members of the Illinois Senate are saddened 
3to learn of the death of Larry N. Varner of Kewanee, formerly

5    WHEREAS, Larry Varner was born to D. Steve and Avis 
6(Beede) Varner in Fort Knox, Kentucky on September 2, 1944; he 
7graduated from Monticello High School in Monticello, Illinois 
8in 1962; he earned his Bachelor of Science in English 
9Education from Illinois State University in 1965; he married 
10Patty Carper at the Morton Methodist Church in Morton on March 
1122, 1969; he received both a Master of Science and an Advanced 
12Certificate in Counseling from the University of Illinois 
13Urbana-Champaign in 1970 and 1971, respectively; and
 
14    WHEREAS, Larry Varner taught English at Pekin High School 
15during the 1965-1966 school year and the 1968-1969 school 
16year, between which he served in the United States Army from 
171966 to 1968; he began his counseling career at Galva High 
18School in the fall of 1970, and he retired from Galva 
19Junior-Senior High School in the spring of 1999; he served as 
20coach of the Galva High School Scholastic Team from 1973 
21through 2008, and he was inducted into the Illinois High 
22School Scholastic Coaches Hall of Fame; and
