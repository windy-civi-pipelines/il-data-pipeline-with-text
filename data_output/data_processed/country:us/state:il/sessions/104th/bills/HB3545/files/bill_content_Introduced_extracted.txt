Bradley Fritts SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 20 ILCS 2705/2705-627 new    Amends Department of Transportation Law of the Civil Administrative Code of Illinois. Requires the Department of Transportation to create a 5-year pilot program to reimburse farmers to leave a row of corn as snow fences. Requires the Department to establish rules and procedures.
