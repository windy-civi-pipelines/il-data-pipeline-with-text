HOUSE RESOLUTION

 
2    WHEREAS, The members of the Illinois House of 
3Representatives wish to congratulate American Legion Auxiliary 
4Unit 392 on the occasion of its centennial anniversary; and
 
5    WHEREAS, The American Legion was charted by the United 
6States Congress as a patriotic veterans organization in 1919, 
7and it has flourished into a group of over one million members, 
8including 12,000 posts worldwide; and
 
9    WHEREAS, On September, 11, 1925, American Legion Auxiliary 
10Unit 392 was formed in Peotone to support the aims and goals of 
11the organization; and
 
12    WHEREAS, American Legion Auxiliary Unit 392 continues to 
13bolster service members, its community, and the country 
14through unwavering volunteerism, with members donating 3,259 
15hours in 2024 alone; and
 
16    WHEREAS, American Legion Auxiliary Unit 392 holds a Poppy 
17Drive every Saturday prior to Memorial Day, honoring those who 
18gave the ultimate sacrifice, in addition to collecting 
19donations for veterans, active-duty members, and their 
20families; and
