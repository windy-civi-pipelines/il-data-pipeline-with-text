and 15    WHEREAS, Keywan Glenn loved barbecuing and riding 16Harley-Davidson motorcycles; and 17    WHEREAS, Keywan Glenn, better known as "China Man", was a 18devoted and loving father with a big heart who shared his love 19and life lessons with everyone; the 4father of Kayla Glenn, Savannah Glenn, Kailani Glenn, Amara 5Shaw-Glenn, and Keywan Lontee Glenn Jr.; the father figure of 6Chloe Shaw, Antwaushea Sago, Gerald Smiter, and Zaria Blaxton; 7the brother of Talia White, Samora Brawner, Shauniqua Brawner, 8and Darrien White; the grandson of Virginia Glenn; the 9great-nephew of Betty; the nephew of Brenda Glenn and Darryl 10Glenn; and a relative and friend to many; therefore, be it 11    RESOLVED, BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTH GENERAL 12ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF ILLINOIS, that we mourn the passing of 13Keywan Cordell Glenn Sr. and extend our sincere condolences to 14his family, friends, and all who knew and loved him;
