Full Text of SB1243 ILGA.GOV SB1243 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026SB1243 Introduced 1/24/2025, by Sen. Andrew S. Chesney SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 25 ILCS 5/3.3 new    Amends the General Assembly Organization Act. Provides that neither house of the General Assembly shall consider or take a vote on any legislation between the hours of 12:00 a.m. and 6:00 a.m., on any day of the week during any regular or special session of the General Assembly, unless approved by the leaders of each of the caucuses of that house.
