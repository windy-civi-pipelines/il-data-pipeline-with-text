Terri Bryant SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 730 ILCS 5/3-2.5-15    Amends the Unified Code of Corrections. Provides that, notwithstanding other provisions of law to the contrary, any person serving as a guard of youth at a Department of Juvenile Justice Youth Center must: (1) be over the age of 21 and (2) have a high school diploma or equivalent.
