6    (815 ILCS 550/10)
7    Sec. 10. Unenforceable agreements.
8    (a) A provision in an agreement between an individual and 
9any other person for the performance of personal or 
10professional services is contrary to public policy and is 
11deemed unenforceable only as it relates to a new performance, 
12fixed on or after January 1, 2026, by a digital replica of an 
13individual if the provision meets all of the following 
14conditions:
15        (1) the provision allows for the creation and use of a 
16    digital replica of the individual's voice or likeness in 
17    place of work the individual would otherwise have 
18    performed in person;
19        (2) the provision does not include a reasonably 
20    specific description of the intended uses of the digital 
21    replica; and
22        (3) the individual was not either:
23            (A) represented by legal counsel who negotiated on
