Full Text of HR0428 ILGA.GOV HR0428 - 104th General Assembly HR0428LRB104 14476 ECR 27615 r1HOUSE RESOLUTION 2    WHEREAS, The members of the Illinois House of 3Representatives wish to express their support for the 4Immigration and Customs Enforcement (ICE), an agency within 5the United States Department of Homeland Security; and 6    WHEREAS, ICE performs a vital role in the enforcement of 7federal immigration laws and the safeguarding of national 8security; and 9    WHEREAS, ICE plays a critical part in protecting public 10safety by apprehending individuals who are in the United 11States or its territories in violation of federal immigration 12statutes, including those with criminal convictions under 13state or federal law; and 14    WHEREAS, Effective cooperation and collaboration among 15federal, state, and local law enforcement is essential to 16uphold the rule of law, prevent crime, and maintain security 17in communities throughout Illinois; and 18    WHEREAS, Illinois state and local law enforcement agencies 19are prohibited from assisting ICE in their enforcement efforts 20due to the Illinois TRUST Act, thereby placing the safety of 21ICE officers in jeopardy; and   HR0428- 2 -LRB104 14476 ECR 27615 r1    WHEREAS, ICE operates detention and removal programs in 2Illinois that support enforcement and public safety 3objectives; and 4    WHEREAS, ICE officers have been publicly identified online 5through the publishing of such information as their names, 6addresses, and job titles with the intent of bringing harm to 7them and their families; and 8    WHEREAS, ICE officers are facing a 500% increase in 9assaults against them while carrying out enforcement 10operations in addition to violent retaliation outside of the 11workplace by citizens who oppose stronger enforcement of 12federal law and have become enabled by this released 13information; and 14    WHEREAS, ICE officers wear masks, sunglasses, and other 15face coverings to protect themselves and their families from 16harassment online and in person; and 17    WHEREAS, It is important to express support for the 18protection of our first responders while they are on or off 19duty and are upholding public safety in the State; therefore, 20be it   HR0428- 3 -LRB104 14476 ECR 27615 r1    RESOLVED, BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE 2HUNDRED FOURTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF ILLINOIS, that 3we express our support to Immigration and Customs Enforcement 4(ICE), an agency within the United States Department of 5Homeland Security, for its efforts in enforcing federal 6immigration law, and we recognize ICE's role in cooperating 7with Illinois law enforcement to apprehend individuals who 8pose threats to public safety; and be it further 9    RESOLVED, That suitable copies of this resolution be 10delivered to all members of the Illinois Congressional 11Delegation and the Acting Director of ICE.
