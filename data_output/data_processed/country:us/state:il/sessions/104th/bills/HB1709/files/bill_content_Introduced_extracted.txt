Full Text of HB1709 ILGA.GOV HB1709 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026HB1709 Introduced 1/28/2025, by Rep. Kam Buckner SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: New Act    Creates the Local Accessory Dwelling Unit Act. Defines terms. Provides that a unit of local government may not prohibit the building or usage of accessory dwelling units in the unit of local government. Provides that a unit of local government may provide reasonable regulations relating to the size and location of accessory dwelling units similar to other accessory structures unless a regulation would have the effect of prohibiting accessory dwelling units. Limits home rule powers.
