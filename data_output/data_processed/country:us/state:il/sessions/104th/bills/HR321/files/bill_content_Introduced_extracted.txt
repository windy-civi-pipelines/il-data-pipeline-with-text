Nebo Baptist 5Church, the Jericho #120 Masonic Lodge, Tyree Consistory #64, 6and the Madison branch of the NAACP; therefore, be it 7    RESOLVED, BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE 8HUNDRED FOURTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF ILLINOIS, that 9we congratulate Mayor Tyrone Echols of the City of Venice on 10his retirement, and we wish him all the best in his future 11endeavors; and be it further 12    RESOLVED, That a suitable copy of this resolution be 13presented to Mayor Echols as a symbol of our esteem and 14respect.
