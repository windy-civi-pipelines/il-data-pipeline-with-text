Full Text of SR0115 ILGA.GOV SR0115 - 104th General Assembly SR0115LRB104 07286 LAW 17324 r1SENATE RESOLUTION 2    WHEREAS, Certified Registered Nurse Anesthetists (CRNAs), 3also known as nurse anesthesiologists or nurse anesthetists, 4are advanced practice registered nurses who are among the 5nation's most trusted professions, having provided anesthesia 6care to patients in the United States for more than 150 years; 7and 8    WHEREAS, CRNAs became the first nursing specialty to be 9accorded direct reimbursement rights by Medicare due to 10legislation passed in 1986; and 11    WHEREAS, CRNAs are the primary anesthesia providers in 12rural and underserved communities and continue to be the 13primary providers of anesthesia care to U.S. military 14personnel; and 15    WHEREAS, CRNAs practice in every setting in which 16anesthesia is delivered, including traditional hospital 17surgical suites, obstetrical delivery rooms, critical access 18hospitals, ambulatory surgical centers, and the offices of 19dentists, podiatrists, ophthalmologists, and pain management 20specialists; and 21    WHEREAS, CRNAs are qualified to make independent judgments   SR0115- 2 -LRB104 07286 LAW 17324 r1regarding all aspects of anesthesia care based on their 2education, training, board certification, and licensure; and 3    WHEREAS, CRNAs are trusted anesthesia experts, caring for 4patients safely and compassionately, delivering specialized, 5cost-effective care to all patients, from newborns to seniors, 6for every type of procedure in all types of facilities; and 7    WHEREAS, CRNAs' experience in critical care nursing, in 8addition to anesthesia care, provides the training and 9education to address unique healthcare challenges of some of 10our sickest patients, including veterans; and 11    WHEREAS, National CRNA Week will be held from January 19 12through January 25, 2025; therefore, be it 13    RESOLVED, BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTH GENERAL 14ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF ILLINOIS, that we declare the week of 15January 19 through January 25, 2025 as CRNA Week in the State 16of Illinois to recognize the importance of Certified 17Registered Nurse Anesthetists (CRNAs) and their role in 18providing high-quality care to the people of Illinois.
