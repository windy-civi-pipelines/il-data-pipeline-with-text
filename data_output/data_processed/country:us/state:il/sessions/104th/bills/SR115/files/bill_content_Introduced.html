2    WHEREAS, Certified Registered Nurse Anesthetists (CRNAs), 
3also known as nurse anesthesiologists or nurse anesthetists, 
4are advanced practice registered nurses who are among the

7and
 
8    WHEREAS, CRNAs became the first nursing specialty to be 
9accorded direct reimbursement rights by Medicare due to 
10legislation passed in 1986; and
 
11    WHEREAS, CRNAs are the primary anesthesia providers in 
12rural and underserved communities and continue to be the 
13primary providers of anesthesia care to U.S. military 
14personnel; and
 
15    WHEREAS, CRNAs practice in every setting in which 
16anesthesia is delivered, including traditional hospital 
17surgical suites, obstetrical delivery rooms, critical access 
18hospitals, ambulatory surgical centers, and the offices of 
19dentists, podiatrists, ophthalmologists, and pain management 
20specialists; and
 
21    WHEREAS, CRNAs are qualified to make independent judgments
