SENATE RESOLUTION

 
2    WHEREAS, The members of the Illinois Senate are saddened 
3to learn of the death of Sgt. Jose Duenez Jr. of Joliet, who 
4passed away on March 25, 2025 while serving his country; and
 
5    WHEREAS, Sgt. Duenez was born to Jose and Leticia 
6(Quintero) Duenez in Joliet on April 8, 1999; he was one of six 
7children and was affectionately known as "Junior"; he 
8developed a deep commitment to his faith after participating 
9in a missionary trip and maintained that faith throughout his 
10life; he enlisted in the U.S. Army after graduating from high 
11school in 2017; and
 
12    WHEREAS, Sgt. Duenez was often recognized by his peers and 
13superior officers as a trustworthy and dedicated soldier 
14during his seven-year military career; he graduated from the 
15Army Basic Leader Course and the Tracked Vehicle Recovery 
16Operations Course; he was promoted five times over the course 
17of his service, ultimately reaching the rank of staff sergeant 
18in November of 2020; he served in three overseas deployments, 
19to Poland in 2019, Germany in 2022, and Lithuania in 2025; at 
20the time of his passing, he was scheduled to attend the 
21Advanced Leaders Course; and
 
22    WHEREAS, Sgt. Duenez was most recently serving in
