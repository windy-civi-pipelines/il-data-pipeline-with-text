SENATE RESOLUTION

 
2    WHEREAS, The members of the Illinois Senate are saddened 
3to learn of the death of Roxie Lee "Rocky" Frederick Jr., who 
4passed away on March 31, 2025; and
 
5    WHEREAS, Roxie Frederick, the son of Roxie Lee Frederick 
6Sr. and Virginia Irene (Lassiter) Frederick, was born on 
7December 28, 1955; he married Joyce Marie Tooley in St. Louis, 
8Missouri on January 8, 1973, and they were married for 52 years 
9at the time of his passing; he obtained a diploma in welding; 
10and
 
11    WHEREAS, Roxie Frederick worked as a laborer at Illinois 
12Coal Mines for five years; he then worked as a mail messenger 
13and logistics manager for the Illinois Department of Commerce 
14and Economic Opportunity for 20 years; for 17 years, he drove a 
15weekday commuter bus to Springfield for State employees and on 
16weekends for Cavallo Bus Lines; he was beloved by his 
17coworkers and the patrons of the bus line; and
 
18    WHEREAS, Roxie Frederick was a skilled photographer and 
19earned a number of accolades, including from Lake Land 
20College; his striking photograph of the Comet Hale-Bopp was 
21featured on the front page of the Litchfield News-Herald, and 
22his image of the Illinois State Capitol was selected by
