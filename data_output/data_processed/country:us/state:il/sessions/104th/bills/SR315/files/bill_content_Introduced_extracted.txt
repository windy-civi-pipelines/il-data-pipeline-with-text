and 5    WHEREAS, HSHS St. Anthony's Memorial Hospital originated 6when Sisters Euphemia Gaertner, Fortunata Honkamp, and 7Franziska Dreisvogt of the Hospital Sisters of St. Francis 8arrived in Effingham on November 15, 1875, where they began 9serving the poor and the sick; their efforts led to the opening 10of St. Anthony's Hospital on September 15, 1877, and the 11hospital was later renamed St. Anthony's Memorial Hospital in 121954; and 13    WHEREAS, HSHS St. Anthony's Memorial Hospital, an 14affiliate of the Hospital Sisters Health System (HSHS), is a 15fully accredited, not-for-profit, general acute care health 16facility today; and 17    WHEREAS, HSHS St. Anthony's Memorial Hospital has 133 18licensed beds and a workforce of more than 700 employees, and 19its medical staff represents more than 70 local physician 20providers in 26 specialties and includes over 80 consulting 21physicians; and   SR0315- 2 -LRB104 13536 LAW 26015 r1    WHEREAS, HSHS St. Anthony's Memorial Hospital primarily 2provides services to Effingham County and Jasper County along 3with portions of Clark, Clay, Crawford, Cumberland, Fayette, 4Marion, Richland, and Shelby Counties, serving a population of 5almost 200,000 people; and 6    WHEREAS, For more than 150 years, HSHS St. Anthony's 7Memorial Hospital has approached administering healthcare to 8its patients by combining compassion and the human touch, and 9the hospital continues to do so while also providing patients 10with the most modern technologies available today; therefore, 11be it 12    RESOLVED, BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTH GENERAL 13ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF ILLINOIS, that we congratulate HSHS 14St. Anthony's Memorial Hospital on celebrating its 150th 15anniversary, and we thank its staff for continuing to serve 16with courage, commitment, and faith; and be it further 17    RESOLVED, That a suitable copy of this resolution be 18presented to HSHS St. Anthony's Memorial Hospital as an 19expression of our esteem and respect.
