Full Text of SB1366 ILGA.GOV SB1366 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026SB1366 Introduced 1/28/2025, by Sen. Sally J. Turner SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: New Act    Creates the State Government AI Act. Provides that, before January 1, 2028, the Department of Innovation and Technology shall adopt rules establishing policies and procedures concerning the development, procurement, deployment, use, and assessment of artificial intelligence by agencies of State government. Prohibits the deployment and use of artificial intelligence by agencies of State government, unless permitted by rules adopted by the Department. Requires certain impact assessment reports. Defines terms.
