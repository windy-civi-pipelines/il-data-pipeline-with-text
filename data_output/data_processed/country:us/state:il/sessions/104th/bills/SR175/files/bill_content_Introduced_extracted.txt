Full Text of SR0175 ILGA.GOV SR0175 - 104th General Assembly SR0175LRB104 12783 LAW 24061 r1SENATE RESOLUTION 2    WHEREAS, Public transportation is a lifeline for many of 3our most vulnerable residents, providing residents without any 4other form of transportation the ability to travel to doctor's 5appointments, grocery stores, and other vital locations; and 6    WHEREAS, Public transportation in downstate counties can 7require travel of over 25 miles from pick-up to drop-off 8locations, and being denied access to public transportation in 9these areas could be the difference between life and death; 10and 11    WHEREAS, There are many counties in Illinois that are the 12single provider of public transportation, making it the only 13reliable option for residents in a given county; and 14    WHEREAS, The Downstate Operating Assistance Program (DOAP) 15covers only 65% of eligible operating costs, while the county 16is required to contribute 35% of the operating costs, putting 17an undue burden on single county providers; therefore, be it 18    RESOLVED, BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTH GENERAL 19ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF ILLINOIS, that we support the 20Downstate Operating Assistance Program (DOAP).
