Full Text of HB3623 ILGA.GOV HB3623 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026HB3623 Introduced 2/18/2025, by Rep. Justin Slaughter SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 20 ILCS 2310/2310-23 new    Amends the Department of Public Health Powers and Duties Law of the Civil Administrative Code of Illinois. Defines "pharmacy desert". Requires the Department of Public Health to provide an annual report to the General Assembly by December 31 of each year that identifies the locations of pharmacy deserts within the State and provides information about health issues associated with pharmacy deserts. Provides that if the annual report contains information from the federal government that identifies the locations of pharmacy deserts in the State and provides information on health issues associated with pharmacy deserts, then the requirements of the provisions shall be satisfied.
