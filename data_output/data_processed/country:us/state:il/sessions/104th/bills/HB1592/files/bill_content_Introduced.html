SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED:
 
75 ILCS 5/5-5  from Ch. 81, par. 5-5
75 ILCS 16/40-45
    Amends the Illinois Local Library Act and the Public Library District Act of 1991. Requires competitive bidding for contracts in excess of $35,000 (rather than $25,000) for specified improvements and equipment purchases. Effective immediately.
