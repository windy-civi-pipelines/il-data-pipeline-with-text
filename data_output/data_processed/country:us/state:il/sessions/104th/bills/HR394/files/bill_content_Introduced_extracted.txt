and 6    WHEREAS, The International Francophonie Day, known as 7Journee Internationale de la Francophonie, is celebrated by 8the International Organization of La Francophonie and across 9the globe annually on March 20 to recognize the 369 million 10French speakers worldwide and to honor the cultural, 11linguistic, and historic contributions of French-speaking 12peoples; and 13    WHEREAS, The State of Illinois is home to a rich and 14lasting French heritage rooted in its early exploration, 15settlement, and trade by French colonists in the 17th and 18th 16centuries, with historical ties still visible in towns, 17traditions, and in the names of places across the State; and 18    WHEREAS, Illinois is a proud member of the French Heritage 19Corridor, a multistate and binational cultural initiative 20dedicated to highlighting and preserving the historic presence 21and legacy of French explorers, traders, and settlers along 22the Mississippi River and beyond; and   HR0394- 2 -LRB104 13936 ECR 26849 r1    WHEREAS, Communities across Illinois, including those in 2Cook County, Lake County, Kankakee County, Tazewell County, 3and Randolph County, continue to reflect and honor the State's 4French and Francophone influence through cultural 5institutions, historic sites, education, and public events; 6and 7    WHEREAS, The continued recognition of Francophone culture 8enhances the State's commitment to multiculturalism, 9education, and international engagement; therefore, be it 10    RESOLVED, BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE 11HUNDRED FOURTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF ILLINOIS, that 12we declare March 2025 as French Heritage Month and March 20, 132025 as French Heritage Day in the State of Illinois to 14celebrate the linguistic and cultural heritage of 15French-speaking communities and to promote the values of 16diversity, cooperation, and cultural exchange; and be it 17further 18    RESOLVED, That suitable copies of this resolution be 19delivered to the Consulate General of France in Chicago and 20the Illinois Office of Tourism as a symbol of our esteem and 21respect.
