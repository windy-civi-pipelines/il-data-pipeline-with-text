SENATE RESOLUTION

 
2    WHEREAS, The members of the Illinois Senate are saddened 
3to learn of the death of Raymond Edward "Ray" Imig, who passed 
4away on March 31, 2025; and
 
5    WHEREAS, Ray Imig was born in Des Plaines to Ray and 
6Frances Imig on September 28, 1937 and was a lifelong resident 
7of Park Ridge and Des Plaines; and
 
8    WHEREAS, Ray Imig began his career as an educator with 
9more than 30 years of service to Park Ridge District 64; after 
10retirement, he earned his CADC license and served as a 
11counselor and an intake coordinator at Keys to Recovery for 
12another 25 years; and
 
13    WHEREAS, Ray Imig and his wife, Ginger, nurtured small 
14businesses that became local treasures; they created fresh 
15doughnuts at Imigs' Cafe and music-filled aisles at Crossroads 
16Record Shop, where they also created custom t-shirts; and
 
17    WHEREAS, Ray Imig gave generously of his time and talents, 
18and service was at the core of his life; he was a proud member 
19of the Elks and the Des Plaines Lions Club; he volunteered for 
20HOTLINE, MCYAF, and the Self-Help Closet and Pantry of Des 
21Plaines; he had also served on the Des Plaines District 62
