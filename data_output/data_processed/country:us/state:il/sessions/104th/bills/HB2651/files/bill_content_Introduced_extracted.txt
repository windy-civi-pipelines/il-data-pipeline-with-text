Full Text of HB2651 ILGA.GOV HB2651 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026HB2651 Introduced 2/6/2025, by Rep. Tracy Katz Muhl SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 105 ILCS 5/2-3.206 new    Amends the School Code. Requires the State Board of Education to identify days during the year when there may be a portion of the student body absent or unable to participate in a major school event due to cultural, religious, or other observances and, by February 1 of each year, to prominently post on its website and distribute to school districts a nonexhaustive list of the corresponding days and dates of potential cultural, religious, or other observances in the upcoming school year. Allows a school district to include additional days and dates based on community feedback or demographics. Requires the State Board to distribute the list by February 1 each year to regional offices of education, the Department of Early Childhood, the Board of Higher Education, the Illinois High School Association, and other relevant associations or entities as determined by the State Board.
