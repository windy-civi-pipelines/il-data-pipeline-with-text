Full Text of HB0049 ILGA.GOV HB0049 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026HB0049 Introduced 1/9/2025, by Rep. David Friess SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 55 ILCS 5/3-6013  from Ch. 34, par. 3-6013    Amends the Counties Code. Provides that, in matters a sheriff deems essential to preventing or assisting with disasters or events of civil disorder, the sheriff may authorize plain clothes to be worn by auxiliary deputies as long as the auxiliary deputies are carrying proper identification while performing this function.
