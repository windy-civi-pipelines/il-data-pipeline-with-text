Full Text of HB2444 ILGA.GOV HB2444 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026HB2444 Introduced 2/4/2025, by Rep. Amy Elik SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 15 ILCS 205/4f new    Amends the Attorney General Act. Provides that the Office of the Attorney General shall record the number of hours, legal fees, and costs incurred each fiscal year by its attorneys and any outside counsel acting on its behalf to defend the State in all legal proceedings relating to the constitutionality or statutory interpretation of Illinois law. Provides that, on or before July 1, 2026, and each year thereafter, the Attorney General shall report the recorded number of hours, legal fees, and costs incurred annually to the General Assembly.
