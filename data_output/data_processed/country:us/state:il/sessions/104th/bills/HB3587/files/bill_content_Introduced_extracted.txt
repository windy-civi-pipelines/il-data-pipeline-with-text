Full Text of HB3587 ILGA.GOV HB3587 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026HB3587 Introduced 2/18/2025, by Rep. Justin Slaughter SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 30 ILCS 500/45-115 new    Amends the Illinois Procurement Code. Provides that any contract that provides for the storage of any State data shall include a requirement that the State data must be processed, stored, and disposed of within the territory of the United States. Provides that, for any contract that provides for the storage of State data, the Chief Procurement Officer shall allocate to any qualified bidder an earned credit of: (1) 2% of the contract base bid for ensuring that all State data that is included in the contract is stored within the State of Illinois; and (2) an additional 4% of the contract base bid for ensuring that all such State data is stored within an opportunity zone.
