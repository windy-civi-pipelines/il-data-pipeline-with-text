HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION

 
2    WHEREAS, It is highly fitting that the Illinois General 
3Assembly pays honor and respect to veterans who have served 
4their country and the State of Illinois; and
 
5    WHEREAS, Staff Sergeant Wilbur E. Troehler was born in the 
6small farming town of Fairbury to parents Grover C. and Aline 
7Troehler on March 3, 1916; he married Ruth M. Urban on February 
821, 1941, and they settled in Chicago, where they had three 
9sons, Eugene, William, and Robert; and
 
10    WHEREAS, SSG Troehler was inducted into the United States 
11Army at Gary, Indiana on March 23, 1943; he received his basic 
12training at Fort Leonard Wood, Missouri and advanced 
13individual training at Camp Breckenridge, Kentucky; upon 
14completion of training, he was assigned to the 75th Infantry 
15Division and further assigned to Company D, 289th Infantry 
16Regiment; the 75th Infantry Division was activated April 15, 
171943 and deployed overseas on November 14, 1944; and
 
18    WHEREAS, SSG Troehler entered combat as a Heavy Weapons 
19NCO and Section Leader under the 75th Infantry Division on 
20Christmas Day 1944 in the Ardennes Counter Offensive, later 
21known as the Battle of the Bulge; and

1    WHEREAS, SSG Troehler returned to the U.S. in April 1945 
2after marching through England, France, Belgium, and 
3Luxemburg; and
 
4    WHEREAS, SSG Troehler received numerous medals, including 
5the Bronze Star, the American Campaign Medal, the 
6European-African-Middle Eastern Campaign Medal, the World War 
7II Victory Medal, and the Army Occupation Medal; and
 
8    WHEREAS, SSG Troehler received the Combat Infantry Badge, 
9the Expert Infantry Badge, and the Expert Marksmanship Badge; 
10he was also given the Honorable Service Lapel Button; and
 
11    WHEREAS, SSG Troehler passed away on April 13, 1989 in 
12Chicago; therefore, be it
 
13    RESOLVED, BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE 
14HUNDRED FOURTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF ILLINOIS, THE 
15SENATE CONCURRING HEREIN, that we designate U.S. Route 24 as 
16it travels through Fairbury as the "SSG Wilbur E. Troehler 
17Memorial Highway"; and be it further
 
18    RESOLVED, That the Illinois Department of Transportation 
19is requested to erect at suitable locations, consistent with 
20State and federal regulations, appropriate plaques or signs 
21giving notice of the name "SSG Wilbur E. Troehler Memorial
