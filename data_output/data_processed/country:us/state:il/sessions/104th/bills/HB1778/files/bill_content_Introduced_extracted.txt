Full Text of HB1778 ILGA.GOV HB1778 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026HB1778 Introduced 1/28/2025, by Rep. Jay Hoffman SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 20 ILCS 405/405-550 new    Amends the Department of Central Management Services Law of the Civil Administrative Code of Illinois. Provides that, within 30 days after the Department of Labor makes a revision to the prevailing rate of hourly wages to be paid under the Prevailing Wage Act, the Department of Central Management Services shall revise the salaries and rates of pay for all employees who are required to be paid the revised prevailing rate of hourly wages.
