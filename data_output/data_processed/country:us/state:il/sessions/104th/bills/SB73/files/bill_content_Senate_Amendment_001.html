7    Sec. 11.7. Baby foods; toxic elements.
8    (a) In this Section:
9    "Baby food" means food packaged in a jar, pouch, tub, or 
10box sold specifically for babies and children under the age of 
112 years old. "Baby food" does not include infant formula as 
12defined in Section 2.28.
13    "Final baby food product" means the finished product of 
14baby food with a unique universal product code (UPC). "Final 
15baby food product" does not include the individual ingredients 
16that are in baby food.
