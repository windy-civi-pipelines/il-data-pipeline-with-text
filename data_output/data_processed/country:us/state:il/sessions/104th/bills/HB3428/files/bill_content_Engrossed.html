1    AN ACT concerning regulation.
 
2    Be it enacted by the People of the State of Illinois, 
3represented in the General Assembly:
 
4    Section 5. The Assisted Living and Shared Housing Act is 
5amended by adding Section 111 as follows:
 
6    (210 ILCS 9/111 new)
7    Sec. 111. Joint provider, surveyor, and association 
8trainings. The Department shall semiannually provide joint 
9training sessions for surveyors and assisted living 
10establishment providers. The Department shall include the 
11State long-term care ombudsman, or the State long-term care 
12ombudsman's designee, and representatives of each assisted 
13living provider association in the State in the planning 
14process to create the topics and content of the joint training 
15sessions as well as the coordination and presentations for the 
16joint training sessions. At least annually, a joint training 
17session shall include, but not be limited to, regional 
18citation patterns relating to complaints, standards, and 
19outcomes in the assisted living survey process. The Department 
20shall also develop standardized training for establishments to 
21prevent common citations in the assisted living survey 
22process.
