2    WHEREAS, The Illinois General Assembly recognizes the 
3importance of Route 66 as a cultural and historical asset and 
4wishes to honor its centennial milestone and to celebrate its 
5impact on transportation, tourism, and the American spirit; 
6and
 
7    WHEREAS, Route 66, known as the "Main Street of America" 
8or as the "Mother Road", has played a significant role in the 
9history and development of the United States, connecting 
10Chicago, Illinois, to Santa Monica, California and serving as 
11a vital transportation corridor since its establishment in 
121926; and
 
13    WHEREAS, The year 2026 marks the 100th anniversary of 
14Route 66, a roadway that has become an enduring symbol of 
15adventure, freedom, and economic opportunity and that has 
16contributed to the culture and identity of Illinois and the 
17nation as a whole; and
 
18    WHEREAS, Illinois holds a unique place in the history of 
19Route 66 as it was the first state to pave the highway in its 
20entirety and serves as the starting point of this legendary 
21route in downtown Chicago; Route 66 passes through numerous 
22communities and landscapes that have left a lasting imprint on

2    WHEREAS, Route 66 in Illinois has supported small

16    WHEREAS, The preservation and commemoration of Route 66's
