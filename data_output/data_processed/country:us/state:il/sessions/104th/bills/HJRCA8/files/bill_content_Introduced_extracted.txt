Full Text of HJRCA0008 ILGA.GOV HJRCA0008 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION CONSTITUTIONAL AMENDMENTHC0008 Introduced 1/29/2025, by Rep. Curtis J. Tarver, II SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: ILCON Art. IX, Sec. 12 new    Proposes to amend the Revenue Article of the Illinois Constitution. Provides that an additional income tax shall be imposed on individuals in an amount equal to 3% of the portion of the individual's income that is greater than $1,000,000 for the taxable year. Provides that the revenue collected from the tax shall be distributed to school districts on a per pupil basis.
