and 19    WHEREAS, Midge Kimberly became president and CEO of the 20Kimberly Group, where she emerged as an influential leader in 21the Chicago business community; during her career, she 22collaborated with a host of celebrities and media   SR0107- 2 -LRB104 12069 ECR 22166 r1professionals, including Muhammad Ali at the 1996 Summer 2Olympics in Atlanta, Georgia; she also launched Champagne & 3Beyond, an online magazine celebrating the accomplishments of 4extraordinary women, in 2014; and 5    WHEREAS, Midge Kimberly was a woman of strong faith who 6always offered a warm smile and kind words to anyone she met; 7she devoted her life to empowering others and will be 8remembered as a mentor, a trailblazer, and a friend to many; 9and 10    WHEREAS, Midge Kimberly was the mother of Richard Carl 11Davenport, Sherry Davenport Henry, Robert Davenport, Troy 12Davenport, and Vernon Nelson; the grandmother of eight; and 13the great-grandmother of several; therefore, be it 14    RESOLVED, BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTH GENERAL 15ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF ILLINOIS, that we mourn the passing of 16Midge Kimberly and extend our sincere condolences to her 17family, friends, and all who knew and loved her;
