Full Text of SB0178 ILGA.GOV SB0178 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026SB0178 Introduced 1/17/2025, by Sen. Laura M. Murphy SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 35 ILCS 5/235 new    Amends the Illinois Income Tax Act. Creates an income tax credit in an amount equal to the amount paid by the taxpayer during the taxable year for the purpose of purchasing materials, labor, and professional services to soundproof a residential home located at an eligible address against aircraft noise generated by an airport governed by the provisions of the Permanent Noise Monitoring Act. Provides that the credit may not reduce the taxpayer's liability to less than zero. Provides that the credit may be carried forward. Provides that the credit is exempt from the Act's automatic sunset provisions.
