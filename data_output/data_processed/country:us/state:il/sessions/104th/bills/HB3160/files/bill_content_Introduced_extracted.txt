Full Text of HB3160 ILGA.GOV HB3160 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026HB3160 Introduced 2/18/2025, by Rep. Katie Stuart SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 20 ILCS 1305/1-90 new    Amends the Department of Human Services Act. Requires the Department of Human Services to, at least annually, provide each provider of home and community-based services an updated, detailed rate sheet for each funded client served by the provider. Provides that the Department must also, within 45 days of submission of any provider-based information to the Department, such as Inventory for Client and Agency Planning scores, which changes a client's reimbursement rate, issue an updated rate sheet for the client.
