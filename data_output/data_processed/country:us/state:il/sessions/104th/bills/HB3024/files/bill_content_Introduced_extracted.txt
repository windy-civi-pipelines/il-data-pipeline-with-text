Full Text of HB3024 ILGA.GOV HB3024 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026HB3024 Introduced 2/6/2025, by Rep. Abdelnasser Rashid SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 15 ILCS 30/5 new    Amends the Disaster Relief Act. Provides that the Illinois Emergency Management Agency and Office of Homeland Security may provide State supplemental grants to meet disaster-related necessary expenses or serious needs of individuals and households adversely affected by any disaster declared by the President of the United States in those cases in which individuals and households are unable to meet those expenses or needs through other assistance.
