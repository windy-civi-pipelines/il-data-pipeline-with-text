14    Sec. 3.25. Cold spa. "Cold spa" means a basin of water that 
15is maintained at a temperature of not less than 40 degrees 
16Fahrenheit and not greater than 92 degrees Fahrenheit. "Cold 
17Spa" does not include the use or installation of portable, 
18manufactured, or commercially available cold spas unless they 
19comply with the provisions of this Act.
