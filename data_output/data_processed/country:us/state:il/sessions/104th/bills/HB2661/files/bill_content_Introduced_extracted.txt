Full Text of HB2661 ILGA.GOV HB2661 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026HB2661 Introduced 2/6/2025, by Rep. Joe C. Sosnowski SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 430 ILCS 66/60    Amends the Firearm Concealed Carry Act. Provides that a licensee requesting a new license shall submit $5 (rather than $75, of which $60 shall be apportioned to the State Police Firearm Services Fund, $5 shall be apportioned to the Mental Health Reporting Fund, and $10 shall be apportioned to the State Crime Laboratory Fund).
