Janet Yang Rohr SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 35 ILCS 5/507MMM new30 ILCS 105/5.1030 new    Amends the Illinois Income Tax Act. Creates the Illinois Youth and Government Fund. Provides that moneys in the Illinois Youth and Government Fund shall be used by the Department of Commerce and Economic Opportunity to make grants to support the Illinois YMCA Youth and Government program. Creates an income tax checkoff for contributions to the Illinois Youth and Government Fund. Amends the State Finance Act to make conforming changes.
