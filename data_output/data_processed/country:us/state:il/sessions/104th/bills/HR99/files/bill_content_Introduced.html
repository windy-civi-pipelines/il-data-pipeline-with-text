7Chicago Vocational High School; he served in the U.S. Navy and

11Science in Theology, and his dedication to his studies also 
12earned him multiple academic awards for excellence and 
13outstanding leadership; and
 
14    WHEREAS, Carl Moore was a talented computer expert with 
15knowledge of various Microsoft programs, DCL programming, VMS 
16fundamentals, and system hardware; he worked as an operations 
17analyst and computer hardware engineer at the Chicago Stock 
18Exchange, as a help desk analyst and computer operator at
