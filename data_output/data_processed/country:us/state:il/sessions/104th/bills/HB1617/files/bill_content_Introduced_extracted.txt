Full Text of HB1617 ILGA.GOV HB1617 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026HB1617 Introduced 1/28/2025, by Rep. Jaime M. Andrade, Jr. SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 720 ILCS 5/2-23 new720 ILCS 5/19-2  from Ch. 38, par. 19-2720 ILCS 5/19-2.5    Amends the Criminal Code of 2012. Provides that the offense of possession of burglary tools includes possessing a vehicle security circumvention device and not being a: (1) mechanic; (2) licensed new or used vehicle dealer; (3) licensed locksmith; (4) repossession agent; or (5) State or local law enforcement officer. Provides that a violation is a Class C misdemeanor. Provides that the offense of unlawful sale of burglary tools includes knowingly selling or transferring a vehicle security circumvention device with knowledge that the device will be used by the person or another to commit a violation of law. Provides that a violation is a petty offense.
