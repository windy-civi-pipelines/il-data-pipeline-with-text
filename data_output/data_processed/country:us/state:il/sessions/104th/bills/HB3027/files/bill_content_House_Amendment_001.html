11    Section 10. Prohibited sale to minors. No person shall 
12sell, offer to sell, or give away, as either a retail or 
13wholesale promotion, an over-the-counter diet pill or dietary 
14supplement for weight loss or muscle building to any person 
15under 18 years of age, unless that individual has a 
16prescription for that item. Retail establishments shall 
17require proof of legal age for purchase of such products.
 
18    Section 15. Responsibilities of retail establishments.
19    (a) A retail establishment shall request valid

21attempts to purchase a dietary supplement for weight loss or 
22muscle building or an over-the-counter diet pill if the retail 
23establishment cannot reasonably determine that the person 
24appears to be at least 18 years of age or has a prescription.
