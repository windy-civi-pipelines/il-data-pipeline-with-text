Full Text of HB2917 ILGA.GOV HB2917 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026HB2917 Introduced 2/6/2025, by Rep. Jackie Haas SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 745 ILCS 49/125 new    Amends the Good Samaritan Act. Provides civil immunity for a not-for-profit corporation organized to assist veterans that requests, sponsors, or participates in providing a suicide prevention intervention at the scene of a threatened suicide by a veteran; or a person, who without compensation, provides such an intervention at the request of a veteran no-for-profit corporation. Exempts an act or omission that was not in good faith or was the result of gross negligence or willful misconduct.LRB104 08591 JRC 18643 b  A BILL FOR HB2917LRB104 08591 JRC 18643 b1    AN ACT concerning civil law. 2    Be it enacted by the People of the State of Illinois, 3represented in the General Assembly: 4    Section 5. The Good Samaritan Act is amended by adding 5Section 125 as follows: 6    (745 ILCS 49/125 new)7    Sec. 125. Veteran not-for-profit corporations. A 8not-for-profit corporation organized to assist veterans that 9requests, sponsors, or participates in providing a suicide 10prevention intervention at the scene of a threatened suicide 11by a veteran, or a person who, without compensation, provides 12such an intervention for and at the request of a veteran 13not-for-profit corporation, is not liable for civil damages in 14any action in this State for any act or omission resulting from 15the rendering of suicide prevention intervention, unless the 16act or omission was not in good faith or was the result of 17gross negligence or willful misconduct. For the purposes of 18this Section, "not-for-profit corporation" has the meaning 19given to that term in Section 101.80 of the General Not For 20Profit Corporation Act of 1986.
