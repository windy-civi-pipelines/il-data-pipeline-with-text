Bruce (Julie) West and Jeffery (Tracy) West; his 3grandchildren, Callie (Levi) Allen, Paul (Amanda) West, 4Victoria (Andrew) Wells, and Elizabeth West; his 5great-grandchildren, Ben and Emilia Allen and Evelyn and Lucy 6West, with Baby Wells on the way; his niece, Jane (Ed) Fussner; 7and his nephew, David (Debby) Doubet; therefore, be it 8    RESOLVED, BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTH GENERAL 9ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF ILLINOIS, that we mourn the passing of 10William Bruce "Bill" West Sr. and extend our sincere 11condolences to his family, friends, and all who knew and loved 12him; and be it further 13    RESOLVED, That a suitable copy of this resolution be 14presented to the family of Bill West as an expression of our 15deepest sympathy.
