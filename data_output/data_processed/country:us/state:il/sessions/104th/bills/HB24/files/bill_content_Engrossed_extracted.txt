If a home repair and remodeling 8contract that is subject to this Act contains a provision 9allowing attorney's fees to be awarded to any party to the 10contract, then, notwithstanding any terms in the contract to 11the contrary, that provision shall be deemed and construed to 12permit the award of reasonable attorney's fees to all parties 13to the contract. This Act takes effect upon 17becoming law.
