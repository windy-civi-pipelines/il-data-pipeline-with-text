Full Text of HB1561 ILGA.GOV HB1561 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026HB1561 Introduced 1/28/2025, by Rep. Charles Meier SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 225 ILCS 458/5-60 new    Amends the Real Estate Appraiser Licensing Act of 2002. Provides that any person or business that, as of January 1, 1995, was practicing as an appraiser in the State of Illinois and had a minimum of 2 years of experience practicing as an appraiser is exempt from the required licensure as a certified general real estate appraiser under the provisions regarding an application for a State certified general real estate appraiser license.
