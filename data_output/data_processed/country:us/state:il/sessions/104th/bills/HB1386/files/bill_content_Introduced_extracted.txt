Full Text of HB1386 ILGA.GOV HB1386 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026HB1386 Introduced 1/28/2025, by Rep. Brandun Schweizer and Tony M. McCombie SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 35 ILCS 5/234    Amends the Illinois Income Tax Act. Provides that, for taxable years beginning on or after January 1, 2026 and beginning before January 1, 2029, the amount of the credit for volunteer emergency workers shall be $1,000 per eligible individual (currently, $500 per eligible individual). Provides that the aggregate amount of volunteer emergency worker credits shall be $6,000,000 per year (currently, $5,000,000 per year).
