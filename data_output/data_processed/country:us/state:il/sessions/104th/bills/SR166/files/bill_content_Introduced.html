SENATE RESOLUTION

 
2    WHEREAS, The members of the Illinois Senate are saddened 
3to learn of the death of Linda Renee Olivero of Springfield, 
4who passed away on February 2, 2025; and
 
5    WHEREAS, Linda Olivero, the daughter of John and Lorraine 
6Olivero, was born in Peru on May 22, 1962; she graduated from 
7LaSalle-Peru Township High School in 1980, where she served as 
8class president; she received degrees in both accounting and 
9law from the University of Illinois; and
 
10    WHEREAS, Linda Olivero worked as a Certified Public 
11Accountant (CPA) and a lawyer; during her career, she clerked 
12for a federal district judge in southern Illinois, served as 
13an attorney for the Illinois Supreme Court, and served as an 
14administrative law judge for the Illinois Department of 
15Revenue for decades; she was known for her brilliant legal 
16mind and unwavering integrity; and
 
17    WHEREAS, Linda Olivero volunteered for the Make-A-Wish 
18Foundation for more than 30 years and as a Big Sister for young 
19girls in the Big Sister Little Sister mentoring program; she 
20also dedicated herself to helping disabled adults at the 
21Center for the Blind and Visually Impaired; and
