Adriane Johnson SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 20 ILCS 2630/5.2    Amends the Criminal Identification Act. Modifies the definition of "criminal offense" for the purposes of the Act to include, among other things, an arrest under the Uniform Criminal Extradition Act.
