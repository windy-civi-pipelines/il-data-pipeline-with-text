Full Text of HR0301 ILGA.GOV HR0301 - 104th General Assembly HR0301LRB104 13440 MST 25829 r1HOUSE RESOLUTION 2    WHEREAS, It is highly fitting that the Illinois House of 3Representatives pays honor and respect to those who have 4served our country and, in doing so, have made the ultimate 5sacrifice for our nation; and 6    WHEREAS, Sergeant First Class Theodore Alexander 7Katsoolias served in the U.S. Army as a member of 1st 8Battalion, 38th Infantry Regiment, 2nd Infantry Division; and 9    WHEREAS, On November 25, 1950, approximately 300,000 10Chinese Communist Forces launched a counteroffensive against 11U.N. forces after crossing the Yalu River; SFC Katsoolias' 12unit was positioned near the Chongchon River and soon came 13under intense attack; unable to halt the enemy advance and in 14danger of being surrounded, his unit pulled back and was 15forced to fight against enemy pursuit while also attempting to 16break through well-defended roadblocks; the withdrawal was not 17complete until early December and inflicted extremely heavy 18casualties on his unit; and 19    WHEREAS, SFC Katsoolias went missing at some point during 20the withdrawal to Kujang; he was never reported as a prisoner 21of war, and his remains have not been recovered; and   HR0301- 2 -LRB104 13440 MST 25829 r1    WHEREAS, SFC Katsoolias is memorialized on the Courts of 2the Missing at the National Memorial Cemetery of the Pacific; 3and 4    WHEREAS, The Heroes Way Designation Program through the 5Illinois Department of Transportation honors fallen Illinois 6heroes who have been killed in action while performing active 7military duty with the United States Armed Forces and has 8designated Route 59 in West Chicago from Route 38 to Route 64 9as the SFC Theodore A. Katsoolias Memorial Highway; therefore, 10be it 11    RESOLVED, BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE 12HUNDRED FOURTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF ILLINOIS, that 13we commend the designation of the SFC Theodore A. Katsoolias 14Memorial Highway as part of the Heroes Way Designation Program 15through the Illinois Department of Transportation; and be it 16further 17    RESOLVED, That a suitable copy of this resolution be 18presented to the family of SFC Katsoolias.
