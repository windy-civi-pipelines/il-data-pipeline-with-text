Full Text of HB3011 ILGA.GOV HB3011 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026HB3011 Introduced 2/6/2025, by Rep. Jed Davis SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 110 ILCS 805/3-7.12 new    Amends the Public Community College Act. Requires each member of the board of trustees of a community college district to, before entering upon the duties of the office, take and subscribe to a specified oath. Provides that the oath or affirmation shall be administered by the secretary of the board, a notary public, or any other officer authorized to administer oaths under State law, and the completed oath shall be filed with the secretary of the board and made part of the official records of the community college district.
