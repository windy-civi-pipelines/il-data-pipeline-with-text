Full Text of SB2265 ILGA.GOV SB2265 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026SB2265 Introduced 2/7/2025, by Sen. Karina Villa SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 735 ILCS 5/9-106  from Ch. 110, par. 9-106735 ILCS 5/9-121    Amends the Eviction Article of the Code of Civil Procedure. Requires dismissal of a complaint in its entirety against all defendants if the complaint names a defendant who is a minor at the time of filing or was a minor at the time that the lease agreement at issue was entered. Provides that such an action shall be immediately sealed. Provides that, in addition to any other remedies available to the minor, a minor named as a defendant in violation of the Act is entitled to reasonable attorney's fees, actual damages, and liquidated damages in the amount of $1,000. Provides that nothing in the Act prohibits a party from refiling an action against any defendants who otherwise may be properly named.
