SENATE RESOLUTION

 
2    WHEREAS, The members of the Illinois Senate are saddened 
3to learn of the death of Earlona Mae Looby of Springfield, who 
4passed away on February 19, 2025; and
 
5    WHEREAS, Earlona Looby, the daughter of J. Earle and Leona 
6(Brown) Williams, was born in Springfield on April 24, 1933; 
7she graduated from Sacred Heart Academy in 1951; she married 
8Vincent L. "Vince" Looby in Springfield on June 23, 1956, and 
9he preceded her in death on August 5, 2021; she earned her 
10bachelor's degree from Sangamon State University, known today 
11as the University of Illinois Springfield, in 1984; and
 
12    WHEREAS, Earlona Looby served as a social worker for the 
13Illinois Youth Service Bureau; and
 
14    WHEREAS, Earlona Looby was a member of St. Agnes Catholic 
15Church and the Altrusa Club; she volunteered for numerous 
16community services, and the Senior Center of the Senior 
17Services of Central Illinois was very important to her; and
 
18    WHEREAS, Earlona Looby was preceded in death by her 
19parents and her sisters, Joan Bishop and Cathe Lewis; and
 
20    WHEREAS, Earlona Looby is survived by her children, Jim
